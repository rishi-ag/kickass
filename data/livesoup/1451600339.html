About this project


THE HOPPER BAG IS NO ORDINARY WORK BAG.

Patent & Design Registered

Hi, my name is Owen Harper, I am the creator of the Hopper bag. This Work bag is no ordinary bag. It has the potential to help out the environment and save construction companies money. Built to protect and organise your PPE, food cooler, water bottle, paperwork and personal items. ( Please note that personal items shown are not included with the Hopper Bag)

It's heavy duty, high quality design and construction make it the most practical and versatile bag ever designed for the construction worker, labourer, miner, truck driver or operator. It's also handy for fishing, camping, a picnic and makes a great present for your hard working partner or family member.

HOW CAN THE HOPPER BAG HELP SAVE CONSTRUCTION COMPANIES MONEY AND HELP THE ENVIRONMENT?

 1. The Hopper bag is built to last, so your not going to be replacing it every year.

 2. It helps protect PPE (personal protective equipment) by increasing their lifespan. Some companies spend tens of thousands of dollars every month on replacing safety glasses and gloves. I know, I used to work for one.

Due to today's safety focused working environment, Around the world millions of safety glasses are thrown away each week, due to scratches caused from other items in the same bag compartment. A company will then have to replace these glasses before the worker can go to work. The Hopper Bag helps solve this issue with an easy excess compartment specifically made for this purpose. A Company will not only have to replace glasses or PPE, but also pay for down time while the worker gets another pair of glasses. That can sometimes cost more than the glasses themselves. 3. This bag is very versatile. So you only need one bag for many different uses.

A RUN THROUGH HOW THE HOPPER BAG WORKS

1. Your safety glasses go in the front pockets for easy excess, dark and clear 2. Then your phone and safety or hazard book. 3. In the end pocket your safety tags, earplugs and glass wipes 4. Your lock out lock goes on the D 5. On the other end D is your work gloves 6. In this end pocket, your medical items. Pain killers, band aid and bandages 7. Inside in the large wall pockets can fit A4 paper, magazines, device or training papers 8. Car keys fit on the D inside 9. Your wallet fits in the double end wall pockets and also fits A5 paperwork 10. Pens in the pen holder, small LED torch and utensils 11. A 1.5 Lt. Water bottle 12. Your food cooler in the main compartment 13. Your Helmet on top And the whole thing closes up. 14. The Hi Vis Tape is around the outside 15. A Double layer bottom to add extra wear and tear.

OTHER USES

 FISHING The Hopper Bag is made from waterproof PVC material Will easily fit a 14inch/35cm tackle tray in it, your food, water bottle, reels, bait. All Velcro, so no zips to rust or stick Hi vis tape in case you loose your way and need to be spotted at night.

CAMPING AND HIKING Not only can the Hopper Bag fit your water bottle, devices, food and medical items but the hi vis tape makes it easier to be spotted at night if you lose your way. Will reflect light for miles at night.

PICNIC Your plates fit in the large front and back internal pockets Thermos in the bottle holder Food in the main compartment Utensils in the front pockets Cups in the end pockets

EVERYDAY USE I have found that I use my Hopper Bag as an every day bag. I know were my wallet, glasses, devices and paperwork are now. All in my bag so if I have to go out or change vehicles I just grab my bag.

ABOUT ME 

         I grew up on the family farm in Queensland, Australia. At the age of 14, I was growing vegetable crops for pocket money. I worked in civil construction for 4 years and mining for 3 years. During that time I just couldn't find a work bag that lasted or did the job. 14 months ago I was made redundant, with no pay out. I had the idea to create the perfect bag for the worker in the construction industry. After buying an industrial sewing machine in 2013 and teaching myself to sew I started on my first prototype. With a few adjustments along the way the Hopper Work bag had evolved into what it is today.

During the time I spent working in the Construction, Mining and Farming Industry I couldn't find A bag to take to work that actually did the job. In the civil construction and Mining Industries, things have changed dramatically in the last 10 years. Employees need to carry a lot of personal protective equipment (PPE) like safety glasses, Safety Book (hazard awareness), ear plugs, glass wipes, helmet, safety lock, gloves, lock out tags. Add to that your personal items like medical needs, wallet, some small tools, magazines, tablet, phone, car keys, training material, log books, pens, drink bottle, and food. In every other bag I had, all of this got jumbled up and hard to find, as well as damaged. Safety glasses were lucky to last a week before they were scratched and had to be replaced. About 4 years ago I came up with the idea to make a bag that worked. June 2013 I bought an industrial sewing machine and taught myself to sew. I basically got everything I needed to take to work and designed a spot for it. It also has reflective tape for added safety at night. Sometimes when you carry a bag on your hip or back you block out your own reflective tape on your shirt. This bag prevents that from happening. It's ergonomic design makes it easy to carry without it swaying around. The bag is also made from tuff PVC material and 50mm seat belt strap(rated at 2000kg), with a safety release buckle. This is the best designed and constructed work bag in the world. IT DOES THE JOB, for not only mining and civil construction, but also farming, fishing, camping and hiking. And every day use. I take mine everywhere because I know everything I need is already in it.
