About this project

EVERYBODY HAS A STORY. PEOPLE WANT TO BE HEARD AND MOST WANT TO KNOW THEY ARE NOT ALONE.

'Give YourSelf Permission' Magazine™ is a high-gloss, 80-100 page, perfect bound quarterly publication providing an invaluable source for everyday people to share their personal stories and learn from other's experiences. Educate, Enlighten, Entertain and Empower.

Homemakers, business owners, single parents, veterans, athletes and people like you and me have joined forces in Give YourSelf Permission™ to share their inspiring stories. Their stories will give us insight into how they gave themselves permission to do something out of the ordinary. Something different from their families' expectations or society's judgements. 

SHARE, TEACH, LEARN – with a DASH of INSPIRATION

Each issue will also feature interviews and profiles of notable people who have also done something different, from Amelia Earhart and Stephen Hawking to Rosa Parks and Richard Branson.

In addition to powerful stories,'Give YourSelf Permission' Magazine™ contains regular sections with features on Health & Wellness, Money & Finance, Family & Relationships, Career & Purpose, Lifestyle & Travel. If someone has written a story on a life changing trip – that might show you how to do something similar.

MAGAZINE ORIGIN

Hot on the heels of Priya Rana Kapoor’s book, 'Give YourSelf Permission to Live Your Life', the magazine will enable us to understand how others see the world and navigate their lives. In her work as a life coach, Priya has met people from all walks of life who share their stories of resilience, perseverance and choice. This is why the principles behind the publication are to give everyday people a voice to compassionately teach others and to be part of a wider global learning experience.

WHY PRINT?

In order to encourage the widest possible readership Give YourSelf Permission™ will be simultaneously produced in print format and available to download and viewed on your tablet, iPhone, laptop or PC.

However, our vision is all about connecting people and whilst innovation has changed the publishing industry as Peter Diamandis has pointed out: 

‘People have not had a software update in 200,000 years’. 

One more app or shiny gadget will not guarantee that you actually connect with people. This is done through the message and the content. GYSP will be delivered in a format that enhances the reader experience with stunning photography and beautiful layout and so in addition to the concept of sharing stories, people will share the publication. 

Our goal is to move, validate and affect our readers and one thing that will never change are our hopes, dreams and aspirations in life.

READER INTERACTION:

Our mission is all about collaboration. GYSP goes beyond publishing and also hosts writer workshops, e-courses, live webinars and events.

Maximum interaction between the publishers, contributors and readers will be strongly encouraged and this all starts with our Dream Team – which is you!

WHAT'S NEXT?

This Kickstarter campaign is designed so that you can be a part of the journey from the very beginning. With your help we will continue to collate articles from all over the world and design a truly remarkable publication. With a well prepared, highly targeted marketing campaign GYSP will be hot off the press in a matter of months and someday soon this image in New York will become a reality.

The level of support you chose determines the standards we aim to stand by and we are setting the bar as high as it will go in order to ensure the best possible reader experience.

NEW SPECIAL THANK YOU REWARD FOR EVERYONE WHO PLEDGES*

 * When we get successfully funded

Please take some time to review your REWARDS which are wide ranging and include the opportunity to meet us in LA and have your picture on the front cover of the launch issue! Together we can make a difference.

Risks and challenges

Maintaining and building a healthy subscriber base after launch.

Whilst many brands will take advantage of advertising opportunities with Give YourSelf Permission Magazine, launching a new magazine is all about the readership. In order to reach our target print sales of 20,000 we have chosen to partner with Barnes & Noble, one of America’s most trusted and effective retailers in addition to ensuring that the publication can be sent directly to your home. Each edition is available in multiple electronic formats in order to accommodate our reader’s preferences. Our sales and marketing activities are vigorous and continuous with multiple bolt-ons including workshops, e-courses, and webinars to build our global subscriber base. We are hoping to increase the print run with each issue.
