TravelCard Plus Upgrades

The TravelCard Plus is an upgraded version of last year's highly successful TravelCard. 

  •  We've developed a breakthrough method to charge The TravelCard -an integrated USB charging cord.
  • We've added a group of beautiful travel and adventure prints.
  • Your favorite photo is now available printed on the TravelCard Plus. Just download the photo(s) and you'll have reserve power and a favorite moment with you wherever you go.
  • Kickstarter backers at higher levels will receive an ultra-thin RFID blocking leather wallet that easily holds your cards, cash and The TravelCard Plus.

Simply plug The TravelCard Plus' integrated USB charging cord into a computer port for a full,fast charge (75 minutes). Say goodbye to your charging cables for good.

Have reserve power and a favorite moment with you wherever you go.  Simply upload your photo(s) and we'll email you back a digital proof for your approval. It's that simple.

Included With Higher Level Pledges:    

An Ultra-Thin, RFID Blocking Leather(or simulated leather) Wallet

 
  • The thinnest leather(or simulated leather) wallet ever(.2 inch, 4.9 mm)
  • Easily holds cards, cash and The TravelCard Plus.
  • Protects your cards from unauthorized RFID use.

 1/4 The Thickness Of A Regular Wallet

Technical Information:

Size: 3 3/8" x 2.13 x 3/16 inches (85.9 x 54.1 x 4.7mm)

Battery Capacity: 1500 mAh

What Will The Kickstarter Funding Be Used For?

 

Why We Chose KickStarter

Kickstarter has become the ideal platform to launch innovative new products.Kickstarter's universal followers provide invaluable feedback for gauging salability of a new product and fine tuning design elements.

Risks and challenges

Problems and delays can occur with any manufactured item.If component suppliers don't meet their deadlines, the manufacturing process is adversely affected.

In order to minimize delays, our factory has been very particular in selecting reliable and quality-conscious component suppliers.

Since our first TravelCard production run, which fulfilled pledges for over 1400 Kickstarter backers,our factory has improved its quality control system,increased capacity and decreased lead times.

We feel we have allowed ample time to deliver all of our TravelCard Plus backers without delay,
