About this project

This year we have made the decision to record our first EP. Our goal with this is to have a professional recording not only for all of our fans and to reach new fans, but also to start sending out for bookings. Who knows what could come out of this. One life changed makes it all worth it.

You could ask what drives us, we would say God. You could ask what is our vision, we would also say God. You could ask what do we want to see in our ministry, and the answer would be God. He is the reason we breathe, the reason we go forward, the reason we are Dream Awake.

Thank you all for everything, lets make this happen!

Risks and challenges

Recording is not cheap, so we ask you for your support. The total cost for recording and creating the CDs is upwards of $2,000 and we are personally putting in as much of that as we can. But we are calling on our fans for presales to help cover part of that. Give a little or give a lot, every dollar helps us and we thank you for everything!

We want to have this project completed by July, but with all of us being full time employees it could take a little bit longer. Be patient with us.
