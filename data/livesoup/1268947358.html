About this project

New Video Alert! 

Click the link to watch this amazing time lapse video of us setting up our brewery. You're going to want to be a part of Epidemic Ales after you see our epic progress. 

https://youtu.be/KjAlt8IYpaQ

It started with a bet on a homebrewer's kit, to see who could make the better beer. The first brews were interesting, but when we started brewing great tasting, unique beer, (and we liked doing it) we put together a partnership of great people who didn't mind turning their houses into a brew floor, and we got to work.

Project video note: This is a chance to see just how hungry (thirsty) we were when we made this video. We think it's important that you know we have made some changes from our original plan but we're even more driven than we were when we only dreamed about moving into a brewery and having professional equipment.

Together we have put in over $200,000 to cover most of the startup costs and took a leap and ordered a 10BBL system that would best suit our needs and your demand for our beer. We are asking for your support and contribution with our efforts in our taproom construction. We're in the final stages of assembly for the brewery and the machinery will be fully operational soon. We can't wait for you to taste the awesome beers we have been working on for the past 4 years.

Here are 2 articles from local publications that will help you understand more about us:

Contra Costa Times: http://www.contracostatimes.com/concord/ci_28012680/art-and-wine-and-beer-walk-downtown-concord

Our website for all of our information and to contact us: http://www.epidemicales.com/

We want to make Epidemic Ales the standard in craft beer brewing. Every beer we produce is a beer we drink and will be proud to serve to Bay Area beer lovers in our tap room. We would love to meet you, have you there to experience the brewing process and culture, and help us achieve that goal.

All Donations – Receive a souvenir bottlecap magnet, full-color logo sticker, and name on donor wall. Donor wall will be a permanent fixture in the brewery and will be prominently displayed. Highest donation names will be featured first, same amount names will be arranged based on time of donation.

Donations of $150 and above get an invite to the Epidemic Ales launch party.  This will be an invite only party and will occur before the brewery is open to the public.

"Epidemic Ales represents what is best of the craft beer resurgence. I know my old friends have a passion for beer and they have a goal of making great beers with the attention to quality a great beer deserves. I fully support their efforts and look forward to trying them as they develop the quality beers that beer geeks deserve."  

~Joe Tucker, Executive Director of RateBeer

We are brewers.  We are pet lovers.  We are doing what we love, and we have fun doing it.  We want you to have fun with us.  Have a pint, relax, chat with us and bring your friends, play a game, tell us about yourself. 

We started with a 5-gallon, store-bought home-brewing system. With the demand for our brews among friends and relatives growing, it evolved and was built up to a 1BBL system. The brewing equipment quickly took over our homes so we recently moved into a 5,000 sqft warehouse in Concord, CA.  

We debuted our beers at Brian and Holly's wedding, the perfect time to get the maximum amount of feedback.  We asked everyone not to tell us what they liked about the beers, but to tell us what they didn't like, and then we set out to make the beers even better. 

We have a number of beers we love (and keep on tap in the brewery at all times), but our most popular are the ones that we will be offering as our flagship beers.

Go to www.epidemicales.com for our full beer lineup and descriptions.

Risks and challenges

We have been able to provide most of the funds needed for the first full year of operations ourselves. That includes licensing, leasing of a facility, insurance, remodeling, etc. But we need help paying off our equipment. The machinery will be fully operational soon. We will pay off the machinery while we produce beer for distribution and we'll be working on constructing and opening our taproom.

Our vision for our brewery is one of a place that you can have a pint, relax, play board games and video games, have a chat with us and your friends. A place where you actually love to hang out. We are confident that with our hard work and your support we will be opening in late summer 2015.
