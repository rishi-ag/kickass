About this project

The ResQ Wallet is a minimalist wallet made in the USA with retired fire hose that was used to save lives and property. Simple, strong and light-weight, it holds the perfect amount of cards and cash. You can have the wallet that comes with a hero's history.

When we set out to design ResQ Wallet, we wanted something that was simple, functional, and light-weight. After months of prototypes and testing several different designs, we've fine-tuned the ResQ Wallet into something we absolutely love to carry in our pockets.

Design: Rugged & Functional

The ResQ Wallet is made from hoses with a high-tech synthetic polyester mono-filament. We have recycled the highest quality hose available. They are built to protect against abrasion, heat, mold, mildew, and every-day wear and tear for up to 25 years! Needless to say, this wallet is nearly indestructible. Each wallet is very light-weight at only 1/2oz (14g) each.

Each wallet will be different and unique with its own story to tell. The hose the ResQ Wallet is made from shows battle scars and character from its service in active duty. This adds to the unique individuality of each wallet.

This is the only double sided wallet on the market that has no center divider. Its unique construction keeps the integrity of the wallet while simplifying the process and reducing weight.

The Game Plan

The funds raised through this project will be used to produce the ResQ Wallet. It will help with the costs associated with a large scale production run, including ordering the necessary supplies and equipment.

We have honed the process of making these wallets: sewing them, designing the logo, and creating packaging and labels, so the process of making the ResQ Wallet has been streamlined.

To prepare for a large order, we have enlisted the help of a U.S. based manufacturer that has agreed to help prepare the ResQ Wallets in time for our projected shipping dates. They have been helping companies deliver heavily demanded products for years with a perfect on-time delivery record.

Making the ResQ Wallet

Help Us Build a Business with a Noble Cause

From the beginning of our journey with the ResQ Wallet we wanted to be involved in giving back to the community. As firemen we see people in their toughest hour and hardest times in their life.

 There are a few charities that aid us in helping these people who fall through the proverbial "cracks" in society. They are the people that need it most but will never ask for help.

Once we are able to launch our wallet and build our company, we will be giving back to our community by donating part of our future proceeds to support those firefighter charities.

Made in the USA 

By backing this project, you're supporting local businesses. Every step of this project is local, from securing the material to sewing the wallets. We choose to only work with people we know and trust to get the job done right. No off-shore sourcing. We pride ourselves on quality of work and bringing back American manufacturing.

Most American Wallets on the Market

The fire hose material was manufactured in the USA. It was used by firefighters to save lives and property in the USA. Now it is being re-manufactured into your new ResQ Wallet, in the USA. If it were any more American, you’d have to run it up a flag pole!

Rewards

We have two additional ResQ products we are offering as part of our rewards for our loyal Kickstarter supporters.You're looking at the ResQ Koffee Koozy  and ResQ Key Keeper. If you want just one or all three, we have rewards set up for you.

Risks and challenges

The challenge of the ResQ Wallet Kickstarter will be getting the word out and convincing Kickstarters to participate with us. There are so many absolutely amazing projects out there, so It is going to be difficult to get the attention that we need to make this Kickstarter successful.
To reach as many people as possible, we are asking our friends, families, and various organizations to help us spread the word.

Risks to the actual creation of the product have been greatly reduced by prototyping not only the wallets, but the production processes that will be used. We have completed a few test runs to make sure the wallets are up to our high expectations and standards. They have been manufactured using the same people and processes that will be used when we launch actual production.
