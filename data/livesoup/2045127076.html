About this project

Nzan3 & David dBrain

For over a year now, David dBrain (Rap/Rock) artist and Nzan3 Zolnay (Drawing Artist) have become close friends to a point they've become brothers. And for some reason when both of them are together, IDEAS come from no where and won't stop coming. No matter the subject, they're an unstoppable force when it comes to imagination.

David dBrain started doing underground Rap/Rock music over 14 years ago, and has grown experience and knowledge in recording anything and making it sound 100% Professional. He also, has a talent for making voice characters. And that is why he will be doing all of the voice acting for all the characters, and doing all of the music and sound effects.

 Zane (Nzan3) Zolnay is a very talented artist that can draw about anything he sets his deep spaced mind to. Very detailed, and always motivated. Nzan3 is the one that draws the characters as they are now, and will be the one to fully animate this entire series.

Once Nzane and dBrain started talking about recording an audio series and releasing it on YouTube, they looked at each other and said, why don't we just create an animated series and expand the imagination for us and everyone else.

They both have been working everyday creating the characters, and their background stories. Building ideas for episodes, short stories, ect. And have also put together a writting team that includes them, and two other brilliant people when it comes to this type of story telling, and dialogue.  

The name they came up with for this animated series, is short and simple. And actually becomes a character itself, they named it "AROUND THE BLOCK"  

AROUND THE BLOCK

How on earth can we explain to you the VERY thing we are asking you to fund for? Well it's like this..........  

This animated series takes place in a town called "Acorn Valley" famous for it's Acorn coffee. And in this town there is a very popular block. That for some reason all walks of life, death, outer space, whatever it may be, seem to be a magnet to.  

On this block are little small business's such as "Cold Water Pawn" which is ran and operated by TWO of many characters on this show. Rick Boofee, a crazy high pitched voice red neck who has a passion for camouflage and singing. And Crazy J, a really crazy Mexican that has been Rick Boofee's friend since elementary school, who also lives in his van in the back parking lot.  

Right across the street from "Cold Water Pawn", is a pizzeria restaurant called "Don Don's Pizzeria". Owned by Big Louie, who so happens to be a retired Mafia Boss. His two sons are right along with him to help cook, and run the pizzeria. The oldest brother Big John, who would do whatever just to get back in action like the good old days. And Little Johnny, who has a hard time keeping his coke addiction away from the pizza dough. And lets not forget about Cut Throat Paulie. He is a big Meatball Muscle bound Italian man, who has a long cut across is throat. He doesn't say much, but he will make his presence known, when being the bodyguard of Big Louie. 

Right around the other side of the block, are "Buckle Wood Apartments". That happen to have a land lord named "Belt Buckle Bickle", that only says, "Belt Buckle Bickle", and "Na, Na, Na, Na. But there will be subtitles available on every episode so you know what the HELL he's really saying.  

Also at Buckle Wood Apartments, is Zane......thats right Zane. The same guy that is drawing all of this animation, will be included as a full time character. But unlike real life Zane, A.T.B Zane is a kind hearted Stoner drug dealer who loves women and cooking five star restaurant dishes.  

And then there's Pastor Due. Who has his church of healing and wounding right across the street from "Buckle Wood Apartments". Every Sunday morning, the great people of Acorn Valley, take their place in church, to hear the great speech that Pastor Due always delivers. Now no matter if he his right nor wrong in his preaching, he is always blunt and honest, and very to the point.  

The cast members even voiced their opinion and predictions on the Mayweather vs Pacquiao fight. So to give you an idea of these very animated characters, just listen.    

 Concept Art & Sketches

But these are just a few, of many, many, many characters. Not including ideas and plans of episodes that dBrain & Nzan3 have already written. This animated series will be the main focus, to get completed once we get the funding we need, to build an empire. If you would like to see sketches and concept art of these characters as they are created. Please subscribe to https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCdYaumMTLXHeoRCSMq2BxVA?view_as=public youtube channel. We will try and post them here when we get the time. As well, as small AUDIO skits, so you can get top know the characters a little better.

Why So Much?

We need $15,000 to buy all of our equipment. From sound boards, music and vocal software's. Desktop Computers and Monitors to support animation and editing software's. Music keyboards, HD cameras, Green Screen Kits, Drawing/Animation Tablet, and Promotion fees. As well as the money to help support the rewards we are offering such as physical copies of cd's, tshirts, posters and shipping fees.

Risks and challenges

Once we get the funding, we can't promise how long it will be until we release the first season..
We will be working with animation software that THE SIMPSONS & FAMILY GUY uses for their famous series.
But we can tell you that we will push everyday to make at least three episodes before the end of the year.
We will begin production Mid August, and will work on a Halloween Episode, Thanksgiving Episode, and Christmas Episode.
And then begin the actual Season One.
We will be working on it everyday. Learning how to create each character and making the design and layout of the town.
We have everything written, and ready to go.
All that's left is learning the animation software we are needing to get. Learning the tricks, so we can put out a lot of material at a full schedule pace.
