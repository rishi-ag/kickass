About this project

Street Meat is a first of its kind Hybrid television and internet show. Born from pieces of the web but made for TV. Imagine if the Internet and the Circus had a baby that could adapt to any media environment. Street Meat is shot mobile and in undisclosed locations and has a different theme and show intro for every episode. The Themed portion is inspired by current events helping to keep the content very fresh and unique. Built like a travel show with the presence of a variety show, we have a host, sidekick, dog, band, celebrity interviews and a ton of spoofs. The thing that really sets Street Meat apart aside from its flamboyant and exotic nature is that its themed around a portion of the show called, "Go Tweet Yourself," where people submit in Tweets about news relevant content that they want us to discuss. This allows the show to change with every episode keeping a fresh and ever changing feel to allow maximum unpredictability. Street Meat looks to change the talk show model and find new and interesting ways to think outside the studio. From a side kick in an inner tube to a host that covers everything from Malaysia air to celebrities and their latest movie roles, Street Meat has got it all and truly is a talk show on steroids. We are in an interesting time where cable television is crumbling and valuations on production companies specifically companies with new and original content are flourishing to new heights. Street Meat was created with a focus on branding and sponsorship opportunities that rank in line with content and longevity and is sure to be a driving force in the media space to come.

Funds raised through Kickstarter will involve a new audience and allow the community to be a part of new media.  The funds will go directly to executing our second and third episodes.  The first episode can be seen online...

http://youtu.be/ade22m5QMfE
