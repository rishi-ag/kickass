Ugh, another Kickstarter Solar Charger?

Thanks to your support, we were able to bring to market the Ultra Compact Solar USB Charger in 2013. Â We took the three most requested features and created The Badger: Waterproof USB Solar Panel. Â 

  • Waterproof, super durable, with protection for gadgets.
  • High amperage USB output for insanely fast charging.
  • Watt Meter to track charging speed and power.

Why would I need this?

If you're just looking for a Solar USB Charger to use through your apartment window, this probably isn't the charger for you.

We designed The Badger to go where you go, do what you do, and keep charging even in the worst possible conditions. Â This means the beach, the trail, the mountain, the river, or the ski slope. Â If you're using a gadget somewhere rough, The Badger will be there charging it up.

DisastersÂ strike when we leastÂ expectÂ them. Â Events such asÂ hurricanes, tornados, floods, and even the occasional zombie invasion can knock out power for days. Â Keeping The Badger Solar Panel in your emergencyÂ preparednessÂ kit or Bug Out Bag means that all your basic power needs will be met.

Water-Proof or Water-Resistant?

The most requested feature from our backers and our customers was a degree of waterproofness. Â We didn't just make these panels "water resistant", we made them full-on water-proof. Â To illustrate this, we stuck a panel in a fish tank for two days. Â Then we took it out and started charging our phones with it.

In more technical terms, we managed to snag an IP65 rating (how industry measures water proof and dust proof levels in gadgets), the best of any solar panel we could find. Â What this means to you is that you Â never have to worry about The Badger failing you due to water damage. Â Ever.

The Pouch

Much like a kangaroo protects its joey inside of a pouch, we started by protecting everything inside of a highly water resistant nylon zipper pouch. Â Keep your gadgets secure inside to protect them from the elements.

To make things extra fun we engineered some awesome elastic holders to secure your gadgets, organize your cables, and keep accessories handy.

The Cap

The main problem with nearly every other USB Solar Panel is that the USB port and circuit board are directly exposed to the elements. Â A bit of water gets inside, goodbye solar panel.

We solved this problem by engineering a tight rubber cap over a separated USB port. Â When the cap is in use, nothing will get inside. Â Not even an aquarium's worth of water. Â If for some reason you leave the cap off and get water in the port, no need to worry. Â We've separated it from all the important electronics which means you just need to let it air dry and you're back in business.

The Guts

To make sure that the all important circuitry is protected, we've encased it inside several layers of plastic, rubber, and sealant to make sure nothing gets inside.Â 

High Output, Fast Charging

The second most requested feature from our backers was a higher output USB port for faster charging of gadgets. Â A high output fast charging USB port is even more important now due to the ever increasing size of gadgets.

If one USB port isn't enough for your charging needs, add on a hub. Â Due to the extremely high output of The Badger you can actually split the power between multiple devices at once. Â We're partial to bringing along a little Travel 3 Port USB Hub when we're out on the road so we can charge multiple GoPro Cameras.

(While not necessary for the vast majority of people, it is possible to daisy chain two of our panels together to double the output or provide even more power to a hub.)

What Meter? Watt Meter?

One thing that has always annoyed us about solar chargers is that you're never able to tell how much solar energy they're actuallyÂ generating. Â While many people are not concerned about the exact numbers, it's often helpful when positioning a solar panel or figuring out how long a gadget will take to charge. Â Instead of integrating a meter into the Badger directly, which would have made water proofing difficult and expensive, we're including an easy to use, removable, USB Watt Meter.

If you want to use it, use it. Â If you don't want to use it, leave it at home or secured in its elastic holder. Â It's a handy gadget to help you optimize your solar output.

Auto Restart

Even today, many commercially available solar chargers are plagued by a rather annoying flaw. Â When a cloud or shadow passes over a solar panel, any gadget plugged in will cease to charge.. Â Completely stop. Â Dead. Â At least until you unplug and replug it back in. Â This leads to many gadgets failing to charge, and a lot of unhappy campers.

Our Auto Restart technology makes sure that you'll never have to unplug and replug your device to keep it charging. Â If your panel does suffer from a cloud or shadow, it will stop charging. However, it will automaticity start up again without any intervention on your part.Â It's an important fix to a very frustrating problem.

How Fast Does It Actually Charge?

Judging the speeds at which solar chargers actually charge gadgets is tricky. Â It all depends on the speed at which your gadget is designed to charge (every gadget is different), the size of your gadget's battery, and the output of the USB port. Is It Durable?

You may be wondering how tough The Badger really is. Â Well, we hit it with a bowling ball, ran over it with a car, shot it with a shotgun, and it STILL worked. Â (Not at the same time, that would be silly.) Â Many solar USB chargers only protect their panels with glass or epoxy, both of which are prone to shattering. Â The Badger features a tough plastic coating on its solar panels to prevent scratches, cracking, and breaks while still allowing for excellent charging.Â

Power Banks

The sun isn't always shining, and there's that pesky thing called "night" that happens every 12 hours. Since our panels don't store energy like a battery, we're giving you the option to add on an actual battery. Not just any USB Power Bank, but a Power Bank that's just as tough and water proof as The Badger. The two make a great pair. Use The Badger, or any other USB power source, to charge up your Power Bank so that you can take your power with you.

We're giving you two options to choose from; Â Our 6,000mAh Power Bank has enough juice to charge up a smart phone 3-4 times as well as being a kick-butt flashlight. Â Rated IP66 for protection against water and dust. Â (That's a good thing.)

Our 15,000mAh Power Bank is built like a brick. It has two 2.4A USB ports for insanely fast charging of two gadgets at once, it has enough power to charge up a smart phone more than 10 times or even charge up an tablet, and you can run it over with a car or drop it to the bottom of a lake without destroying it. All this in a form factor that still fits inside of The Badger's protective pouch.

Grab a Power Bank if you want one. They're handy for when the lights go out, you want to play some cards in your tent, or anywhere you need power.

If nothing else, this project will help support our educational solar electronics business as well as allow us to put on workshops to share our knowledge of solar energy with teachers and educators. Â Your support will go well beyond just funding a super tough solar charger.

Our biggest challenge will just be logistics. We've got our manufacturing, shipping, and distribution all lined up. FAQ

  • Can you add more USB ports to the panels?

    We decided against that. We wanted to focus on making the product super waterproof, which was most simple to do with a single USB port. However, if you find yourself needing extra USB ports there is a simple solution. Since The Badger has such a high output you can easily buy a sub $10 USB hub from most anywhere and use that to expand the available ports.
