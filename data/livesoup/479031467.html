Director's Notes

We put Bad Guy #2 online for the duration of this campaign. Then it goes away until after its fest run. It's had great success so far. Including it's world premiere at Fantastic Fest 2014. Hope you enjoy!

From the team that brought you Bad Guy #2 comes a comedy short film about a young metalhead who gets handed down a guitar by his metalhead dad who got it from his dad and so on and so on. This badass guitar comes with some rules though. And of course our irresponsible metalhead hero doesn't follow those rules and the carnage begins! 

We have the same SFX team from Bad Guy #2 on board! The carnage we speak of is in the form of over-the top, practical effect driven, bloody craziness. Extreme to the point of comedy. We are gonna step it up even more this time. Right now the script calls for 8 different crazy practical effect set ups. If we get to the point of stretch goals we can add more. It'll be so worth it I promise! 

Here's a picture of Bill & Ted. Because it's Bill and Ted!

 Here are some early storyboards created by the very talented artist Caleb Sawyer.

The hand me down guitar has to be badass. It's been around for hundreds of years. We are toying with many possible looks and backstories for it. Here's some early concept art for it. We will add more here as the campaign goes on.

We are gonna need some long locks for our metalhead hero so he can headbang with the best of them. So some of the money will be going towards that. Just thought you guys would want to know. Metal Wig!

WRITER/DIRECTOR - Chris McInroy is a writer/director based in Austin, TX. He currently works as a director/editor of video content for such clients as Sprite, Capital One, Chase, Phillips 66, UPS, Allstate and Ford. He watches an average of 180 movies per year and his favorite genres are comedy and horror. His latest short film, Bad Guy #2, premiered at Fantastic Fest 2014 and is currently on the festival circuit. He likes metal, queso and the Alamo Drafthouse.

PRODUCER - Ben Steinbauer made his feature directing debut with the award-winning comedy documentary, Winnebago Man, which was released theatrically in 2010. Michael Moore called it “One of the funniest documentaries ever made!” and Roger Ebert named it one of his favorite documentaries of 2010. Ben and Chris have worked together on numerous commercials and films including Bad Guy #2. 

PRODUCER - Berndt Mader is an Emmy Award winning writer/director/producer whose debut feature, 5 Time Champion, premiered at the 2011 SXSW Film Festival. The film was released by Warner Bros. in 2012. Also in 2012, Berndt co-produced David Gordon Green’s feature Prince Avalanche starring Paul Rudd and Emile Hirsch. The film had its world premiere at the 2013 Sundance Film Festival. Berndt, Ben and Chris all met while attending the MFA program at the University of Texas at Austin.

PRODUCER - Veronica Leon is a producer in Austin, Texas. She started working for the Vice President and co-founder of Troublemaker Studios and President of EYA Productions, Elizabeth Avellán, in 2013.

CO-PRODUCER - Denny Phillips is a Creative Director at a top ad agency in Austin, TX. He and his team of Hans Zimmerman, Israel Beer and Caleb Sawyer are in charge of the look and feel of all marketing for Death Metal. 

MUSIC - Bird Peterson is an Austin based DJ/Wizard. His originals have made him known across the globe with releases on Warner Bros, Ed Banger, Mad Decent, Sony, Teenage Riot Records, and quite a few others. His remixing of artists like Three 6 Mafia, MSTRKRFT, Tiesto, Feadz, The Wu-Tang Clan, Robin Thicke, Ocelot, Felix Cartal, Gucci Mane, Wale, and soooo many others have set the planet’s ears aflame. He’s shared the stage with such greats as MSTRKRFT, Diplo, Tiesto, DJ AM, Craze, The Bloody Beetroots, Rusko, Chase & Status, and hundreds more. He scored Mcinroy’s previous 3 movies and contributed music to Steinbauer’s Winnebago Man.

SPECIAL EFFECTS - Doug Field and Susan Benson of Bloodyfiled SFX. Credits include Machete, Friday the 13th, Teeth, All The Boys Love Mandy Lane and of course Bad Guy #2.

PRODUCTION DESIGNER -  Jake Kuykendall's recent credits include Joe, Manglehorn, Bad Guy #2 and 7 Chinese Brothers.
