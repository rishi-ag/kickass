About this project

Popcorn’s Revolution

Welcome to gopo

Popcorn is ready for a revolution. We know that flavored popcorn is not new to the market. Popcorn has been one of America's most popular snacks for some time. From strolling around local festivals to watching a summer blockbuster, popcorn is America’s #1 go-to snack. Recently, a revival of gourmet popcorn has been popping up in Chicago, Seattle, New York…(add something here). We are jumpstarting our own, unique revolution to bring back delicious, local, and diverse gourmet popcorn to Missouri- gopo. What makes our gourmet popcorn and gopo different from all the other popcorn on store shelves and at movie theaters? Our popcorn is our art. Each gopo flavor is a mix of our daily inspirations, local ingredients and passion. We tailor our own recipes, in small batches, to make popcorn magic. Our ever-growing list of popcorn flavors include something for everyone as we can make any flavor organic, vegan or gluten free upon request. Find your new favorite gopo flavor at the bottom of this page.

Why do a Kickstarter? 

gopo just had a birthday - we are in our 2nd year of business. We are encouraged and excited at our progress over the last year, beginning with burning batches in our home kitchen to having a consistent and quality popcorn product today crafted in a commercial kitchen.

We have served gopo to over …. well a lot of folks - all with great response and more repeat orders than we can keep up with our limited kitchen equipment. The equivalent of trying to make an excellent espresso without an espresso machine. In order to keep up with the demand for gopo we need to take things to the next level. To spread the popcorn love, gopo needs to purchase commercial popcorn equipment that will get more gopo to our growing popcorn revolution’s local fans and build a sustainable business. We also REALLY want to be downtown, so we need a space or mobile station to make it just for you, when you want it...SNACK TIME!

So what equipment do we need?  

They have names like: Air Popper, Kernel Sifter, Mini Mixer's, Tumbler, Convection Oven, and Trailer.

Why the end of May? 

With your help we will purchase Popcorn Equipment to run at full capacity, this means spending minutes popping and mixing instead of our current rate of hours. With the equipment, we can pursue our next goal of opening up our own store/space in Columbia, where we plan to expand our current parties, events, and community outreach programs. You may not have seen gopo out there, but rest assured once we have the right tools - delicious snacks, meals, and parties will be ever more available to the community.  

We know with this equipment boost, gopo could grow into a Columbia staple. Our desire is to continue to bring excellent popcorn to Columbia and Missouri and beyond!

Early gopo beginnings

Popcorn has been central snack in Nick’s family for generations. Our extended family always had some variety of flavored popcorn at family gatherings. Nick loves hosting parties and perfected his art of having the right combo of tasty food and great interaction to keep his gatherings hopping in college. Brooke has experience in event catering and had a dream of running her own event space and catering company.

Why Columbia MO? 

When Nick and Brooke married a few years ago they explored many ways to start a local business, from niche food businesses to opening an event center with catering. When the idea came to combine Nick’s love for popcorn and parties, with Brooke’s expertise in event coordinating, gopo popped to life! From there, they committed to loving and serving Columbia, Missouri and to put its flavors into the gourmet popcorn whenever possible. Take a look at our Ingredient page to see what we have been able to do so far! 

The community got us where we are today. You may have even been a part of getting us here. The popcorn community is always inspiring us to try new things, different recipes, creations, and ideas. All we really do is the work of making and packaging it. It was friends and family from the community that even came up with our name, our favorite flavors and our special, local ingredients. It is the community that partners with us to bring the rest of the world the best popcorn that we can craft!

gopo wants to reward your help!  

When you commit to help gopo expand we will reward you with mounds of popcorn and thankfulness. Check out the reward levels in the right column of this page and choose what fits you best. Maybe you want a bag of your favorite flavor or you want buckets and buckets of popcorn for years to come or at your next big party or wedding, gopo will thank you with what we do best - gourmet popcorn.

Risks and challenges

With continuing business, the risks and challenges are what make it worth it to do business. We already make and sell great gourmet popcorn, just ask our Facebook fans, the challenge for us will be to develop a great team, and to lead that team to become better as a unit than we are by ourselves.

There are direct and indirect competitors out there who also make and sell great gourmet popcorn, the challenge is to use them as allies to continue to improve on what has already been done in the past. With your help, we strive to not only continue to bring to market a great product, but to build a team that enriches a community!

If we do not reach our goal, you will not be charged anything, you can still purchase gopo from us through a variety of measures, but unless those of you that still wanted those rewards just end up giving us the money, we will fulfill the rest of our contracts, close up our operation, then pursue other ventures.

Thanks so much for reading all this way if you have, if you are in the Columbia area and want to try some gopo, email me at comogopo@gmail.com to find out how you can get your hands on a sample. - Nick Bartlett

FAQ

  • Where do you get your popcorn?

    From America. We get all of our ingredients from where clients want them. If they don't mind, we have our sources, that are very industry standard. We know organic producers, who sell higher cost popcorn, so if you would like, we can get it for you!

    Last updated: Mon, May 4 2015 1:16 am EDT
  • Can you do ____ flavor?

    Yes? It really depends on the availability, everything has a price. We have gone as far as New Orleans for a flavor for a customer, so if you want to pay for it, we are happy to make it for you!

    Last updated: Mon, May 4 2015 12:56 am EDT
  • Do you use preservatives?

    Not directly, unless you consider sugar and salt a preservative. Some of our ingredients do come with preservatives in them, but we do not add any chemicals to ours for the purpose of preservation. We make it to taste delicious, whether it keeps depends if you have the constitution to not eat the entire bag by yourself.

    Last updated: Mon, May 4 2015 1:16 am EDT
  • Where do you get your inspiration?

    We come from a long line of foodies, and there are recurring flavors in the market, however I am inspired my local chefs and other popcorn makers. I look at what Chef's are doing in the market, and either try to partner with them, or make my own variation on what they are making. From time to time I also am in the mood for some food, and see if I can recreate that taste with popcorn. My latest creation, Flan! It has the great flavor, if not the creaminess of the custard.

    Last updated: Mon, May 4 2015 1:16 am EDT
  • If you are already in business, what is stopping you from funding the equipment on your own?

    Great question, and thanks for cutting to the heart of the issue. I don't do this full time. I volunteer with my community multiple times a week. I also have a full time job where I invest a lot of physical and emotional energy. The short of it is that our current methods of cooking are prohibitive of efficient sales. With your help, we will get the industry standard equipment that we have been sorely missing in our operations. If things go really well, I will be able to be a popcorn chef full time, and bring excellent food and parties to people in more ways than I would have been able to on my own.

    Last updated: Mon, May 4 2015 1:16 am EDT
  • Why are the estimated times of delivery different?

    The equipment has to ship to us, and depending on the numbers of supporters, we may have to make several large batches to get them all out on time. If you are really hurting for some goodness, consider ordering some from us now while you wait. Special orders are our specialty.

    Last updated: Mon, May 4 2015 1:16 am EDT
  • The retail value of the rewards doesn't seem like they will cover the cost of the equipment?

    True. However, we need the upfront cash for the equipment. We already have clients who want us to produce more than we are currently able to supply. With your help, we will be able to get you your flavors, then go on to stock the shelves of other local stores and businesses.

    Last updated: Mon, May 4 2015 1:16 am EDT
  • Why are there limits on the quantities of some of the rewards?

    We can only prepare so much. We have a goal to be in business for five years, the limits on each reward would give us enough to be at full capacity for the duration. After that, we have in place to expand to another city, making unique flavors for that area. However, if we don't meet our initial goal, we won't charge you anything, unless you want to purchase some outside of Kickstarter yourself.

    Last updated: Mon, May 4 2015 1:16 am EDT
  • What is up with your last reward, the wedding one?

    We love weddings, and we love to travel. We also love being in business because it allows us to travel for weddings. If you want us to come to your wedding to set up a popcorn whatever, and we are available, that sounds awesome. Worse case, we make a ton of popcorn for your wedding, and ship it out to you so you can give it to your guests. The value starts at $300 per wedding, but could easily go up from there. There is no fine print here, you have blessed us, and we want to be a blessing to you in return.

    Last updated: Wed, May 6 2015 1:27 am EDT
