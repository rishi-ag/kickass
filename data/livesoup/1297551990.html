About this project

Stikey is a portable, magnetic stand for smartphones (android, iOS & windows) that works equally well on portrait and landscape orientation.

Stikey tidies up your cables and prevents them from getting tangled and damaged when carrying them around.

It is designed to always be with you by easily attaching to your keys.

Stikey comes complete with its packaging which is a magnetic mount in disguise!

The magnetic mount is conveniently incorporated in the packaging as its base.

It adds more mounting possibilities for your smartphone, for Stikey, or your keys. 

Stikey has a built-in magnet that securely attaches to the small and thin metal tab positioned on the back of your phone or case. 

Every Stikey will be delivered complete with its packaging.
Early birds and Stikey + 1 incorporate 1 magnetic wall mount with an extra adhesive tape.You also get 1 stainless steel tab with a tab positioning guide and 1 easy access keyring.
Stikey + 2 has a slightly different packaging, incorporating 2 wall mounts and has 1 extra tab (2 in total).  
Extra wall mounts and tabs can be added in all pledges by adding £3 for each extra wall mount and £1 for one extra metal tab.

We want Stikey to reach as many people as possible so we have decided to give all of our backers free shipping, asking only for one thing in return: help us out with the campaign by spreading the word by pressing the "share this project button".

As soon as we reach £14.000, all backers will have the option to choose their Stikey to be in two different colors, mix and matching from all 6 available colors! Check the picture below to get an idea and follow the link https://bitly.com/1bUjn7t to see close ups from the possible color combinations!

Stikey is made from polyurethane and has a matte finish similar to that of hard rubber. There is a built in neodymium magnet, that helps Stikey to securely attach on the stainless steel tab on the back of the smartphone or case.
The innovative packaging comprises of the magnetic wall mount which acts as its base and the rest is recyclable cardboard. This ensures minimum waste.

The metal tab is laser cut with rounded corners and is 0.8mm thick. It is attached with a 0.2mm thin 3M VHB tape that has a very strong bond and is made to be taken off without leaving any residue behind. The tape works on many materials, as long as they are flat, so you can even stick the tab on the case instead of the smartphone.

MORE CREATIVE USES...

Stikey works with iPad mini and other similar tablets on landscape orientation. Ideal for typing and watching movies!

The magnetic mount works in your car as well!

IDEA & DEVELOPMENT

We developed Stikey in order to enhance our smartphone experience and add the element of versatility in the way we use them. Another important aspect of our design approach was portability. We wanted users to have Stikey with them at all times in order for them to benefit from its features whenever needed.

We have analysed different user profiles and developed several concept designs.

From the initial concepts we moved on early prototypes which we tested with users for functionality and form.

Finally we ended end up with the final models.

In order to offer a complete product to our backers we decided to design the packaging. We believe that product packaging should not be an afterthought and treated as potential waste, so it was designed with the goal to bring extra functionality to the product and value to the user.

MANUFACTURING

Throughout the development we have met and discussed our designs with various manufacturers in Greece and in the UK. After we agreed on the timeframes and related costs, we selected the production plant that the final product will be produced.

Our decision was based on 3 factors: 

  • Production capability for the required volume 
  • Production track record and high quality product samples 
  • Close proximity and easy access location, in order to supervise the process closely and avoid any delays caused by long distance production

FUNDS BREAKDOWN

We strived to absorb as much of the cost for the development and manufacture of Stikey as possible. Therefore, the funds from our backers will go to:

  • £5000 for materials and production costs of the first 1000 Stikeys. 
  • £7000 for the production of the required molds for the injection molding process.

a few words on magnetism...

The storage technology on smartphones and USB sticks, that you may have on your keys, does not include any moving parts therefore cannot be affected by magnetic force. 
The only thing which is temporarily affected from our magnet is your smartphones digital compass when Stikey is attached to your smartphone and used as a stand. But using the compass while having your phone on a stand is improbable.
As far as navigation is concerned it does not require the compass to work, position and direction is calculated while moving through the signals sent back and forth between the smartphone and a satellite in space.
Check out the following links for additional info:

  • http://www.techhive.com/article/116572/article.html

Risks and challenges

We carried out and agreed a detailed production and delivery plan with the selected manufacturing unit. (tooling 7-9 weeks, production capability 2k Stikeys within 3 weeks). In that way we tried to ensure that we will be able to stick with our schedule and make our backers happy.

We are more than confident that the final product will exceed expectation as far as quality and function is concerned. We have carried out a detailed production and delivery plan for Stikey and are committed to stick to it and send out rewards a.s.a.p. But in the development of a new product a minor unpredicted issue might cause delays, we have worked hard to narrow down these possibilities as much as possible. We prefer to ship the best product possible later, than an inferior one sooner. In any case, we will be in contact with our backers throughout the production process, letting them know exactly how the process goes.

We are also waiting for backers questions and comments throughout the manufacturing process of Stikey.

FAQ

  • How does Stikey work with my phone?

    In order for stikey to work, a stainless steel tab needs to be placed on the back of the phone. This metal tab is attracted by magnetism and the built in magnet inside Stikey safely attaches to it to function as a stand.

    Last updated: Fri, May 22 2015 10:43 am EDT
  • Is Stikey only made for iPhones?

    No, Stikey works will any smartphone or phablet as long as the backside is flat. This is crucial for the stainless steel tab to be safely positioned.

    Last updated: Fri, May 22 2015 10:43 am EDT
  • Will Stikey work with my phone, and what are the limits?

    Stikey works with any smartphone or phablet and is tested with devices up to 5.5” and 175 grams in weight with and without a case on. That means that all these devices work well with Stikey on portrait and landscape orientation. For larger smartphones in size or weight we are confident, judging from the performance with our larger device, that Stikey will work equally well and are happy to test any individual case if we are asked to.

    Last updated: Fri, May 22 2015 10:43 am EDT
  • Can i use Stikey with my tablet?

    We have tested Stikey with small tablets, with an iPad mini retina in particular, extensively and can positively say that it works very well on landscape orientation for watching movies or writing with an external keyboard. Making Stikey work with more tablets would require to make it considerably bigger, thus sacrificing a very important aspect, its portability.

    Last updated: Fri, May 22 2015 10:43 am EDT
  • Is the tab that sticks on the phone a magnet?

    No! This is not a magnet. It is a grade 430 stainless steel that is laser cut and finished from a stainless steel sheet. The dimensions of the tab is 17mmx17mmx0.8mm and along with the 0.2mm 3M adhesive tape that is already positioned on it, it measures only 1mm in thickness.

    Last updated: Fri, May 22 2015 10:43 am EDT
  • Can i use the tab if i use a case on my phone?

    The 1mm thickness, ensures that it can be attached on the back of the phone and allow for cases to be put on top of it. We have tested many of them and found that the majority works fine!

    Last updated: Fri, May 22 2015 10:43 am EDT
  • Is the stainless steel tab reusable?

    The tab can only be attached on your phone or case once! It can easily be taken out with dental floss and leaves no residue behind, but cannot be re-attached afterwards because there is a risk of the bond not being secure enough to function properly.

    Last updated: Fri, May 22 2015 10:43 am EDT
  • What if i have more than one device?

    More tabs can be added to any pledge level. They cost £1 each, so you need to add an extra £1 for every extra tab you want to receive with your Stikey.

    Last updated: Fri, May 22 2015 10:43 am EDT
  • How is it possible to attach the phone to surfaces?

    The phone can be attached to all flat surfaces (such as mirrors, cupboards, walls, car dashboards etc) with the included magnetic mount that is part of the product packaging and also has a strong built in magnet.

    Last updated: Fri, May 22 2015 10:43 am EDT
  • How is the magnetic mount put on surfaces? Do they have to be magnetic or metal?

    The magnetic mount is securely attached to any flat surface with a 3M VHB adhesive tape, similar to the one used on the tab but 0.5mm in thickness for better results on the different surfaces. It’s mount comes with an extra 3M tape so it can be repositioned once.

    Last updated: Fri, May 22 2015 10:43 am EDT
  • What weight can the magnetic mount support?

    The bond between the wall mount and the phone’s stainless steel tab can hold easily a 200g device (not tested for heavier yet) The bond between stikey's magnet and the stainless steel tab of the phone can hold up to 400g at around 60 degrees angle.

    Last updated: Fri, May 22 2015 10:43 am EDT
  • Can i buy more mounts?

    Yes, more mounts can be added on any of the pledge levels and cost £3 each. So you have to add an extra £3 for each magnetic mount you want to receive with your Stikey. Each mount comes with one extra adhesive 3M VHB tape.

    Last updated: Fri, May 22 2015 10:43 am EDT
  • Is the 3M tape able to be taken on and off from the surface it is attached?

    The 3M tape on the stainless steel tab is not reusable. Once on the phone it cannot be taken off and added on another device. More tabs can be purchased at £1 each.  For the magnetic mount only, that can be done once or twice but very short after the first try (after 15 minutes maximum) and only on a clean surface. Since the chances of reattaching the mount are higher compared to the stainless steel tab, we will give you an extra 3M tape so you can position the mount safely at any time.

    Last updated: Fri, May 22 2015 10:43 am EDT
  • Does the 3M tape leave glue on my phone if i take it off?

    The 3M tape we use is specifically designed to be very strong and to be taken off without leaving any residue behind. As an example, the same adhesive tape is used to attach action cameras on very expensive cars for filming purposes without having to worry about damaging the paint job.

    Last updated: Fri, May 22 2015 10:43 am EDT
  • How strong is the 3M tape bond?

    The 3M tape bond is extremely strong. It is used to secure go pro cameras on very demanding application where very big forces are applied, e.g. securing the cameras on helmets while sky diving.

    Last updated: Fri, May 22 2015 10:43 am EDT
  • Do i get more 3M tapes? What happens if i want more?

    The magnetic mount comes with the adhesive tape attached and with an extra 3M tae so its can be repositioned once. The tape we use is a 3M VHB 0.5mm adhesive tape and can be purchased in hardware stores that store 3M products if you need to get more. The stainless steel tab comes with the adhesive tape attached and you can replace the adhesive tape on your own. It is not very easy so we suggest that you buy more tabs since they are only £1 each.
    Again the tape used on the stainless steel tab is a 3M VHB 0.2mm adhesive tape, and can be purchased in hardware stores if you want to go the DYI way!

    Last updated: Fri, May 22 2015 10:43 am EDT
  • How many keys can the magnet hold?

    The bond between Stikey's magnet when used as a magnetic keychain depends on the surface you want to hang your keys (e.g fridge, coat racks etc.) check this link to get an idea. http://s280.photobucket.com/user/iliaskalog/Keychain/story If you choose to hang your keys with Stikey on, on the magnetic mount, the bond created can hold easily around 8-10 regular keys. 

    Last updated: Fri, May 22 2015 10:43 am EDT
  • How can i attach Stikey to my keys?

    Stikey can be attached to your keyring with the supplies easy access keyring, so you can quickly get it on and of when you need to use it. This keyring can also be used as your main keyring if you prefer and can hold many keys.

    Last updated: Fri, May 22 2015 10:43 am EDT
  • Will the magnet inside Stikey damage my phone?

    There is nothing in any smartphone or tablet that can be damaged or permanently affected by our magnet. There are many magnetic product that are sold for quite sometime without any issues. You can check out the following link for more info on magnetism and safety. http://www.techhive.com/article/116572/article.html

    Last updated: Fri, May 22 2015 10:43 am EDT
  • Will the magnet erase my phone data?

    The storage technology on smartphones and tablets (just like USB sticks) does not have any moving parts and isn't affected by magnetism.

    Last updated: Fri, May 22 2015 10:43 am EDT
  • Will the magnet affect the compass and my GPS navigation accuracy?

    The compass when Stikey is attached as a stand or when the smartphone is mounted on the magnetic mount, is temporarily affected as is expected. But as soon as the phone is taken away from the magnet the compass is recalibrate and works perfectly fine. For the explorers out there, you should not worry since Stikey will not be attached to your phone when you are out and about on your explorations and as for GPS navigation and the magnetic mount in your car, GPS technology does not require the compass to function properly. It can calculate direction on its own. All the above have been tested thoroughly by us during development without noticing any problems at all.

    Last updated: Fri, May 22 2015 10:43 am EDT
  • How can i buy more than 1 Stikeys?

    In order to buy more than 1 Stikey, you have to choose any of those pledges: - Twin pack, which includes 2 Stikeys - Family back, which includes 3 Stikeys - Full house, which includes 6 Stikeys For more info for every pledge check out the graphic that illustrates in detail what you get with each pledge.

    Last updated: Fri, May 22 2015 10:43 am EDT
  • How can i buy more than the pledge options offer?

    For more than 3 Stikeys, choose the family back and add the amount of £10 (just like the early bird pledge) for every extra Stikey (complete with the packaging and mount) you want. The same applies if you want more than 6. You have to choose the Full house pledge option and add another £10 for every extra Stikey (complete with the packaging and mount) you want.

    Last updated: Fri, May 22 2015 10:43 am EDT
  • How do i choose colours for my Stikey?

    When the campaign ends, and if it is successful, you will be automatically be sent an email from us asking for preferences such as colour, and double checking the shipping address.

    Last updated: Fri, May 22 2015 10:43 am EDT
  • Will i be able to get my Stikey in 2 different colours (mix and match) after all?

    The option to choose your Stikey in 2 different colours will be unlocked only if we reach £14.000 before the end of the campaign. The reason is that using 2 different colours will result in higher production costs from our manufacturers, so a higher goal in necessary so we can afford it. Even so, we chose a very low goal, only £2.000 more, in order to make sure that we can reach it and give all our backers the options to choose their Stikey in any of the 30 combinations from our 6 available colours!

    Last updated: Fri, May 22 2015 10:43 am EDT
