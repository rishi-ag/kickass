About this project

 nexpaq allows you to easily customize, enhance, and add features to your smartphone through the case. To put it simply: You can add and remove physical “modules” (think Lego bricks with special powers) directly from the back of your case without powering down! 

It's time to allow your phone to do things it was really made for. Unlimited potential all in the palm of your hand.

Each nexpaq case has built-in battery power to boot (1000 mAh). This provides your smartphone with a significant amount of extra juice and powers all of the modules.

 nexpaq is the first, and only, modular technology that can be swapped between Android and iPhone devices. What does this mean? Collect and share modules with other nexpaq users, regardless of their operating system allegiance!

It also means that if you decide to change smartphones you'll still be able to use your modules on your new phone, as long as you have a nexpaq case for it.

If you have an iPhone 6, Samsung Galaxy S6 Edge or Samsung Galaxy S5 then you can use nexpaq. We are looking to expand the lineup so please let us know on our poll [click the link below this] what smartphones you want nexpaq to work with!

 To make this new smartphone model happen we will need a minimum of 1,000 pledges. This means that we need 1,000 of you to back iPhone 6 Plus for it to go into production.

We know that we are being ambitious, and we want to do everything we can to make this new case happen which is why we have a contingency plan.

If iPhone 6 Plus does not achieve 1,000 backers during the next 17 days of the campaign, we will extend the pre-orders (at a higher price) for two months on our website and continue to rally until we get the minimum order amount. We will make sure we keep you updated with how it is getting on and of course if (or hopefully when) we hit the 1,000!

What happens if we don’t get 1,000 backers? If after the 2 months of pre-orders through our website, we do not get the 1,000, we will refund your pledges (minus KickStarter fees).

The batpaq is now available for pledging. It functions the same as a nexpaq case and works with any smartphone. It even has 2600mAh of rechargeable battery!

The batpaq: the first modular battery pack.

The batpaq is the ultimate companion for your smartphone.

Any smartphone. All modules work the same as they do on the nexpaq, connecting via usb cable or even wireless Bluetooth. The batpaq fits conveniently in your pocket or handbag.

Just some of what's possible with batpaq:

  • Slide 1 or 2 Amplified Speaker modules in for a remote audio device.
  •  With Hotkey modules it becomes a remote control for your phone (even for pictures and selfies)  
  • Stack USB, Backup, or SD card modules and make it a storage drive.
  • Make it a super power bank by stacking battery modules, or use it to recharge them.  
  • Stack Air Quality and Temperature and Humidity modules to get remote information
1. Put your phone in the nexpaq case
2. Choose your modules and slide them in
3. Start the app and control your modules  

You can access all of your modules through the app. Simply click on the module to check a reading of how much storage and battery you have. When you swap in a new module, the app will automatically update so you can get going straight away.

 Even if your case is not attached to your phone, you can still switch on the case and remotely control the modules via bluetooth from your nexpaq app.

Risks and challenges

Any hardware products, especially a modular general purpose product like nexpaq, comes with its risks and challenges. The nexpaq team has been working hard to address every one of them so we can deliver a high quality product which fulfills your expectations and more.

Our first priority is the reliability and longevity of our product, specifically all of the components that move, slide, and plug in and out. We have already made more than 100 designs and prototypes to select the best working and looking. We will repeat all lifetime tests under different conditions, 10,000 cycles or more with the final tooling before mass production.

Our next, but top, priority is to provide an easy to use modular product which is highly customizable and where the modules work identically, cross-platform for Android, iOS and for any upcoming future operating systems. Our technology is developed, tested and working on Samsung Galaxy S4, S5, S6 and iPhone 5 and 6.

Our team has great experience in developing products from the ground up, leading to mass production and we are confident that we’ll be able to deliver a great product and overcome any other challenges.
