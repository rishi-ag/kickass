About this project

The SonnoBT Sleep Mask is the most comfortable Sleep Mask anywhere. It only weighs 3.7 oz. and feels like a soft pillow. You feel no pressure points, there are no buckles snaps or Velcro at the back or the side of the mask. Our Patent Pending design has electronics and size adjustments in the front of the Mask permitting you to sleep on your sides or back without waking up.

Have you ever wished you could get a good night’s sleep when you sleep with a loved one that snores? Are you a light sleeper? Do you work shifts that require you to sleep during the day? Have you been diagnosed with Insomnia? Well the “Sonno BT” Sleep Mask might just be what you are looking for to solve all these issues and more.

False Claims:

There are devices that track your sleep habits, supposedly give you lucid dreams, hold your head still, and a variety of other claims. But don’t we just want a restful night’s sleep on a consistent basis?

There are those who tell you, you can sleep 2 hours per night and get the rest you need – that’s totally not true all the reseach tells you that your body needs a minimum of 6 to 8 hrs per day.

Your body must have time to repair itself, process memories, solve problems and a multitude of other processes.

 Let's take a look at the SonnoBT Mask

 
Key Features:

  • Bluetooth compliant
  • Free Smartphone app that can be programmed for different events through out the night
  • Simulated Biological Tone Technology (SBTT)
  • Subliminal Learning Events
  • Power Nap Events
  • Light and Sound elimination
  • Listen to your favorite music or white noise to aid you in falling asleep faster and staying asleep
  • Program bed time music or white noise timer, wake up settings, reminders, emergency responders & auto call 911, even have the option to listen in on the baby in the next room
  • Adjustable Velcro adjustment on forehead location for maximum comfort
  • Electronics package is located at forehead so as to be out of the way for sleeping comfort
  • Wide comfortable construction
  • Soft breathable satin fabric
  • Choose a Male or Female voice response
  • Standard mini USB charging using your Smartphone charger
  • Folds into a convenient carrying bag to take with you on trips or to the office for that occasional power nap

Android & iOS interface screen shots:

Sleep Event Sample:

This feature can be programed to set the type of music, time cycle, volume levels for each event, fadeout times, etc. Also can be configured for different events throughout the night such as white noise, or looped messages for subliminal learning while user is asleep. 

Subliminal Sleep Event Sample:

http://www.mindfithypnosis.com/what-are-subliminal-messages

Create a Subliminal Sleep Event and be able to learn a new language or something you need to memorize. These events are customized by you, say you have a big test tomorrow or you have a business presentation with a mountain of facts to remember. Subliminal learning is very useful for some people.

Nap Event Sample:

This function allows the user to set different time intervals for taking a nap. User can set the duration time of the nap. Set duration time of music, also volume with option to fade out, at a set time. User can set time to be awakened, type of event such as standard alarm, music alarm, positive message alarm or today’s schedule alarm, with option to fade in, at a set time. These settings can be used in any combination.

Wake Event Sample:

This feature gives the user multiple ways to start the waking process. Some of these might be standard alarm functions for time and alarm sound. Another might be user sets up 15 minutes before waking time the selected music that will begin to fade in from zero volume and at the end of the 15 minutes max volume. User could also select a message for important events for the day and have it fade in and loops until user shuts off. We are open to other ideas to make the app more functional for the user.

Reminders Sample: 

This feature can be programed to tell you something you want to remember either before you go to bed, during your sleep cycle or as a wake up message. Use it to learn a new language by setting it as white noise, Sound volume, Female or Male voice responder, and other options will be available so you can customize the way you receive your reminder.

Emergency Responders Sample:

The phone app can monitor the room all night long for sound in case of a fire or monoxide alarm going off. The sound elimination of the “Sonno BT” would make it very difficult for you to hear a fire alarm if you are in deep sleep. So this function protects the user. Also will have the option of dialing 911 and repeating a voice message to the 911 operator that there is an emergency at a specific address, number of occupants living there and occupants are unable to respond. This message is customized by use.

Baby Monitoring Sample:

By offering an optional Bluetooth listening device that can be placed in the child’s room Mom or Dad wearing the “Sonno BT”can set an option to listen in for baby. Option could be set for frequency of crying event or length of event or different combinations of listening. Because the SonnoBT is a SMART Monitor, Mom can get a better night's sleep.

  • Hippocampus- Inhibits behaviour and helps form memories
  • Amygdala- Processes memories and emotional reactions
  • Prefrontal Cortex- Linked to personality and critical decision-making
  • Insular Cortex- Regulates heartbeat and is connected to empathy, pain and social awareness
  • Broca's Area- Affects language comprehension and generation

What is Simulated Biological Tone Technology? We have been experimenting with simulated biological tones that are very close to the frequencies as if you put your fingers in your ears. These tones are biologically natural to your brain and seem to work better than white noise generated from fans, motors or other external devices placed in your bedroom. We call this technology “SBT”.

SBT will be exclusively available for the “SonnoBT Sleep Mask” as part of the White Noise options.

We have the utmost confidence that our project will be completed as promised. We have worked hard to setup manufacturing and assembly processes so we can produce a quality product. FAQ

  • What about sleeping on your side?

    Side or back sleeping is no problem with the SonnoBT Sleep Mask. Unlike other sleeping devices the SonnoBT Sleep Mask hugs you with a smooth outside surface that stays put when you roll over on your side. No worry of lumps, buckles, Velcro latches, or electronics causing pressure points on the sides or back of the head. The SonnoBT Sleep Mask secures the position of your ears so when rolling over you don’t need to reposition your pillow to conform to your ear. Simply the most comfortable sleep mask anywhere.

    Last updated: Mon, Apr 27 2015 8:09 am EDT
  • Can I play music from my play list to create a sleep event?

    YES – In fact you can choose any audio source to create a Sleep Event as long as it is being accessed through the SonnoBT Sleep Mask Application. This same answer applies to the “Wake Events”.

    Last updated: Wed, Apr 29 2015 4:25 pm EDT
  • How good is the sound isolation?

    I agree that sound isolation is very important. SonnoBT was designed to give an alternative to ear plugs. Sound isolation with just putting on the SonnoBT, without any sound input from smart phone application is somewhat like cupping your hands over your ears. By adding the sound input using either white noise or music increases the effect of sound isolation considerably (watch video of “Peter in Springville”) located above in details. My intent when I created the device was to give a user multi options to create individual sleep events. The focus was on soothing the brain to relax the user into a sleep cycle faster.

    Last updated: Wed, May 6 2015 9:29 am EDT
