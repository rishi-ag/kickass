By now you've probably noticed these two icons:

The fist icon represents the game of Rock/Paper/Scissors. The preferred weapon of all LEVEL 1 creatures. Later LEVELS will feature different ways to battle, but for LEVEL 1 there is classic Rock/Paper/Scissors.

The heart icon represents your health or Hitpoints. This is how many bouts you can lose of Rock/Paper/Scissor before you lose.

The game is simple. Challenge one other Monster/Hero or NPC (non-playing character, ie. someone without a shirt or button) to a Rock/Paper/Scissors duel. If they accept, itâ€™s now an Encounter.

An Encounter is comprised of a series of Bouts. If you have played R/P/S before, you know that a Bout is both players counting off ONE, TWO, THREE while pounding their open palm with a closed fist and then, after THREE, revealing their chosen attack.Â 

Typically you lose 1 Hitpoint for every Bout you lose. The moment one of the players loses all their Hitpoints the Encounter is over.Â 

BUT, NOTHING IS THAT SIMPLE IN THE DUNGEON....

Classic Rock/Paper/Scissors can be fun. But we decided to throw something else into the mix. We call them Loot Coins. There are 7 different types that can modify the rules of the R/P/S you are playing.Â 

When you begin an Encounter, you decide whether you are going to use one of your Loot Coins' power. You show it to the other player and then place it into your fist. The other player does the same if they choose to use one of theirs.Â 

Some of the powers can only be used once during the Encounter. Others may buff you, or change the format completely.Â 

The Loot Coins are powerful ways to swing an Encounter your way. But beware - the WINNER GETS TO KEEP ALL COINS used in the Encounter, especially the losers.Â
