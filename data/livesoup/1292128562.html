About this project

Ever since we started Nerdache Cakes, a tumblr blog running on cake requests in 2011, we've been asked where our store is located. Now we are ready to provide our fellow geeks a place of their own.

Nerdache Cakes Bakery + Café, where you can get awesome, level-up inducing treats that are decadent, fun, and a nod to your favorite icons in pop culture nerd-ery!

Whether it's perfectly replicating nerdy icons in cake, cookies or cupcakes, all of our customers keep finding reasons to come back for more. And with your help, we can bring nerd cake everyone in an interactive bakery environment that's geeky, fun and easy to enjoy a cup of coffee or blended smoothie in!

Smoothies and Drinks

A sampling of some of the geeky goodies on our menu! This would also include smoothies and our delicious Cake Shakes made from your favorite cake flavors.

Why a Bakery Now?

After 3 years of building Nerdache Cakes- from piping cupcakes in a corner in my kitchen to making 6 tier cakes for geek weddings, I've outgrown my tiny space. This bakery would be built from years of work done just by myself, to a team of people ready to make your geekiest sweets come true!

 When you become a Nerdache Cakes Bakery Backer, you'll receive some geeky loot good enough to sink your teeth into. Get a cute Catbug Squishable, some delicious and unique Catbug Cookies, and lots of Nerdache Gear so you can show everyone your Nerdache side!

If we reach our 90K goal, we'll unlock some Exclusive geeky goodies available only to you, our backers! We'll also have some pretty sweet extras like a food truck, a on-site teaching studio to share some Nerdache Baking tips and tons more.  

Risks and challenges

Every project has it's risks, and turning a at-home business into a full-scale bakery has it's challenges. Staffing, construction permits, and the inevitable copyright issues. But Nerdache Cakes has always offered a ton of support by the nerd community and helped me in ways no other has. I'm confident in my bakery and the people who've always inspired me!

We have diligently put strategic thought into everything that goes into this bakery. The Promise? To our future customers and backers: We will make sure we provide you with the top most quality baked goods, service and we will treat our employees and associates with the utmost respect. In an environment that is comfortable, creative and most importantly fun!

FAQ

  • What's a Nerdache Box/ Digi Box tier?

    The Digi Box tier is 3 months of our awesome monthly subscription box- you'll get a new box full of geeky treats in a new theme every month! You'll also receive our Catbug Exclusive Box not available to any one but our backers, PLUS a Catbug Micro Squishable!

    The themes change every month- you can see some of our past boxes here: http://www.nerdachebox.com/#!past-boxes/c1xy8

    Last updated: Wed, Apr 15 2015 7:45 pm EDT
  • What are the Cookies in the Cookie Combo reward tiers?

    The $10 reward tier are our small package of Cookie Combos- CocoGroots, The Dark Bite and Ma Kent's favorite chocolate chip cookies.

    The $25 reward tier has a large package of Cookie Combos- everything listed before PLUS our Pinkie Pie frosted sugar cookies.

    Coco Groots- a crispy tea cookie with toasted coconut baked right in.
    The Dark Bite- a delicious chocolate chocolate-chip cookie with dark chocolate chips.
    Ma' Kents Fave- Oversized Homestyle chocolate chip cookies- gooey and light with a crisp outside.

    Last updated: Wed, Apr 15 2015 11:47 pm EDT
  • Are the Rewards add on? As in, If I pledge $45, do I get all the tiers below it?

    Some tiers include the tiers below it (I.e the Catbug Super Adventure Tier, or Tee Tris teir including the cookie combos tier.) while others are just those stated in the tier description.

    If you pledge $45, I believe you get the Catbug Adventure tier- which is the simpliest package Nerdache Box. For just $25 more, you get the Super Catbug Adventure tier, which includes cake pops, more cookies, AND a Catbug micro Squishable! :)

    Last updated: Sat, Apr 18 2015 12:26 pm EDT
  • Does the Catbug Adventure Tier ($45) come with just the 3 cookies?

    Hi! Oh no, the Catbug Adventure Tier comes with 3 Catbug cookies, 2 oven mitt cookies, a baking Catbug Cookie, a Rebecca Cookie, and a Sir Jeffer cookie. It's more of a Cookie Playset because it comes with every cookie you need to recreate your favorite Drama Bug scene! :)

    The next tier comes with a large version of this box, including Cake pops and a Catbug mini Squishable!

    Last updated: Wed, May 13 2015 12:36 pm EDT
