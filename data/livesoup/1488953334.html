GogglePal is the world's first universally mountable augmented reality heads up display for ANY goggle. From the cheap pair you inherited from your older brother to the latest, quick-changing lens models, your goggles are naturally GogglePal compatible. Over the last two years, our engineers have been hard at work testing and fine tuning the technology to be smaller, lighter and more affordable than the competition.Â 

GogglePal is small. Very small. The projection device sits inside your goggle without obstructing your vision, held in place by a subtle, super-strong magnet. The lightweight controller pack secures to your goggle strap with a simple clip. The most recent prototype of the controller pack and projection device weigh just 58 grams.Â 

GogglePal is able to stay so light in part because it pairs seamlessly with your iPhone. Your GogglePal and iPhone work together (through the companion app) to store data and connect with your friends.Â 

The iOS version of the app is free to download and available now on the App Store. You can even use the app without the GogglePal AR-HUD.

Risks and challenges

The risks and challenges with this project are like a lot of other hardware projects. Challenges include the timely manufacturing and sourcing of the materials needed to produce the hardware and software in the quantities we need.

To mitigate these risks, we've brought on experienced and knowledgable team members who have experience taking a project and seeing it from prototype to final product. Their experience will help mitigate these risks. We have also partnered and nurtured close relationships with factories with proven track records. We trust them completely to make our GogglePal dream a reality, and trust them, too, to manufacture a high quality, high performance device.

Of course, as with any startup and product campaign, unforeseen challenges do come up. You can be assure that we will keep our backers up to date and well informed throughout the process.
