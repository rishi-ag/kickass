About this project

Our family bought this company from another family who could not handle the stress and time that went into a food truck. We wanted to keep this local company and brand local so we bought the company. We have expanded the menu, customer demand, and quality of food. However we are still a very young business who needs a kickstart to accomplish our goals. With this fundraising event we will be able to keep the company local and offer the same type of business that we have been able to this past year. We also want to begin expanding with a food truck trailer, which is why we need the money for this fundraiser. 

Risks and challenges

Some of the risks with expanding into a trailer will be additional food costs, more employees, and hopefully more business.

The best part about this project though is all extra insurances and liabilities are under the new umbrella insurance policy we took out. Also the trailer and my personal truck that will tow it will be under the automobile insurance. So many of the fixed costs will not change.
Also my personal truck is more reliable than our current food truck, and better on gas. So we are able to cut costs there.

With regards to other risks will be employees, currently we are in the stage of hiring more people and having someone around to do day to day office work to help find more jobs for the trailer.

With this trailer too the food cost will stay the same or even go down, since we will use to trailer for smaller events that can use less food and product.

I have gone to business school at a local private college, broken down the costs for the trailer and the amount of time it will take to make a profit. I feel that with my knowledge, great staff and a welcoming city this expansion will be a huge success and help my family more.
