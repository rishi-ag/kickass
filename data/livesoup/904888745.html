MY STORY

Started in 2007 by Fantasy Illustrator, Heather V. Kreiter,  My Little Demon is the "dark side" version of ponies and is often considered the Garbage Pail Kids of a new generation!  


MLD started with just 6 drawings and in just over 7 years has expanded to include Polystone Resin figurines, coloring books, calendars, personal checks, and most recently, Plushies!

I'm hoping that with YOUR support I can bring even more characters from the My Little Demon universe to life! With this campaign, my goal is to bring forth Cupcake Chaos, Electro Cutie, and Frostbite!

About the plush: 
These plush measure approx. 12" from the tips of their noses to the end of their tails and are made of high quality super soft Minky fabric.  Their Booty Marks and facial features are all embroidered so they are safe for children (and adults) of all ages!  Their mane, tail, and feet feathers are of a long pile faux fur fabric.

They are loosely stuffed and have floppy legs for extra cuddliness!

Risks and challenges

Of course, the main challenge in running a campaign is trying to raise the funds! In 2013 I ran a successful campaign to fund the first 4 plushies in the line. That campaign was my largest to date and I learned quite a lot of dos and don'ts from the experience.

My fan base for the Demons has grown exponentially since then. I have been promoting this Kickstarter since November on all of my Facebook pages, twitter, Instagram, my personal website and my mailing list in the hopes of getting everyone excited and ready to pledge!

I am working with a different manufacturer this time around and trust in their communication with myself and their factory. It should take about 1.5-2 months to produce the plushies and another 2-3 months for them to arrive at my doorstep. This is a rough estimate. Delays can always happen as the factory may have issues finding all of the fabric necessary to complete the plushies and shipments may get stuck in customs. I've tried to allot for any delays by extending my "estimated delivery" by a few months longer than expected.

I have had an account with the USPS for several years now. The majority of the packages will be sent via USPS Priority, so all will come with a tracking #. When I mail your package, I will also send you an email with your delivery confirmation # so you should have an idea of when to expect your rewards.

The main delay will mostly be for anyone who orders a custom Demon from me. I will do my best to prep any and all pony bases if we meet our goal early to expedite the sculpting process. Please know that each custom is created by yours truly. I want to take my time with each one so that the custom you receive will be finely crafted and something you will be proud of!

If you've been one of my backers before you'll know that I always believe in letting you know EVERYTHING that's going on with the campaign, good or bad! This time is no different, so feel free to pledge with confidence!
