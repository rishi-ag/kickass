About this project

PMJ shoes are stylish leather shoes, hand-made in Italy and designed in Paris. Their great style and quality are what makes PMJ shoes so unique. PMJ Footwear reinvents modern sneakers and high heels by adding colors and creating new styles. Our goal was to create $500+ luxury shoes for less than $200. Because style is confidence, we want you to feel confident in your shoes.

We built the best possible team around this project, from the french designers with their savoir-faire, to the italian artisan shoemakers. And now we are proud to present you this exciting project and hope you will be a part of it.

So what makes PMJ Footwear a unique shoe company :

  • Affordable stylish shoes 
  •  Made in Italy 
  • French team of designers 
  • Price under $200 
  • No middleman

 We focused on making the best possible design for our shoes, but also the best possible quality on the market. We spent months working on our designs, looked at today's trends and finally came up with what we think are the most elegant, stylish and comfortable shoes. To you, we present our first collection.

Our Mens collection is made of four different models, it includes twenty different colors. They are all made out of the best italian materials. We use the finest calf leather, patent leather, fur and even neoprene for our shoes. We wanted to create a colorful and elegant collection so you can combine different colors and materials with your outfits.

Comfort is a big part of how we make our shoes. Because most luxury shoes are actually not comfortable, usually due to the stiffness of their leather. At PMJ Footwear we promised ourselves to make the most comfortable fashion shoes, while still using premium materials. 

The PMJ Signature Edition is the best of the best. The full gold patent leather is the pure expression of exclusivity, beauty and elegance, for the people who feel special. Its design is unique with the two velcros on top and the gold zipper on the inside. It also has gold laces and a gold sole to complete the style. This edition is limited to only 50 pairs so hurry and get your pair to be part of the club.

SIGNATURE EDITION LIST

The lucky owners will be part of the signature limited edition list and will receive special offers on future collections, and more exclusive deals. You will be part of our VIP list with all its advantages, plus you will be invited to PMJ events with our team and will meet our founder Pierre Marc Jannot.

Our Womens collection has five different models, including high heels and sneakers. We also wanted the collection to be modern and elegant, that is why we chose very simple styles, and classic colors.

 While making high heels, we know that comfort is the number one criteria for a great shoe. Because stylish heels that are not comfortable stay inside the closet. We want our customers to be satisfied with their PMJ shoes. That is why we dedicated ourselves to make truly comfortable heels.

 These shoes above will be unlocked once we reach $150,000 along with five other colors. Only with your support we will be able to add more exciting designs and colors. So let's do it !

For more exclusive content, please visit our Instagram page, and follow us at https://instagram.com/pmjfootwear/

We made a T-shirt collection, for men and women. We wanted the t-shirts to be simple, colorful and classic. We made them colorful so they can be matched with the shoes, for a fun look.

 The key concepts in our designs are timeless elegance, simplicity and comfort. We wanted our shoes to look elegant no matter what the next trend is. Years from now, PMJ shoes will still be stylish, and they will be so because of their simplicity. Because when things are simple, they go through time without aging. And finally comfort, that's one of our top priority to come up with shoes that are wearable, because nobody wants to wear shoes that are painful, we worked hard to make PMJ shoes a reference in comfort.

 Our prototype is a non-final version of what is going to be the actual shoes. Once the project is funded we will be able to conceive a closer to the design shoe for a perfect result. We have made that prototype in Italy while looking for a factory. It is made of genuine calf leather with a embossed snake print, patent leather and rubber sole. With your help we will be able to manufacture the final version, along with the rest of the collection. The factory is ready for production and is waiting for further notice to begin.

 By getting the shoes directly from our factory to your feet, we are able to offer you the best possible price on the market for those amazing quality shoes. While other brands have business models that include retail markups and additional fees, we are able to come up with an unbeatable wholesale price.

 From Paris, Milan to Los Angeles, we built a team of strong individuals to make this project very successful. Our head designer and founder Pierre Marc Jannot is a designer from Paris, after high school he decided to move to Los Angeles to pursue his studies in fashion and business at SMC then UCLA. After graduating he worked a year for Giorgio Armani. But what he wanted was making his own shoes, so he launched PMJ Footwear.

He is being helped by Marc (our Business Executive), Michèle (our Marketing Executive), Cédric (3D artist and website editor), Aurore (Photographer, Videographer) and Christophe (fashion designer and graphic designer).

Our shoe manufacturer is a team of artisan shoemakers in the suburb of Milan that have years of experience in the making of handmade luxury shoes. They have worked with premium brands and throughout the years have gained the trust of some of the best shoe companies in the world.

 Our team is the representation of European excellency, from french designers to italian shoemakers, we are here to bring true premium shoes to your feet.

PMJ shoes are rightfully sized, we recommend you to follow the size you usually wear with other brands. Please follow this chart above to find your size. If the size doesn't fit you right, we do offer to exchange your pair for another size, within the first 10 days you received your pair. 

 We came up with a list of rewards for you that show our huge appreciation for your support. Offering you a great value going up to 50% off retail price on our shoes, only here on Kickstarter and for a limited time. We also want to show you our gratitude by attaching a thank you card with a personal note on each of your pledge. And finally, with this early support, we grant you a spot onto our PMJ VIP list, that will give you future discounts on our products, access to limited items and many more advantages.

 We have made a list of goals to show you that we are an ambitious brand, and so that together we can make this adventure go beyond all expectations. With your support, we can make PMJ Footwear a brand that grows fast and offers a larger variety of products.

Above is a timeline we predict. The production will start a few days after the campaign is over and successfully funded. The first rewards will be sent to our backers within the month of May, while our first shoes will be sent throughout June. We have planned the time ahead and are looking forward to our successful future as a fashion brand with your support.

Having said that, we cannot always control everything in life, delay in manufacturing sometimes happens, even though the probability of it happening is low. We are doing our best to prevent any of that to happen and will always be in touch with you, giving you clear details of the process and frequent updates of our work.
