About this project

Our Campaign

Holographic Optical Technologies is bringing professional-grade holography to the consumer market through Kickstarter. Our holograms are true, three-dimensional sculptures of light that float in space in front of our Voxbox viewers.

This Kickstarter campaign is our way of bringing our Voxgram hologram viewers and custom hologram production service to you.

Our Voxbox Pro hologram system has been proven in the medical field. Doctors and patients are not the only ones who can benefit from this technology. We believe that businesses of all kinds can use this technology to wow clients and offer an enhanced level of service. Realtors could show their clients holograms of distant properties, and designers can bring detailed representations of their ideas to life as sculptures of light.

This technology was originally invented in order to visualize vast expanses of interstellar space, but it has since become the basis of a state-of-the-art medical device. Earlier versions of our Voxbox Pro and our Voxgrams have been deployed by physicians and medical educators for years to assist in understanding complex anatomical structures within the human body, and as an aid in surgical planning.

Holography has wider applications, however, and we have decided to use Kickstarter to make holography accessible to consumers and professionals alike. Our Voxbox, Voxbox Pro, and Voxgrams are now available exclusively through this Kickstarter campaign.

Your contribution goes further than these products, however. With a successful campaign, we hope to bring our hologram production technology to hospitals and professional practices around the world.

Our System

Our holography viewing system may sound complex, but it consists of only two components: our Voxgrams, the films on which we print our holograms, and our Voxboxes that we use to display the Voxgrams. Simply place the Voxgram into the Voxbox, and a hologram will appear floating in front of the screen.

The Voxbox is the smaller, consumer version of our revolutionary hologram viewer. It has an eight-inch viewable area, and is compact enough for desktop use. It was painstakingly developed to meet a low price point without sacrificing the exacting precision of our professional units.

The Voxbox Pro is our flagship, medical-grade hologram viewer. Its 22-inch viewable area is ideal for medical data, but it can also be used to visualize complex architectural and engineering renderings. Our holograms are also engaging enough for beautiful, full-color artistic and commercial projects.

Developer Kits - Makers Wanted!

Are you a tech-savvy Maker? Do you have access to a 3D printer? We are particularly excited to be able to offer our Developer Kits to our backers that would like to integrate our Voxbox technology into their own projects. The Developer Kit and Developer Kit Plus include all of the electronic components necessary to build one Voxbox, and the 3D printer-compatible files needed to produce the viewer's housing. This option is ideal for Maker spaces and tech enthusiasts who want to get up-close and personal with a state-of-the-art hologram viewer.

Risks and challenges

While this technology has long been proven in the research community and the medical field, challenges may arise when printing large numbers of custom holograms.

We currently have the technology to print full-color holograms, however our current process may need to be refined to produce backer-created holograms requiring high color fidelity. These challenges will be overcome by the expertise of our inventors, chief holographers, the and the rest of the Holographic Optical Technologies team.

This is the fifth-generation of our holographic printer, and we are more than ready to tackle any technical challenge.
