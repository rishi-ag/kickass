About this project

Like the video?  Click the "Share this Project" button above... and then when you get a chance, “Like” us on Facebook.  www.facebook.com/TheOfficialWonkeyDonkey 

Before I ask you to help our Kickstarter Campaign, I want to share the story of how the meeting with our beer drinking donkey has evolved from a cool logo for my annual golf trip into ultimately what the WONKEY DONKEY has come to represent for a devoted group of WONKEY DONKEY disciples, the symbol and a reminder of a lifestyle we love – the Lifestyle of Sophomoric Sophistication.

 Sophomoric Sophistication you ask?

If this picture* puts a smile on your face, you’re on the right Kickstarter page.

Simply put, the Lifestyle of Sophomoric Sophistication celebrates the enduring fraternity that is guy bonding. You know the scene – 3 or 4 of your best buddies, lots of laughs, busting stones, quoting memorable movie lines, good music playing, your favorite drink in hand and that overwhelming feeling of how lucky you are to have these jackasses as friends.

 The WONKEY DONKEY Code of Conduct

Man up!  Don’t complain; get your priorities right and take care of your responsibilities.

Guys who truly live the WONKEY DONKEY Lifestyle understand that you take care of your responsibilities first – no exceptions; whether that’s staying back at the dorm to study for your Business Statistics test, or giving up your Thursday night softball league for a few years because you have two small kids at home, or turning down an invite to go on a fishing trip because it would conflict with plans your spouse made for this weekend.  For those occasions, throw on a WONKEY DONKEY hat or golf shirt and be there in spirit – your herd will understand.

The WONKEY DONKEY Brand

Over the past 7 years I have developed some of the coolest merchandise and apparel on the planet that we’ve given out on our annual golf trips, around the club, and at parties to friends and family.

With your help, this successful WONKEY DONKEY Kickstarter Campaign will officially launch our popular micro-brand into production.  Our immediate plan is to open the official WONKEY DONKEY KICK ASS ecommerce website (www.wonkeydonkey.com) that will provide instant access to all the great WONKEY DONKEY gear and accessories. 

In addition to being the first guy in your herd to own some great WONKEY DONKEY merchandise (at a Kickstarter Only deep Discount), by backing this brand, you have the opportunity to become a member of the WONKEY DONKEY Founder’s Club – for your backing of $100 or more, you will receive a Limited Edition Founder’s Club "Donkey-Tag" Opener and future exclusive Insider offers and benefits reserved for our Founder’s Club members.

 Experience & Expertise

With your Kickstarter backing, we are ready to build the WONKEY DONKEY brand together.

We have manufactured apparel and merchandise for over 20 years here in the U.S. and around the globe for some of America’s largest companies and greatest brands - we know how and where to get things made – the way they should be made…with quality.  You will see that our commitment to quality shows in everything we produce.

We have great merchandise like custom WONKEY DONKEY shirts, hats, coolers, golf gear, and weekend bags all ready for you.  High quality gear that is very stylish, without trying too hard to be trendy.  Think of it as Ralph Lauren meets Margaritaville.

We also have become experts in creating Branding and Marketing strategies in the sports, alcohol and entertainment industries – three industries near and dear to the WONKEY DONKEY lifestyle - so we understand how to appeal to our target audience.  Most importantly….We have a history of making amazing items starring the WONKEY DONKEY.

In appreciation for your brotherly love and some of your cash, the WONKEY DONKEY has assembled all of his favorite items in our REWARDS section to say “Thank You” and welcome you into the H.E.R.D. 

Join the H.E.R.D. and enjoy your new, or simply improved, Lifestyle of Sophomoric Sophistication.

If you often experience a Lifestyle of Sophomoric Sophistication  – CONGRATULATIONS – you are having a great day – immediately get back to your herd and don’t do anything to kill the mojo.  Enjoy the Lifestyle. 

*The actors and athletes pictured are not an implied endorsement.  But for the record, the Wonkey Donkey gives them a full four "hooves up" endorsement!

Risks and challenges

If you've read this far - Thank You. We have a good feeling about you too.

We have been diligent in reviewing all aspects of the project. Based on our decades of experience developing, sourcing, and marketing merchandise for leading brands and companies, there are always unknowns, but the most likely challenge will be in area supply chain management. To minimize our risks, we have worked with our various factories and suppliers to establish our primary and secondary list of vendors. We can tell you this… they are all excited about the WONKEY DONKEY brand and are ready to go. We are confident about our delivery dates for our backer rewards, but we will not make any order commitments with our suppliers until our $40,000 funding level is met.

Also, we have a great working relationship with our web design and development partner who is on standby, awaiting the green light to begin the build out of the WONKEY DONKEY e-commerce site. We have already seen concept drawings and we are very excited.

Other challenges could include a backer selecting the wrong size, moving to a new address prior to shipping their reward or providing Kickstarter with inaccurate information. If this type of situation occurs, we will work with you, within reason, to remedy these types of issues in a timely manner. Our goal is to exceed your expectations, not just meet them.

Lastly, if everyone on the planet is suddenly diagnosed with Gaidarosaphobia (the fear of donkeys), that will present a unique and unanticipated challenge that may take us some time to sort out – but we will sort it out.

If you would like to e-mail or speak with me directly about a concern, or just about the project, I will be more than happy to discuss it with you.

Finally we need you… we need your backing and help to spread the word, and launch the WONKEY DONKEY brand.
