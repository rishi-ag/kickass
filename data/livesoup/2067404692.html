About this project

Аbout Voice of Steel:

  • A fighting simulator game, based on physics
  • The ability to create your own strikes  
  • The ability to create a unique fighter  
  • A hybrid control system, including a voice command system  
  • Cross-platform multiplayer  
  • No Subscriptions  
  • No Pay to Win
  • No microtransactions. Buy once, play forever

   This is an online physics-based robot fight simulator, where you create movements and strikes for your robot. The robot consists of 16 different modules. Your role is to be the operator, the coach, the manager, and the mechanic of all your robot-fighters.

  •  Movement freedom! Each player can create any movement or strike and use it in a battle. That gives everyone the opportunity to create his own fighter with a unique fighting style. After all, "the frog strike" or "double strike to the head" at a special angle can give a great advantage when your opponent does not expect this.
  • Create your own fighter! Each robot consists of 16 items, which each carry different characteristics. Now more than 100,000 combinations of robot assembly are available, without taking into account colors. This allows the player to create both internally and externally, a completely unique fighter!
  • Maximum control! The game has a traditional keyboard and gamepad control, and we developed a unique voice control system that will allow you to manage your fighter by voice, because remembering and saying the name of the hit that you came up with is easier than finding the needed buttons among dozens of options.
  • Real physical system! We have developed a unique physical system that allows you to animate the robot, and create movements and strikes for them. It also determines the outcome of the battle as well. The skeleton of the robot has more than 2000 connections; as it simulates rigid structures, joints, tendons and muscles. No ragdolls! 
  • The collision system runs in full accordance with physics. During the rendering of a strike, a reality-based model is used. It takes into account the mass and the velocity of each collision from head to toe. A properly delivered blow is the key to victory! Adaptive algorithms are used to simulate both the robots and the environment without any appreciable loss of performance. The physics frequency is 60 ticks per second without FPS failures. All this is possible at the stage of tech-demo! Do not worry about the fact that the game might not work with a slow computer - our approach is to simulate the battle on the server side, which also largely eliminates cheating.
  • Strike variability! You can create your own strikes, and what is more you can vary them in a fight. Any strike can be modified by crouch stands and lunges that can change the momentum of a fight radically. As you understand, the lunge, made in correct time, adds speed to your stroke. Therefore, it can save you or take down your rival.

 History

   2067. People live in a world where robots are widespread in all spheres of life from housekeeping to labor at the factory. People don’t even fight themselves, they use robots. Cyber sport has replaced other sports and young fighters come to the ring to prove themselves. GlobalTech Corporation, the leader in modular robot production, announces an annual robot fight tournament. The prize fund is $100 million. Everyone wants to win the jackpot and the participants are even ready to cross the line. But time will show who is the best robot-fighter…

 About the league structure

   Official and non-official arena description There are two types of leagues in the game – official and non-official. You can choose either one you like. In the official leagues all the fights are by the rules, where a gamer gets money and glory for a fight he has won. He can become a champion step by step.

  The Non-official league is different. There are no rules and money is not important – only the construction of your robot is important. Power rules there – if you lose the fight, you lose everything. Every league consists of 16 subclasses, which depend on your game skills. In the game you will also find a system of clubs and fights between clubs, where gamers can train with each other and create official fights between clubs.

   The game developer team is a part of Point Cloud Studio, where every member is a highly qualified and enthusiastic expert in his field.

   Point Cloud Studio started its work in 2007, and they have worked on various projects that allowed them to get the necessary experience to be ready for this one. They noticed that the fighting game industry was in a crisis, as the games have become boring and there seemed to be no development trends. Being true fans of fighting games, the team has come up with their own project - Voice of Steel - which promises to become a real breakthrough in the online games industry.
