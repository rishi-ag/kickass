About this project

 Sneak preview of your future book.

Our Story 

We’re excited to introduce Storyfy.me - the world’s first publishing service which enables ordinary people to publish their life-stories in an affordable way. Our team believes that we all have a story to tell, whether it’s about ourselves, a friend or our family. 

Fredrik - our founder - wanted to make a book about his grandfather to keep his memory alive but couldn't find an affordable quality service anywhere. Worried by the idea that all his grandfather’s great life achievements might be forgotten, he came up with the idea to create Storyfy.me to help other people to record and pass on stories of their loved ones. Biographies of great men and women of our history are very popular because people are interested in reading life stories. 

The digital dilemma and pictures taken during our life journey

According to a survey the average camera owner has 1,200 pictures saved and 38% failed to copy them for future safe keeping. This is despite 75% of those surveyed are saying that losing family photographs by technological mishap is their worst digital nightmare. 

Even if a pictures is worth a thousand words its value might fade away for future generations without the life story of the people behind them. 

Storyfy.me is first to combine written life stories with pictures in an affordable way

After months of relentless work on the project we - a group of professional writers, designers and artists, are happy to bring out this new concept. We come from Scandinavia and so far we have been able to produce the first books in Swedish and Finnish. With your help we will be able to publish books also in English and Spanish so that more people can share their stories.

Storyfy.me is a modern way of storytelling and celebrating the best moments in life. We want to change the way people are remembered and to make their memories live forever. 

Our products

To make a book affordable for everyone, we offer book sizes with different price levels. For Kickstarter backers only we also offer a Mini life story book so everyone can share life stories.

  • Mini Life Story Book (£259) with 4-10 pages of text and up to 35 pages including photos. 
  • Basic Life Story Book (£359) with 10-15 pages of text and up to 60 pages including photos. 
  • Premium Life Story Book (£499) with 15-30 pages of text and up to 80 pages including photos. 
  • Deluxe Life Story Book (£719) with 30-50 pages of text and up to 100 pages including photos. 
  • Additionally, you can make your own request and we will offer a customized solution. 

    Below you find more information about our timeless and elegant book alternatives: 

1. Biographies: covering your story from your childhood to the present to be acquired for yourself or e.g. as a birthday present for your beloved.

2. Wedding and Anniversary Book: telling the life journey of two persons and story from how they met up to that big moment. 

3. Memoir: cherishing the memory and life of a loved one who is no longer with us.

4. Tailored Theme Books: enabling you to tell the story of your dog, kids, a special journey, hobbies or recording the events from previous years.

Our working process 

In the meantime, all of our backers can prepare requests and gather stories and pictures. We will contact you as soon as the project ends successfully. Due to the Kickstarter's policy we cannot reach you before the project end. However, you can always send us your questions or order our products via our website or our email. 

Our plan 

Once we reach our target goal of £30,000 we plan to unveil our business to the UK and US markets. To achieve this we will strengthen our team by adding more professional designers and English native writers. These people will enable us to tell our customers' stories and fulfill our company's vision.

Another key area where we particularly value your contribution is in the development of our advertising and marketing, particularly in creating targeted and effective online campaigns. We hope that by making the ordering process as simple as possible for our customers we will be able to drive traffic and create the orders that will allow us to to grow and develop, which will help us to make many people’s dreams of having their own life stories become alive. 

Our team 

We are a close knit team of young, hardworking and dedicated professionals who are passionate about writing life stories. Our team has a multicultural background with members from Great Britain, Finland, Spain and Vietnam, who have gathered together to make this fairy tale come true.

Fredrik Sundell is the founder of Storyfy.me and it is his vision that has enabled us to move forward with this project and help to bring it to life. He has a MBA from the Swedish School of Economics and Business Administration.

Fredrik has worked as an entrepreneur for 15 years with experience in many business fields including international trade, management consulting and HR. He founded HR consulting company Talentor and expanded it into 17 European countries. The company was acquired in 2013. He is now passionate to build Storyfy.me into a global phenomenon.

Our Photographic team is led by Toby Veitch. Toby has over 20 year of experience in Quality Photography and over 10 years experience in Photographic Legacy products. As part of the Storyfy.me team he is now passionate about taking his expereince in photographic legacy products into a new area by combining photograps with written life stories.

Our group of writers are led by writer Niina Bell, who has a BA Honours in Journalism from a UK university. Niina is a bestselling children's author, presenter and musician in Finland. She also has a strong background in journalism, having previously worked for the Finnish News Agency as a news reader, subeditor and profile writer. Niina loves discovering and writing both factual and fictional stories. 

Our Design team is led by Graphic Designer Mark Soutar, whose experience spans over 15 years where he has studied and worked both in the UK and America, as well as here in Finland. The skillful and creative team behind Mark helps us to complete the creative process by presenting your story and images together in clean and simple design that reflects the true character of your tales.  

Finally, we want to thank you for spending your valuable time discovering and supporting our project. We believe that with your support we can change the way people are remembered and make their memories live forever.

Risks and challenges

The risk of this project is low since each product is separately produced based on you backing. Our team includes professional writers and designers with previous work experience in the publishing field. The books are printed in collaboration with a specially selected high quality printing company. However, something unforeseen problems could emerge and cause a delay especially due to the specialty of the service.

However, we will do everything in our power to make sure that no delays occur and that you will get your rewards as agreed.
