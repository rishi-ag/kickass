ABOUT THIS PROGRAM

Our simple goal here is to give something back to the art community and to have us, as a group of people whose lives have been enriched by the guitar, come together to help us spread the same inspiration we found somewhere to others. Along with the release of our 2015 line, our goal here is to target numbers that allow us to donate instruments to music educational programs and then to local music stores who, as a group, struggle daily to stay alive in the days of bargain discount instruments and “big box” retail, let alone compete.

Really, all we want to do is give away some guitars to people who may pick one up, or pick it up again. But we’re not a huge guitar company (and there are many of them). We can’t sponsor rock shows, have TV ads, buy stereo companies, develop clothing lines, etc. So we’re asking for your help so we can afford to share the inspiration nationwide.

ABOUT OUR PHILOSOPHY

The guitar is a tool, but it is also a tool of inspiration. “When you design in an uninspired manner, your designs can never inspire,” says Diamant. “But when you create something inspirational, like in a guitar, you create the song that comes from it, the personal experience the player has with it, or even the inspiration to learn in the first place. That’s what we’re all about.”

ABOUT DIAMOND

Diamond is one of the fastest growing, most highly respected guitar brands in today's market. Our instruments find their way into all genres of music and are consistently heralded for their quality and styling worldwide. Offering a full range of electric guitars, and headquartered in Houston, Texas, Diamond was founded by Jeff Diamant, who also founded Diamond Amplification, which is considered among the top guitar amplifier brands in the world. 

Diamond's mission was to create a line of guitars accessible to all musicians, yet true to the standards of high-end, boutique guitar products. Known as fundamentally philosophically opposed to cutting corners or sacrificing in design or construction, Diamant brought high-end design and construction systems and standards to this guitar line as well. Combining the elegance of tradition with modern styling and edge, Diamond offers guitar players premium guitar design that satisfies the need for sound, feel, reliability and attitude they want in their instruments.

  • What needs to be done to the "build-your-own" guitar kits to finish the guitar?

    The body is prepped and light sanded. All holes and routing are "pre-drilled/pre-routed" so you don't need to do any of that. You'll do light sanding as necessary, paint (if desired), and assemble. This is a bolt-on neck so assembly is easy. You will have to install the fret-wire, but the neck is pre-slotted for frets. No inlays in the neck so you can create your own!

    Last updated: Thu, Apr 23 2015 9:54 am EDT
