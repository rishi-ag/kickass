About this project

Hey!

You can call me Gl1tch, welcome to my KS :) I'm basically an introverted guy with a busy mind who loves to try to bring his imagination to life. Whether it's a sculpture of a moose eating cookies, a drawing of a sabertooth tiger skull, or glass beads I made on the torch, I am always working on something. Paint in my beard happens on a regular basis.

I came up with the name "Gl1tch" because I often have so many thoughts and ideas that I will feel like a computer suddenly short-circuiting that's in need of a shutdown and restart. However, thanks to many sticky notes and my handy dandy sketchbook wallet, I'm proud to say that I've been short-circuiting less and creating art more.

Which means...

I want to share my art with all of you!

So what do you say? 

 Give one of these little guys a fur-ever home?

Risks and challenges

If I could continue to create art every day of my life, I would be the happiest man.

That is my goal with this project. I would love to be able to gain enough support that I could sculpt and paint and draw full time. I am also a big fan of writing letters and sending mail. I like the convenience of social networking, but there's just something about a handwritten note that really does it. So it would honestly really make my day to be able to bring a bag of letters to the post office!

As for risks? The biggest risk is on me. If my work turns out not to be a success, I risk losing the money and time I invested in my pieces. Every artist has had at least one work of art that they felt so sure of..only to find it to be a big flop. But as artists, we keep trying because it's something we can't help but do. So if you like my work, I would love to be given a chance.

As for the risk you take in donating, your package could get lost in the mail, but no worries! Let me know and I'll get another item out to you asap. Not a problem. Donations that exceed $20 will get tracking.
