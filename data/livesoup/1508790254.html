Our Story

Meet the Patels was a small, unknown film that was rejected from film festivals for two years. A year ago, it finally had its world premiere at Hot Docs Film Festival and its U.S. premiere at Los Angeles Film Festival. It won audience awards, the crowd went wild, and what followed was a chain of sold-out screenings across the country, making Meet the Patels the sleeper hit of the 2014-15 festival circuit.
