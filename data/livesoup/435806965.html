About this project

Synopsis 

While dealing with the loss of her mother, a young woman is torn between two worlds, one of good and one of evil. She turns to drugs in her weak state and during an overdose, she is faced with her final judgement. For the better of mankind, she will have to make a choice. Her choice, a predetermined path to darkness. 

Will she, Feed the Black?

Concept

At its core, Feed the Black is a non conventional love story and tale of morality told in a grim horror film. We have created a dream like fantasy world of macabre creatures and morbid visions as the setting for this movie. 

As this movie will be silent short film, we plan to fill every frame with memorable visions of ecstasy and ghastly soundscapes.

We are all faced with the choice of right and wrong, it's up to us to make the conscious decision. What one person may find stunning is another persons nightmare. What some may find sanctuary in, some would consider hell.

Budget

The funds will be spent on custom made costumes, make-up, EFX materials, various props and most importantly, feeding the cast and crew. 

Here is a budget break down:

  • Materials for costumes - £150
  • Alginate, plaster bandages, other life casting materials - £100
  • Various make ups - £50
  • Mechanical components for animatronics - £120
  • Foam Latex System - £180
  • Catering - £66 

  100% of your investment will go to what you see on screen.

Director

Hi, my name is Klayton Dean and I am an award winning film maker based in Portsmouth, United Kingdom. My journey as a film maker started at an early age after watching Tobe Hoopers Classic film, "The Texas Chainsaw Massacre." Immediately, I was hooked! I quickly developed an appreciation for cinema not only in the Horror genre but other genres as well. From crime thrillers to intense dramas to Exploitation, I dig it all. 

I have directed three short films and many music videos. My short film, "One 2 Another" won best film at the Pembrokeshire Film Festival. I also won Best Director in the same year for a different film at the same competition. Recently, one of my short films, "63P012" was featured on JABB Pictures series "The Collective."  Trailer below. 

https://vimeo.com/99417491 - "63P012" 

I have also directed music videos for the Cypher Killers, Black Pope, Elemental, GirlFlesh and many more.

https://vimeo.com/109312233 - Black Pope "Ain't no Grave" Music Video 

https://vimeo.com/125627411 - Girl Flesh "Gore Habit" Music Video

Risks and challenges

Terror Visions is a fresh face when it comes to the Horror Film Industry. Although new as a company, our crew have many years of experience in film & media related fields. We are all like minded individuals that strive for high quality and a drive compared to no other.

We realise there are risks and challenges with any venture so we have rigorously planned and scheduled all aspects of this project. From the shot list to storyboards, gear that we will need as well as back up cast & crew just in case. We have meticulously organised as best as we can for a clockwork project.
