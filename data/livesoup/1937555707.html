MARS ATTACKS IS BACK-ACK-ACK!

The cult favorite sci-fi trading card series returns with an all-new story!

In Mars Attacks: Occupation, we’ll see what happens when a race of hideous alien invaders conquers our world. 

New artwork by some of today's hottest comics and sci-fi illustrators-- including Alex Horley, Dan Brereton, Greg Staples, Ed Repka and legendary veteran Bob Larkin-- will showcase everything you love about Mars Attacks.  From amazing pulpy art to horrific scenes of alien mayhem, terrifying atrocities, over-the-top madness, and of course a healthy dose of blood and babes!

Take a look at the infographics below and choose your pledge level on the right.  You can buy Mars Attacks: Occupation any way you want it:  a few packs, a complete set, a box of cards, big master set bundle, and unique rewards like the chance to be painted into a card or to create one of the cards with our editors and artists!!   You can also upgrade your pledge with additional boxes, binders or packs of special cards.

So dive in, pick your pledge, and be sure to join me in the comments section to talk about ways we can make it even better.

-Adam from Topps

One box of Mars Attacks: Occupation will contain 24 packs.

Each pack will contain:

  • 4 base cards
  • 1 “insert” card.

Insert Cards include:

  • Chase Cards (10 Per Box)
  • Foil Parallel Variants (4 Per Box) - all 72 cards
  • Heritage Parallel Variants (6 Per Box) - 45 story cards
  • Concept Parallel Variants (2 Per Box) - 45 story cards
  • Super Rare "Hit" Cards (2 Per Box)

Super Rare Cards will include:

  • Sketch Cards (1 guaranteed per box)
  • Metal Cards (55 Designs to collect, reprinting the original 1962 series on solid metal)
  • Hand-Made Letter-Press Cards with Woodcut Design (4 designs to collect) - First-ever card type never before produced by Topps!
  • Creator Autographs (signed by artists and writers of the series)

 Sketch Cards will include:

  • Red Logo (common)
  • Green Logo (Uncommon Variant)
  • Creator (LIMITED!) by Mars Attacks creators including Dan Brereton, Ed Repka, Alex Horley, James Nelson, Bob Larkin and more!
  • Throwback (1 Per Artist)  inspired by the classic 1960s series
  • Dual Artist  (3.5 x 5) featuring the work of TWO artists!  Limited to 50, and will be a THIRD Super Rare box-topper in boxes they are randomly inserted into!  

ALL SKETCHES will be COLOR and drawn on premium 24pt stock.

Risks and challenges

When it comes to fulfillment, every project has potential obstacles, and ours is no different. Every Topps card set involves many moving parts: from production and design, to business and marketing. For Mars Attacks we have the added necessity of working with more than a hundred freelance artists.

However, as a company with more than 75 years of experience delivering high quality collectibles, as well as a pool of well-respected, veteran artists at our disposal, we have added resources that most Kickstarters don't. We will be doing everything we can to both keep this project on-schedule, as well as deliver the best, highest quality product possible.

Our superstar illustrators are already working on new base card art, while our sketch artists are well underway drawing their allotment of cards. One of the many advantages of Kickstarter is that with a more flexible production we can give our artists even more time to produce their very best work-- for YOU!

Meanwhile our production partner (Sidekick Lab in Vermont) has already begun test printing the various card types, and has mapped out the printing and packing of the entire series to ensure we are ready to go as soon as the campaign concludes. Along with Sidekick, we have spent many months preparing for this campaign, costing out the printing, production, packing, shipping and creation of everything involved.

But whether issues arise or not, we plan to communicate to our backers throughout the entire process and update the status of our project in real time. We hope that in addition to reassuring you of our commitment to creating the best set possible, it will give you a unique insight into the behind-the-scenes making of a Topps card series!

FAQ

  • I’m new to trading cards. What are parallels, hits, chase cards, inserts and all that?

    Parallels are rare variant versions of the main cards that can be found randomly inserted into packs. In this set there are 3 “levels” of variants, that increase in rarity. The Heritage Parallels format the art to look like the original 1962 series; the Concept Parallels show the artists original sketches for the final paintings; and Foil Parallels simply print the cards on reflective silver foil stock.

    Chase cards, are simply additional cards that are harder to find in a box.

    “Hits” are super rare cards, usually 2 per box, and typically have a very unique attribute: in the case of this set, our “hits” are original sketches (hand drawn by one of our specially selected artists), artist autographs, metal cards (reproducing the original 1962 cards) and letter-press cards (hand-pressed premium cards, and using unique woodcut style artwork).

    Inserts is an umbrella term that refers to any card that is not a common base card, and includes all of the above.

That said, we feel that the “Sweet Spot” $300 Prize Captive Bundle is the best value for the average collector, and contains a good cross section of the set. If you are a more casual fan, the $100 bundle is a great deal, and contains all of the basic components of the main set, including a collector binder to store and enjoy your cards.
