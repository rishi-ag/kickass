Risks and challenges

Game is 100% complete. Once kickstarter is funded the money transfers hands then we use it in the production cost of the first edition bulk printing of Colours and Shapes and the rest is used in the shipping of said game from production facility to Athens, GA then we take said game and put it into generic postal envelopes, slapped with shipping labels made for every order using the rest of the fund then sending them out to you lovely individuals that want to give this game a shot.

The T-shirts will be paid for out of my pocket as well as the bonus Kickstarter Character.

I believe that focusing too much on the risks can bog you down with what-ifs and could-of-beens. Just know that we will do everything on our end to get you the premium quality game that you ordered to your door. The only thing we can't control is that of the USPS but hey who can.
