About this project

Our Goal:

The idea behind Baku is to create a relaxing beverage that uses Kenyan ingredients. Our team is committed to the notion that you can create a high quality, healthy product that fosters a positive relationship between Kenyan Farmers and our customers.

Some describe Baku as a sweet-tea that uses substantially less sugar, some thought of it as a hybrid tea with juice flavors. One thing that remained constant is thatÂ BAKU TASTES GREAT!

Our Story

Our team met in college, where consistent all-nighters drove us to find a better alternative for coffee and energy drinks. Either in a moment of creative genius or our sleep-deprived mania, we decided to cold brew Kenyan tea. We add a mixture of organic juice and a combination of matcha, chamomile, hibiscus and passionflower. Our goal was to reduce our stress and remain focused for studies. The Baku recipe proved to be a wholesome and nutritious answer to our abuse of coffee and energy drinks. We found it difficult to find Kenyan tea in the market. In fact, itâ€™s almost desperately under represented. By supporting us you are supporting ethically sourced, healthy and delicious ingredients in every can. We can hand out samples all day, but with your help we can increase our production and take our passion for tea to the market!

Risks and challenges

We have already decided on our supply chain, as well as our production facility, warehouse, labels, and the facility which we will be conducting nutrition tests. The aim of this kickstarter is to raise the necessary level of funding to start the process of mass producing our beverage. Producing a clean looking bottle for every drink served is inherently more difficult than selling and sampling our home-made Baku recipe. To offset many of these problems we have planned for 6 long months about ways to limit our exposure to major risks. We have valuable mentors who have showed us the most cost effective way to safely bottle our product, procure equipment, and create an appealing label. Our plan for manufacturing is low-cost due to the small size of our operation, but it is almost infinitely scalable once we are able to increase our sales. We are projecting for not only a successful launch in the DMV area, but the ability to rapidly grow across the country. Our goal is to approach stores that fit our values as a brand; we want to break into the Whole Foods of the world. One of our mentors has helped us identify a plan to eventually approach Whole Foods once we can satisfy a larger order. Additionally, we have been helped immensely with the development of our business plan by the Maryland Small Business Development Center at the Montgomery County Department of Economic Development. By working with a department that helps small businesses on a daily basis, we hope to alleviate many of the financial problems that many startups often ignore.

FAQ

  • How does the tea relax you?

    We strive to create a relaxation beverage that avoids using chemicals or sleep-aids like melatonin. Our beverage aims to relax without creating a feeling of grogginess. We do this by utilizing a variety of ingredients with natural relaxation components, from chamomile to lemongrass and matcha just to name a few; Our ingredients create a naturally relaxing profile.

    Last updated: Fri, Apr 17 2015 10:25 am EDT
  • Is it a functional drink?

    You could say that, Baku is aimed to help you feel more focus and calm after drinking it. And that is accomplished by the blend of the ingredients we use, from passionflower, chamomile, to matcha to name a few. Each ingredient offers different relaxation components without slowing you down or make you drowsy. With the little amount of added sugar we put into each can of Baku, we are confident that Baku is a great healthy alternative to many functional drinks in the market while still can be drank as a tea beverage.

    Last updated: Fri, Apr 17 2015 10:36 am EDT
  • So... it's a tea right?

    That is correct! Baku is a ready to drink tea based relaxation beverage that has Kenyan black tea, chamomile, hibiscus, matcha green tea, sencha green tea, passionflower, lavender, lemon grass, lemon balm, blackberry leaves and stevia leaves all into one single tea beverage.

    Last updated: Fri, Apr 17 2015 10:36 am EDT
  • Where are the ingredients from?

    Majority of our ingredients comes directly from Kenya, we have set up a network with the farmers to give us the privilege to use the finest Kenyan tea leaves to make a ready to drink beverage.

    Last updated: Fri, Apr 17 2015 10:36 am EDT
  • How do you help Kenyan farmers?

    Kenyan farmers produce a high quality product, but due to a reliance on a more complex supply chain they are only able to sell a small amount of inventory in any given year. Our plan is to buy the high quality surplus inventory that farmers are unable to sell, creating more opportunity for income with continual business for Kenyan farmers. Additionally, our goal is to procure 80% of our ingredients from Kenya to support other Kenyan produce from tea leaves to lemongrass.

    Last updated: Fri, Apr 17 2015 10:36 am EDT
  • Is it Vegan? Is it Non-GMO? Is it Organic?

    Yes, Yes, and Yes. We use only 100% Organic Kenyan black tea and the only way we sweeten our tea is by adding 100% organic fruit juice and 100% organic cane sugar.

    Last updated: Fri, Apr 17 2015 9:22 pm EDT
  • Where are you selling?

    After Baku's kickstarter campaign, we will be aiming to produce Baku mostly for farmers markets and organic food stores in the DC, MD, VA area and online store. With your help, we can expand our operation and begin not only offering our beverage online to kickstarter backers, but begin our plan of expansion and eventually reach a store near you!

    Last updated: Fri, Apr 17 2015 10:36 am EDT
  • Why cans?

    It chills faster, it's more recyclable than your plastic bottles, and the design looks sleek and modern!

    Last updated: Fri, Apr 17 2015 10:36 am EDT
