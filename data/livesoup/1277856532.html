About this project

PYRAMIDS - Artwork designed and made by Sebastien Cuk

The art will be made from several layers of 1/8th inch baltic birch plywood. Each piece is glued together, finished by hand, and will ship ready to hang without additional framing.

This is a limited edition. If ten people back this tier, those pieces will be an edition of 10. The first person to back that tier will get a piece numbered 1/10. And so forth.

Hello everyone, I'm Sebastien.

My passion is woodworking. I built a box to store my personal engraved business cards and display them with style. It holds 50 wooden cards total.

I designed wooden engraved business cards to promote my artwork. And, I received great response from people who've seen and had the cards in their hands. 

I decided to combine both and this is how BOX OF CARDS was created.

I want to share BOX OF CARDS with you, so you can share your BOX OF CARDS with your personalized engraved business cards with the rest of the World.

That's where you come in and save the day!

In order to succeed, we need to reach a minimum funding of $1,500 to purchase wood in bulk and get this project going.

As a reward, you will get a BOX OF CARDS with business cards designed for your needs and a special backer price to re-order them in the future.

I cannot do it without your support.

Thank you for making this project a reality and for being hip.

You rock!

The boxes are designed to hold 50 engraved business cards and display one card on top of the cover. They are laser cut and assembled by hand.
They come is 2 different finishes (Natural or Stained).

The dimensions are 4.25" x 4.25" x 2.5".

The designs have already been perfected and the cards look AWESOME. I've got a lot of good feedback and people are blown away when they see the details.

The cards are made by laser cutting and etching. They are standard business card size and lightweight (less than 3 grams. That's 0.1 ounce). They are made with 1/16" thick wood and fit perfectly in your wallet.
