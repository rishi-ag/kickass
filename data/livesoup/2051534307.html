TO ANYONE PLEDGING PLEASE READ:

1. WRITE YOUR 3 WORDS AND ADDRESS IN THE PERSONAL MESSAGE TAB AFTER CHOOSING THE REWARD 

2. well that's it actually..

So anyway! This is probably the biggest art project I've ever attempted and the most insane seeing as I have no idea what words are going to be thrown at me or how many people will join in. 

Basically the idea of this is, you tell me 3 completely random words (or maybe even an actual sentence) and I'l draw it up into a picture! I've always loved art and hopefully this will help me get a lot more practice and have a few laughs on the way... and you get pictures out of it! BARGAIN

I may be in way over my head...

Literally any words are a go though! How about " lemon snatching buddha" or "iron dragon boat" (just a few examples)

Risks and challenges

With anything like this there could be risks, the most glaring would be those dastardly postmen losing your belongings! Depending on the amount of backers a matter of finishing every piece in time could result in a later delivery date but other than that it should be smooth sailing!
