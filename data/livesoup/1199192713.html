About this project

Valdis was founded in New York City in 2012 by Christine Dow and Kate Chen as a collaborative effort to give women beautifully crafted and thought out designs that have a story and a life. Over the past three years we've created 5 collections and work out of our studio in the garment district in New York.

By backing our campaign you are not only pre-ordering our beautiful, high quality dresses, jackets, and tops set for delivery in September 2015, but are also becoming a supporter of the local fashion community including manufacturers and independent designers in New York City. 

**To our $3,000+ donators we are offering a custom made designer piece tailored for you! You'll meet with us for an initial consultation followed by 2 fitting sessions. Choose from one of three designs from a previous collection that we will provide for you as well as receive a custom selection of fabrics and colors to make it truly one of a kind.

We are very happy to be able to offer our Kickstarter backers exclusive special prices on these items. Once the Kickstarter is complete we must sell our items at the regular retail price.

Why we need your support

Valdis is passionate about keeping production local and made in New York. We believe that the most beautiful garments are created with the most hands-on techniques. However, the rapid decline of the NY garment district has become a serious concern for us and many other emerging designers in the city.

Throughout the first 5 seasons Valdis has been able to build a strong foundation. Now with a little momentum, we're trying to share our vision of well-made clothing made in New York with more people by expanding production through Kickstarter. This campaign gives us the opportunity to broaden our reach while also building a community around our vision.

In 1960, 95% of clothing sold in the U.S. was manufactured in the Garment Center, now that number has decreased to approximately 3%. Despite this drastic decline, there are still 846 fashion companies headquartered in New York City, which is more than London, Paris, and Milan combined. New York City is the fashion capital of the world, and the Garment Center continues to make that possible. The existence of the Garment Center continues to be threatened by new development and production outsourcing. These pressures have resulted in the loss of thousands of manufacturing jobs and cripples New York City’s ability to serve as the epicenter of research and development for fashion. https://www.scribd.com/doc/69466169/2/II-WHERE-WE-ARE-TODAY#

Our Story

 What brought us together was our love for the craft of fashion and the need for well made clothing that is modern yet timeless. We want every woman who wears Valdis to be clothed in beauty and confidence. As two girls living in New York we understand the need for function as well as quality and design. We created this collection for women to feel amazing and empowering in. We want the women who wear Valdis to reconnect with their clothes and think beyond the three-week fashion cycle of quick trends.

We design out of our studio in the garment district and work with factories just a few blocks away who create the samples and run the production for each collection. Over the years it has become increasingly more difficult for fashion companies to resist the pressures of outsourcing. Even though it's just the two of us, we design, merchandise, create, sell, and market everything ourselves.

Thank you for your support and we invite you to join with us and the local community in our endeavor to support keeping fashion hands-on and alive in New York!

Risks and challenges

Valdis is in its fifth season and we are confident in our ability to produce this collection in accordance with our projected timeline.
We have been working with our manufacturers and fabric suppliers for over 5 years and maintain a great relationship with them. We will work our hardest to try and ensure we meet our projected delivery dates without compromising our high quality standards. However if any issues present themselves we will notify our backers and work quickly to resolve them.
