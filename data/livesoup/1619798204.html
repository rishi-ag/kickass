About this project

Bozu is the idea of Philippe who partnered with Antonio to bring on KICKSTARTER a playful and colored device to hold your toothbrush or razor away from tilting glasses and cup gunk.

You probably experienced that using a cup/glass as a holder can be inconvenient especially when using a small sink. Bozu is perfect to use in showers ( to hold razors) or small bathrooms (especially the ones found on boats and camping vans, in these instances you can attach the Bozu directly on the glass fiber walls). Older kids love putting back their toothbrushes in the Bozu rather than leaving them hanging around the counter for mum and dad to collect. 

Bozu behaves like any other suction cup: attaches well on glass/mirrors, glazed ceramics with no texture, laminate panels, polished surfaces like metal or fiberglass panels. 

Bozu is not intended to be a toy and should be used only as a holder for toothbrushes and razors.

The idea is to collect funds to finance a mold and initial roll out batch of Bozu holders in 4 colors.

Risks and challenges

This project is about manufacturing an object, as such it's exposed to the typical issues you can encounter when launching a fabrication process: issues with the mold, quality of the items injected, color consistency etc.
In our case the object's shape was designed to be simple, easy to assemble and made with an inert and durable material like silicon.
The Bozu comes assembled with it's detachable mouth ( the holding element) already inserted.
We do do not expect particular challenges in production.

Antonio in his full time job rolls out major packaging programs for many international brands and is very familiar with the entire pipeline of the process: from design to fulfillment.
