What if you took the idea behind Quantum Leap and went after something big?

Like Indiana Jones going after the Holy Grail, our main character Professor Francis Kane destroys his career and reputation when he proposes a time travel experiment that will give him the chance to crash a major event, such as the Assassination of Abraham Lincoln. Â 


With the authorities and an evil cabal on his trail, Professor Kane successfully travels through time, where he appears right in front of Ford's Theater as John Wilkes Booth is making his fateful walk across the street. Â Professor Kane anxiously tries to save the life of the President.

Waking up from his coma, Faro learns the true consequences of time travel when his actions have led to the kidnapping of now living President Abraham Lincoln to Mexico while the American government is left in shambles and disarray.

This next adventure will result in a duel to the death that will radically alter the global stage for both the European & Western theaters.

Whether you're coming aboard for the first time or returning for the latest issue, you will be treated to ALL NEW COVERS for ALL FARO CHAPTERS

The theme of the 5 Covers is THE DEVIL's HAND, which is a Tarot Card Flush featuring 4 Aces & The Death Card. We are using this theme to celebrate the 5 female protagonists who are perpetually pulling at Faro's priapic puppet strings. What is a hero without his gallery of Bond Girls?
