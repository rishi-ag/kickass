Welcome to the Kickstarter for THE LAST LINE graphic novel series!  The TLL squad and I really appreciate you taking the time to visit our humble fundraiser :)

First, let's go over what you'd be supporting and what your investment provides.  Most importantly, what's...

Youth is the embodiment of idealism and naïveté.  Dependence and freedom.  Wonder and expectation.  These dynamic opposites propel the young of the world into disparate outcomes and maturity.  In essence, leading them to become either good...or evil.

The YOUNG RANGERS USA, a tried and true Youth Organization (much like the Boy Scouts) has steered adolescents toward the light since its inception during the Progressive Era, circa 1912.  By instilling the virtues of survivalism, conservation, and self-reliance, it has produced some of America's best and brightest.  

Over a century of technology, modern dogma, and social media later, the virtues of the YRU are falling on deaf ears.  There are now only a few still willing to adhere to the code.  One of those few, Artie Penski, perseveres to rally his fellow youth to the YRU banner...to no avail.  In this new world a Young Ranger is a standard joke.

But that standard is forever unhinged when an OLD ENEMY plunges America into darkness through an EMP event.   Making uncontaminated water scarce and technology useless.  This dawns a new age of chaos and destruction which shows up on Artie's front door in the form of the nefarious biker gang: THE RATTLEHEADS.  

With its military and civil organizations in tatters, the light of America is sputtering...and if snuffed out will never be reignited.  

In this time of great peril for the American Ideal, Artie, his squad, and the YRU discover that they are THE LAST LINE of defense.

THIS KICKSTARTER will help to raise the funds for that first all important 28-page issue of THE LAST LINE in digital and print formats. We've gotten off to a great start with the creation of the first five pages.  The script for the this first issue is complete as well as the scripts for the other five issues for the first graphic novel.  But with these funds we should be able to get this first issue out to all of you young progressives and idealists!!!  Also we should be able to make some headway in distribution!

ILLUSTRATIONS - RYAN WINN

Ryan is a true blue professional who has illustrated, pencilled, and inked for some of the most hallowed halls in comic-dom.  SPAWN for IMAGE, ULTIMATE FANTASTIC FOUR for MARVEL, and BATMAN for DC to name only a few of the many books that bare his moniker.  Ryan is an innovative, industrious risk taker who has helped to shape THE LAST LINE into what it is evolving into.  His work here speaks for itself and his work ethic is unrelenting - a gift to behold.  Check him out here.

COLORS - JEAN-FRANCOIS BEAULIEU (JOHNNY BLUE)

Johnny has brought his dynamic creativity to core of THE LAST LINE.  His professional portfolio is awe-inspiring as he has also splashed his palette all over the comic world: for MARVEL the likes of X-MEN, ROCKET RACCOON, and AVENGERS ARENA and many others.  Without his expert craftsmanship the potency of TLL would lack a great deal of its vitality.  Check out some of his other inspired offerings here.

SCRIBE - CHRISTOFER FREY

As creator of TLL I take a lot of pride in the fact that this comic, being my first and foremost, is finally ready to be brought into the world.  I'm a screenwriter and playwright in Los Angeles, but isn't everyone?  There's a little more about me here.  You can also follow me and TLL on Twitter, Tumblr, and Facebook for more exciting news and events. 

TLL has taken an eon to bring to fruition, but I'm so grateful to my squad, my wife, my friends and family that have helped it to rise out of the primordial ooze.  I'd also like to send special thanks out to our Video Producer Cameron Freeman, who's excellent workmanship you can see in our KS video.  As well as to Phred Brown, Grammy Award Winning Musician of Bruno Mars, who furnished us with this phenomenal TLL score.  And finally to Troy Peteri who we enlisted to do the initial lettering on these first five pages.

What does my pledge fund?  Most of this money will go to my squad - Ryan and Johnny to complete the remaining pages of the comic and cover art.  Remaining capital will finance the digital production (in all likelihood for Comixology), a print run, the backer rewards, and shipping.

Risks and challenges

The world of comics is fraught with pitfalls and the squad and I well versed in that world. Production setbacks, time commitments, deadlines, distribution headaches to name a few. These would have (and have) deterred me or anyone from trying to produce such a book before. But I believe that the TLL squad that we have in place, my current position/access within Sony Pictures Entertainment and the connections already made here in Los Angeles can ensure not only that TLL will reach its goal but move on to the next stage of its evolution. While this is not the first incarnation of TLL, through trial, error, writing, and rewriting this edition now has the maximum potential. Because now all of the questions have been answered, the networking groundwork has been laid, the professional talent has been acquired, and we are committed to making this simple story about simple heroes simply successful.
