About this project

No ordinary audio company

Flare Audio’s founder Davies Roberts is not your typical loudspeaker designer. Davies has used applied physics rather than acoustics to re-invent the loudspeaker from first principles.

Flare's groundbreaking work in professional audio has already been wowing some of the world’s most respected musicians and producers. Jimmy Page recently introduced the 40th anniversary playback of Led Zeppelin’s seminal album Physical Graffiti on the Olympic Studios Flare system, telling audience members that this would be the best that they, or he, had ever heard the album sound.

Get Flare’s pro sound, in miniature

We’ve now distilled everything that’s great about Flare’s professional products into a range of headphones and earphones - researched, designed and created from beautiful materials in our UK production facility.

Reference R2 Earphones

Our Reference R2 Earphones take Flare’s unique distortion free sound down to the micro level. R2’s innovative design offers unrivalled purity of sound in an incredibly small and lightweight package.

Turned on a robotic precision lathe and created from one solid bar of aerospace aluminium, R2 is incredibly compact and has a far higher degree of sound isolation than other earphones due to its solid metal construction. Each R2 driver enclosure measures just 12mm x 7mm.

R2's cable and driver assembly is user-replaceable (a first for an earphone product), and comes with professional grade Comply™ Isolation memory foam earbuds.

Reference R2 Features

  • Pure, distortion free sound
  • Highest degree of sound isolation
  • Incredibly compact, durable design and construction
  • Precision-turned from aerospace aluminium
  • Micro 5mm neodymium driver
  • Pressure-balanced design is safer for hearing
  • Pro grade Comply™ Isolation memory foam tips
  • Unique user-replaceable cable & driver assembly

R2A - what to expect from the sound

You'll be able to identify individual bass instruments. Highs are extended and are not obscured by bass, meaning every instrument can be heard clearly, even with the most complex tracks. The sound quality R2A produces is far beyond that of any other traditional earphone.

R2A is manufactured from aerospace aluminium 6082. Each enclosure is one piece turned from a solid bar of material, making it extremely rigid, helping stop vibrations that damage sound. This improved rigidity works in tandem with our internal pressure-balancing technology to achieve a truly linear response.

R2S - what to expect from the sound

The increased material strength of stainless steel creates superior high frequency response and very detailed stereo imaging.

Each R2S is manufactured from a solid bar of stainless steel 303. This material is 6x stronger than aluminium 6082, which is key to improving sound quality when coupled with Flare's pressure-balancing technology.

R2PRO - what to expect from the sound

R2PRO is our highest quality earphone in terms of realism and stereo imaging. There is zero audible distortion, which means zero ear fatigue.

All frequencies sound true and clean from the deepest lows through to the highest highs and pure vocal presence. R2PRO's stereo imaging is a mirror image of your natural imaging (what you hear without using headphones).

Each R2PRO enclosure is manufactured from a solid bar of titanium grade 5. This material is 12x stronger than aluminium 6082 and is the only element to feature a close packed hexagonal structure. It's this atomic symmetry coupled with R2's cylindrical design (the strongest possible shape) and Flare’s patent-pending pressure-balancing technology that results in the R2PRO’s enclosure being the most rigid possible, creating the highest possible quality of sound.

Reference R1-Mk2 Headphones

R1-Mk2 creates the ultimate headphone listening experience, revealing details previously masked by traditional ‘acoustic’ approaches to create a totally natural listening experience.

Featuring DSV (Dual Sided Vortex™) technology R1-Mk2 releases pressure on both sides of the driver. R1-Mk2's vortexes are precision milled into solid billets of aerospace aluminium which are compressed with Space™ technology to remove enclosure interference.

Reference R1-Mk2 features

  • The first ‘fully open’ headphone with dual sided Vortex™ technology & Space™ technology
  • Precision milled from solid billets of aerospace aluminium
  • Hand-stitched memory foam ear pads and headband
  • Rotatable parts for flat storage
  • Studio grade cable with anti-kink webbing
  • 3.5mm gold mini-jack connector
  • Flare Audio carry bag

R1-Mk2 - what to expect from the sound

The first fully open headphone has a sound similar in nature to electrostatic loudspeakers. High and low frequencies roll off naturally so that depth can be heard in the sound. Audiophiles and those wanting to explore natural sound are best suited to R1-Mk2, as ears take time to become used to the sound. Once you become used to the sound of R1-Mk2 your ears open up to a new world of sound, and you'll find it hard to go back to traditional headphones.

The Pro version of the R1 is designed for maximum comfort during long studio sessions, and features memory foam padded headband and ear pads for the ultimate in comfort and acoustic isolation.

Reference R1 Pro features

  • ‘Fully closed’ headphone with pressure-balancing technology
  • Precision milled from titanium grade 5 (enclosures) and aerospace aluminium 6082 (ear pad holders)
  • Hand-stitched memory foam ear pads and headband
  • Black anodised finish
  • Rotatable parts for flat storage
  • Studio grade cable with anti-kink webbing
  • 3.5mm gold mini-jack connector

R1PRO - what to expect from the sound

The sound produced by R1-PRO headphone is identical to that of our R2PRO in-ear earphone. Bass frequencies have extreme depth with incredible accuracy and high frequencies are detailed with clear stereo imaging. Traditional closed headphones distort sound. R1PRO's pressure-balancing technology removes this distortion while staying closed to the outside world. Being closed means total isolation for the listener, and no audible leakage to others.

R1PRO’s enclosure parts are manufactured from a solid bar of titanium grade 5. This reduces enclosure resonance to the bare minimum, delivering the highest sound quality possible as a result.

Flare’s revolution in sound quality

The problem

Traditional design treats the loudspeaker like an acoustic instrument, leading to:

  • Additional, unwanted resonance
  • Limited and distorted driver movement
  • Standing wave interference
  • Asymmetry distortion of waveform information
  • Phase distortion
  • Non-linear frequency generation

Flare’s solution

  • Distortion free sound
  • True transmission of source material (you hear exactly what was recorded)
  • Sonic immersion with precise stereo imaging
  • Linear driver movement for total timbre accuracy
  • Full frequency response
