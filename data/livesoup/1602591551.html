About this project

Thank You For Supporting Destructive, This project is to help raise money to further investment into the brand- Destructiveco. with the money raised we will use it toward buying all the products needed to send all the rewards you backers buy, to you all. 

After the kickstarter is done, and all the profits made will go back into the brand to help make it bigger and better for the next project.

We will end up making multiple kickstarter projects for us to make and save as much money as possible to open a bigger warehouse and store so we can mass produce in the future. 

We can't wait to hear all the feedback from all our backers. 

Thanks again Chris & Cameron 

Destructive Co - Always Strive for more!

Risks and challenges

We don't see us having any risk or challenges- We might end up pushing the delivery date back a couple weeks, but from the time we finish the campaign it gives us a solid 7 weeks to produce. Which should be enough time.
