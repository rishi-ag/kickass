Risks and challenges

I have prepared for this project by selecting the wood and completing prototypes of most of the bowls offered as rewards. I am familiar with the Kickstarter format having completed 15 projects including the successful delivery of all the rewards. I believe this project will be completed successfully and on time. As with my last project I will begin shipping rewards early as they are completed. If there are any delays or problems I will keep my Backers up to date with regular “Updates” Possible causes of delays in a project like this are machine problems or injuries which are rare. All the materials and supplies necessary to complete the project are already in my possession
