MEET TRAVIS

Travis walked in our front door about 6 months ago for the first time. The next time he came in just a few weeks later he was in a wheelchair. Below is the email he sent us following that visit. No one better represents what our brand is all about than Travis. He is our "Michael Jordan".

"What's good DTE?! I just filled out your contact form on your website expressing my desire to get involved with your company. I am looking for anything possible, including volunteering, an Internship, or even a part-time position if that would make things easier. I have now been to the Tacoma location twice and each time I came away making a purchase. Not only did I fall in love with the clothes, jewelry, and accessories, but I fell in love with what DTE represents as well. I feel like who I am as an individualÂ and what I'veÂ fought through, fits in seamlessly with the message of DTE. Another reason why I want to be involved with Do The Extraordinary is how inspired and motivated DTE has made meÂ towards my everydayÂ life. DTE has played a vital role inÂ recently turning my life around. I am currently in a wheelchair and have now been paralyzed 4 separate times. I originally broke my back playing college football back in 2001 while playing for Eastern Washington University. Since myÂ initial injury, I have undergone 38 spine surgeries, have hadÂ over 40 implanted titanium screws/rods into my spine, have undergoneÂ 4Â levels of spinalÂ fusion surgery, required 2 Intrathecal Pain Pumps beÂ implanted into my stomach and spine for pain management, and most recentlyÂ hadÂ a Spinal Cord Stimulator implanted onto my spinal column. I've been told over and over to "stop dreaming" and toÂ "get used to life in a wheelchair". So far I've beaten the odds of becoming permanently paralyzedÂ 3 times now and am in the process of beating the odds for a 4th time. And one of the main reasons why I'm able to constantly fight, continue staying positive, and never giving up is from the inspiration I get from DTE. How you might ask? Well, since everything has gone down with my health, I haveÂ relentlessly been looking for some type of fresh passion. Something different than football. Some days getting out of bed seems impossible due to the insanely intense pain. The pain and limitations are endless and unforgiving, constantly reminding me of what I'm limited toÂ and no longer able toÂ do anymore. However, the passion I get out of living the DTE life can be applied towards facing my health challenges...and help me inÂ overcoming those seemingly insurmountable obstacles. So many doctors or "experts" have told me to give up and start getting used to life in a wheelchair. In all honesty, I'm not ready for that life. And DTE will help me achieve my latest goal of re-learning how to walk again for the 4th time!

Looking good and feeling good go hand-in-hand in my opinion. DTE is the perfect combination for my life and health. Due to my health issues, I must wear huge and cumbersome leg and back braces, as well as requiring the use of a cane, walker, or wheelchair to live my life. Because of these requirements, finding clothes that are both comfortable AND stylish at the same time have been impossible...until now! I have been living in comfortable sweats, but I haveÂ looked like I belong at a high school gym for too long now. Not exactly how you want to look on a date or going out with friends. I also feel that by wearing sloppy and unforgiving clothes, I am putting out the message that I don't take pride in myself and how I represent my story. It makes me feel defeated when I see myself looking disheveled. And once I feel defeated, I've lost the battle and allow my disabilities and pain to take control of my life. On the other hand, if I did dress up on the rare occasion, I would be so uncomfortable or overheated that I would most likely avoid the activity all together and once again be stuck at home, a prisoner to my bad health. And that is another loss in my book. You can see how important clothes are to me and my life. I'm so tired of living and feeling like that.

Enter DTE!! The functionality of DTE is far greater than anything I've ever owned before. I can go to Physical Therapy in the same outfit I would go out to dinner in! And I would look and feel great while doing it! At the same time, I could add a DTE hat or necklace to truly individualize my outfit. Feeling confident in myself goes very far in helping me deal with my health challenges. I think I have found everything I have been looking for in DTE. I would be ecstatic being involved at any level or capacity with DTE. At the very least DTE has a customer for life. And please know that what DTE represents and creates is far more important than just designing incredibly stylish and athletic clothes. DTE inspires and motivates individuals to continue grinding, pushing, and fighting in their everyday lives. To continue searching for that self confidence and individuality, as well as ways to express ourselves. And to never stop believing in ourselves. For that I thank you DTE! I'm so excited about my first pair of denim joggers and T-shirt and I look forward to hearing back from you. Peace. -Travis

WHO WE ARE

Do The Extraordinary was birthed out of an idea and a friendship between DTE Owner Justin Kercher and Seahawk legend and 2005 NFL MVP Shaun Alexander. A shared vision to create a local, handmade lifestyle brand that merges edgy, urban design with the outdoors and the natural world that surrounds us. A brand that exists to encourage, inspire, unite and identify with the style and heart of OUR generation. A style-conscious generation committed to seeing their world a better place. A generation of world changers. Passionate about LIVING life! EVERYDAY. Outside of the box.

As we look out on the world, we see a generation longing to live lives of purpose, meaning, and impact. We are part of a generation faced with enormous challenges: the environment, the economy, and human rights violations around the world. We are inspired by the extraordinary people before and around us, the people who decided to make a conscious choice to not follow the ordinary cultural message of passivity. We believe that each person is not only capable of doing extraordinary things, but even more, longs to express themselves in a way that matters and makes a difference in the world. For us, Do The Extraordinary is a platform to connect with others who are deciding that they will not be passive, who will be aware, who will educate themselves about what they believe, and who realize they are responsible for the choices that they make. Those everyday choices that have farther reaching effects and ultimately are shaping our world economically and socially. We are joining this movement that is not new, but rare; is now, but not fad; and is part of a fight to not compromise. We are not asking for you to join us. We are asking if we may join you in this fight.

Risks and challenges
