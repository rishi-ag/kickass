Muse Monthly is comfort delivered straight to your door! As the only subscription box of it's kind, Muse Monthly delivers a unique experience for lovers of literature and tea. Every month, you will get a new novel and a full box or tin of tea on your doorstep! So go ahead and curl up, let go of all your stresses, and enjoy. 

Who are we?

We are Christina and Cara, two twenty-something bibliophiles from New Jersey. Christina is an English Literature, Creative Writing, and Art History graduate, as well as a creative writer. Cara has her MA in English. 

That's fine, but let's really get to know you. What are your Hogwarts Houses? 

Cara is a Gryffindor and Christina is a Slytherin. Proving that inter-house cooperation is possible! 

How does it work? 

 Each month, you will get a brand new novel and a full box or tin of tea delivered to your mailbox. The books and tea are curated by us and paired together to provide a unique experience of comfort and warmth. We believe in the healing powers of tea and the enlightening powers of literature, and that there is nothing better than an afternoon spent curled up with a good story and a hot cuppa. 

 What will be included? 

 We are working with several publishers, both big names (Penguin, Harper Collins) and independent (Tin House, Two Dollar Radio) to provide you with some great novels. Our focus is on literary fiction, with a preference towards new publications and new writers. This way, you won't have to fear getting something you've already read. Nor will you have to fear missing out on that great new publication that everyone on your newsfeed is talking about! 

 To go along with your book, we're excited to provide high-quality and unique teas from great companies. Our affiliated brands include local tea companies who we're excited to support, tea companies whose profits go towards building schools in underprividelged parts of the world, and brands who provide unique blends that you won't be able to get anywhere else. We assure you, this is not your average earl grey! You'll get something new and exciting in every box. 

 Will I get to pick the book that is included? 

At this stage in the game, we are working hard to choose books that we're sure you'll enjoy. As we grow, we are hoping to be able to provide more choices. 

What will I get if I back this project? 

 For just $20, you'll get your very first Muse Monthly box! Included will be Our Endless Numbered Days by Claire Fuller and Mt Hood Vanilla Tea from Townshend's Tea Company! Your first box will come with members-only benefits, including access to our Muse Forums, where you can connect with other members to discuss your new book and tea!

If you're already in love and want more, you can also sign up for a three month, six month, or twelve month subscription right off the bat! That way, you'll never have to worry about going to the bookstore when you need something new to read, or having to refill your tea stores! You'll also have access to all our members-only benefits, including the Muse Forums, and special gifts and exclusives along the way! 

 What does my money go towards? 

Mainly, great content. Our focus is on providing great new novels and top quality teas for you to relax with. Your investment will also go towards packaging, marketing efforts, and building an amazing website.

What are the members only benefits you speak of? 

Anyone who buys a subscription will have access to members-only portions of our website, including the Muse Forums. This will allow you to connect with other members and discuss the books you're reading, the tea you're drinking, and whatever else you please. We are excited to build a community of tea and book lovers! 

 Muse Monthly members will also get sneak previews of what will be included in upcoming boxes, exciting videos, and our Snapchat account. Everyone who donates will be added to our mailing list, but members will be the first to get feedback surveys, which will help us improve and curate great content for future boxes! 

When can I expect my first box? 

We are hoping to ship out the first box at the beginning of June!

Risks and challenges

Right now, the biggest risk would be in shipping delays, but we're allowing ourselves some time after the Kickstarter campaign ends to get all that taken care of.

All backers will be asked to provide their email, along with any social media links so that we can update you in case anything happens. Don't worry, we won't keep you in the dark!

FAQ

  • Is the tea included loose or in tea bags?

    Both. The first month's tea is loose. We're hoping to include tea bags or a strainer for 3 month, 6 month, or year long subscribers.

    Last updated: Mon, Apr 13 2015 6:58 pm EDT
  • Can I give this as a gift?

    Absolutely! Just indicate who your gift is going to and where to send it. A survey will be sent out after our project is funded to collect this information.

    Last updated: Mon, Apr 13 2015 6:58 pm EDT
  • Why does buying 3, 6, or 12 months look more expensive?

    All these tiers take into account your shipping. This way, your shipping rate will never go up no matter where you live or how heavy the box is. We're looking out for you!

    Last updated: Tue, Apr 14 2015 3:37 pm EDT
  • Will you be offering International shipping any time soon/

    Yes, we are hoping to offer shipping to Canada and Europe after the Kickstarter is over and we have a full website. The way Kickstarter works is that there is a flat shipping rate for each backer level, so everyone who buys one month pays a certain amount of shipping no matter where they live. If we did this for all our international darlings, shipping would be INSANE and we don’t want to do that to you. Basically we don’t want to end up overcharging you.

    The other reason we aren't doing international shipping yet is because of import laws. Because Muse Monthly includes tea, which is a food item, some countries prohibit us from shipping to you. We want to make sure all our legal business is squared away before we start doing that.

    So, keep following us because we hope to be offering this within the first few months. We are just as stoked about this as you are, and we want to be able to spread the joy of books and tea everywhere!

    Last updated: Tue, Apr 28 2015 7:12 am EDT
