About this project


         ~ART OF THE RODEO~

American rodeo was a passion of mine long before I ever actually attended any of the wild and woolly events. As a young child growing up in northern Indiana, I have no memories of whether rodeos were commonly staged in that part of the country. What I do know is that absolutely no one else in my family had any interest in them: neither of my parents were horse people, so we lived mostly in towns, away from farms and ranches. A traveling photographer's pony was the very first equine I met. At four years young I fell desperately in love, and never got over wanting to live my life among horses and cattle, cowgirls, cowboys, and rodeos! I never changed. 

This project is all about the same things I loved as a child: horses, cowboys, cowgirls, rodeos, and making stuff. As an adult, I added God, America, and prayer.


If you love rodeo, please share this project with other fans. My goal is to have the funds to print and ship 1000 posters, or more. There must be at least that many fans of rodeo in America, so I'm hoping you give me a hand up because I sure can't do this without all of you!

In addition to helping fund this project directly, you can also notify cowgirl, cowboy and rodeo organizations in your area. Any and all of the fine artwork rewards are imminently collectible and are the perfect gift for everyone who loves both rodeo and beautiful art.

Thank you for taking the time to read about my project, and I hope you'll support and share it with other rodeo fans. May God bless you and yours.

Profile photo and video editing: Herschel Mair

Risks and challenges

Fulfillment begins with the existence of product, so since the images I'm offering have already been painted, Step 1 is complete.

The next one is also already in place; that of having "Prayer" and assorted other images that are part of this project photographed in readiness for reproduction. Quotes for printing fine art quality posters, including graphic design where necessary, are on file, as are costs for packaging, labels, shipping, etc.

Advertising in national western magazines is factored in, too, one of the more costly parts of bringing a project like this to successful fruition. As a professional artist for more than three decades, though, I have a great track record of following through on bringing my visions into physical reality: my very first commissioned artworks were fulfilled while I was still in high school.

The production numbers, including amounts that will be paid to Kickstarter.com, and for credit card processing, have been crunched and re-crunched, believe me, in order to arrive at a goal that is as workable as I can make it. Dependent upon the actual volume of rewards to be produced and shipped, I am also prepared to hire a team to help with packaging and shipping. I'm really hoping all of you totally cover me up in work!

Short of temporary delays with printing or shipping, I am optimistic that all will proceed as expected, or even better! At any rate, I will be posting frequent updates for this project.

In the unlikely event that I become unable to personally handle the promised development and fulfillment of Rewards, I am taking care to select someone who can step in and act on my behalf.

There you have it! So, come on, put me and my team to work sending "Prayer" out across America!
