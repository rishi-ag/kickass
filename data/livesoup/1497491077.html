About this project

This project is the first phase of the release of a collection of 12 original songs. The distribution will include full production & acoustic recordings plus additional bonus tracks.

History & Intention

12 years ago I collaborated with a team of talented musicians to produce the album Shining also released as Effortless Right Action (ERA). In 2015 I continue to receive positive feedback and requests for more music. "Where is it?, we still listen to the old record, that recording changed my life, we want a new record to listen to". Recording ERA 12 years ago changed my life too. It taught me that I had a lot of good work to go out and do! In the aftermath of its release I set off on some new creative adventures.

I built a small business, raised a baby girl who is now a teenager, birthed a baby boy, managed a health crisis and lovingly laboured over the songs that will make up Avalon Isle. Years worth of work, devotion & faith in the process has been lovingly poured in to this project which to date has been funded by me personally.

A collection of beautiful new songs continue to gather momentum as they follow me in my dreams. I often wake at 3am with melodies & lyrics painting the walls of my mind with colours that depict hidden meaning to some event, relationship, emotion, character, time and or place.

Additional resources are required at this critical time where the mirage has stopped glitching in the heat waves and a swim in the oasis sits in the valley below. Lets take a stroll down the hill together! One of the songs has been on the drawing board for over 20 years and has finally come to a resting place. I want to share this labour of love with you and take the listener on a journey to the abundant Isle of Avalon where magic and healing happens.

Track: Effortless Right Action (Acoustic)

The Promise To Deliver

Having been committed to this project for over 12 years I know that with the right level of support the project will be delivered on time and distributed as promised. It is my ultimate goal to have the opportunity to share this realised dream with its supporters. I promise to provide regular updates and deliver the work in a condition that qualifies as a timeless jewel in the crown of your listening pleasure.

THE TEAM

Songsmith - Isis Ashton

A decendant of Gambian Griot heritage and European white magicians Isis brings a fresh language to the art of songs. A dedicated songwriter for over 20 years her point of view invites discussion, thought and encourages taking time for self care, compassion and tolerance. The goal is to bring forth a record that you want to dance to but will also soothe you when you need it. "I want to share songs you can walk to, run to, dance to, drive to, laugh to, cry to and feel that YOU are being heard."

 Track: Fly (Acoustic)

Producer/Composer - Marc Scully

Marc Scully aka Omegaman began honing his skills as a bassist with bands MX Warheads, Love Rodeo, Deadly Hume, Nick Barker & The Reptiles & RATCAT. After many years of touring, Marc moved into the realm of DJing & music production soon to release a growing number of original compositions & remixes under the Omegaman banner.

Omegaman draws on his influences & brings the funk with a side order of reggae, latin, lounge, soul & beats. The debut Omegaman album 'Pheromone' was released on independent Australian label Catalyst Recordings (Inertia) 2007. The release caught the attention of label managers across the UK, Europe & USA. A selection of tracks have been licensed for a number of cd compilations & have also featured in TV adverts & soundtrack sync for film & TV. Omegaman recently joined the roster at Fort Knox Recordings as the first artist released on the label outside of the Washington DC close knit brethren.

Marc is the resident Producer & Studio Director at Studio 57.

www.omegaman.com.au

www.studio57.com.au/recording

http://fortknoxrecordings.com/omegaman

http://soundcloud.com/omegaman

Musician - Blain Cunneen

Blain Cunneen has been working professionally on the Sydney scene for the past 5 years, cutting his way in and finding himself becoming one of Sydney's finest young musicians. Currently, he studies Jazz performance at the Sydney Conservatorium, whilst maintaining a busy performance/session schedule. Inspired by a multitude of styles, Blain draws upon his love for singer/songwriters, jazz, funk, soul, rock and so much more.

Musician - Rhys Melbourne

Rhys Melbourne’s face is one you may see around the Sydney as he tickles the ivories in venues like Opera Bar, Ivy Pool and the Golden Sheaf or at a smattering of festivals. These appearances take many forms. He has teamed with an eclectic mix of artists including Rephrase, the Omegaman Sound System and a variety of ad hoc musical collectives, not to mention his main squeeze (musically) Wires (wiresmusic.com) for whom he regularly tours and writes. 

The auditory component of his finger dancing and knob twirling (session playing and composing/producing) can also be found within the newly discovered phonographic recording process. 

His sounds appear in tv shows such as Neighbours and Wonderland, various commercials and compositions for short films, art installations and video productions. If not doing one of these you will probably find him asleep in the UNSW library in the process of acquiring a music degree.

Drummer - Reuben Alexander

Acclaimed drummer Reuben Alexander has been in the business for over 25 years, touring and recording nationally and internationally in Europe, the USA and Australia. 

After cutting his teeth in London playing an eclectic mix of styles including jungle and drum ‘n’ bass during the European dance music explosion of the early ‘90s, as well as rock, funk, blues and classical percussion, Reuben worked extensively with the band Jack Fury (Alberts Music, London), and entertained large crowds nightly over five seasons in Covent Garden street shows.

In 1997, Reuben started his own project, Must – a four-piece band featuring the singer from Jack Fury – and took it to America where they were signed with Wind-Up Records (Evanscence, Creed, Filter, The Griswolds) in New York. After recording an album in 2000, the band toured the States extensively for four years as a headline act and also as support on tours with Aerosmith, Stone Temple Pilots, INXS, Kid Rock and more... In 2003 Must returned to London, touring with Def Leppard and Thunder as UK and Europe support act at the start of the ongoing ‘80s rock band resurgence.

Since then, Reuben has been living and working in Australia, working extensively in the session scene with renowned producers such as: Nick Launay, Tony Cohen, Wayne Connolly (Alberts), Rob Feaster (Nashville, USA), Tim Carr (Studios 301), Paul Pilsneniks (Studios 301), Marc Scully (Studio 57) ... and many more.

www.reubenalexander.net

Vocalist/Harmonies - Alphamama

ALPHAMAMA is a dynamo on stage! Combining her love of 90's rnb, hiphop, jazz, reggae and soul she serves up unique renditions of the songs that inspired her, always making them her own. A generous performer who loves to interact with her audience, she is a charismatic entertainer with a magnetic energy.  

As a session singer based in Sydney, ALPHAMAMA has been one of the most called upon vocalists in the corporate entertainment world. She has proved herself a versatile performer, taking on virtually every possible genre and wowing audiences in venues as diverse as: The Basement, Tone, The Mac, The Opera House, The Espy, The Toff (Melbourne), Tokio Hotel, The Vanguard, Oxford Art factory, The Factory Theatre, Cave Nightclub, Civic Underground, Cargo Bar, Tonic Lounge, Melt, Sapphire Suite, Notes, Raval, Marble Bar, Hemmesphere and Republic Bar. She has played at countless festivals including Byron Bay Blues and Roots, Woodford Folk Festival, Peats Ridge, Island Vibes, Dreaming Festival, Crave Festival and Global Beats Festival. She has supported and shared the stage with artists such as KORA (NZ), Dead Prez (USA), Jean Grae (USA), Electric Empire, Watussi, Jade Macrae, Paris Wells, Jess Harlen and the Killaqueenz.

Artwork & Video - Peter Metro

Peter Metro is a Multi-Award-Winning Creative Director with extensive advertising, design and illustration experience, having worked in Australia and overseas for some of the biggest names in the business. Peter has a passion for music and the people that make it, and combines his love of photography, typography, illustration, story telling and travel to cook-up tasty artwork, promotional shots and video clips for his many muso mates.

peterm@metroco.com.au

Risks and challenges

1. Artwork & design elements are already in progress & close to completion
2. Suppliers for sustainable printing & packaging have been sourced
3. Planning of the launch party events has commenced and will conclude in May 2015 with final dates being distributed by mid May 2015. Woo Hoo!

Years of work has already been poured in to this project all funded by me personally. The Team has willingly contributed their time, talent and attention to keep moving the project forward in preparation for delivery.

REALLY EXCITED! SO CLOSE!

To get to final production stages I will be relying on the minimum crowd fund raising goal to be reached with additional personal input by me. The estimated total cost will exceed $33,000. Your contribution will be well rewarded and we are so pleased to have the opportunity to share this with you.
