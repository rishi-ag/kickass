About this project

WHO WE ARE

Thanks for visiting our page! We are Ascension Brewing Company, the first microbrewery in the city of Novi, Michigan (population 57,000) in over 10 years. We feature 10 house brewed ales and an amazing farm-to-table kitchen, all served in a relaxed and casual atmosphere at prices that won't destroy your wallet. Our dining room features natural wood accents, a truly unique tree slab bar top, some community-style bench seating, and is 2100 square feet. We have a seating capacity of approximately 80 people. Our location is 42000 Grand River Avenue, approximately 1.5 miles from 12 Oaks Mall. We are a Novi-owned business are proud to support our local economy! 

WHAT KIND OF BEERS WILL YOU BREW?

Short answer: LOTS of styles! Long answer: IPAs, wheats, pale ales, stouts, porters, kolsch, Belgians, sours, barrel aged, ambers, reds,  browns, fruit beers, meads, and MANY more. We love to experiment and strive to be on the leading edge of our customers' palettes and the brewing industry. Our smaller 3.5bbl electric brewing system allows us a lot of mobility to experiment and try new recipes!

WHAT TYPE OF FOOD WILL YOU SERVE?

We are a gastropub and can't wait to serve amazing dishes that pair well with our house made ales. Our goal is to have the best food of any brewery in Michigan. Lofty, indeed!

ONLY 200 MUG CLUB MEMBERSHIPS WILL BE OFFERED.

We are offering 165 memberships in our Kickstarter, and the remaining 35 will be sold in the taproom on opening weekend on a first-come first-served basis. For more details, please visit our Facebook page or our website. 

WHY WE NEED A KICKSTARTER

We are strong believers in community. We think that, in general, our greatest ability to impact the world around us lies within the communities we live. We hope to use Kickstarter to rally our community behind us as we begin the journey to bringing craft beer back to Novi! The money to purchase quality upgrades for our facility. Specifically, we plan to buy two additional fermenters. These fermenters will help us ease the supply and demand restrictions and maintain 10 taps open (instead of running out of them all the time!). If we also reach the stretch goals mentioned in the video, we plan to use the money for better seating, additional kegs, and a fire suppressed hood for the kitchen. 

WHAT HAPPENS IF YOU RAISE MORE THAN YOUR GOAL?

IF WE RAISE $10,000...

We will be able to afford better seating for the brewery. Currently, the chairs we've selected (mainly due to budget restrictions) are on the lower end of the scale and are not as comfortable as we'd like. If we hit $10k, we will use the additional funds to upgrade to these really cool cafe chairs that are way more durable and comfy.

IF WE RAISE $15,000...

We will purchase an additional shipment of kegs so we can begin selling our beer off-site at other local bars and restaurants. This helps our marketing efforts and gives our customers more chances to taste our beer at their favorite dining establishments!

IF WE RAISE $20,000...

We will make our executive chef's dreams come true by being able to purchase a fire-suppressed hood for the kitchen. This will pave the way for us offering an expanded menu and a nearly unlimited number of different flavors and combinations to pair with our beer! Like.. steak!

WHEN WILL I GET MY STUFF?

Most of our reward items will be ready by mid-June, although a few of them could take longer.

WHEN WILL YOU OPEN?

Our target opening date is the weekend of May 30th, 2015. Depending on how the rest of our build out goes, it's possible we may need a few more weeks. Rest assured, we will not open before we are 100% ready!

Risks and challenges

The primary obstacle to us completing our project would be mostly financial. Running out of capital is not likely to happen, but it's always a possibility. Currently we have raised money through private funds and by selling equity stake. If the need arises, we will seek additional investment by selling more equity.
