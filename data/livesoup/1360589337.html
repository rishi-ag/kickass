About this project

Use New Wallet™ to simplify your life, no matter what you are doing!

Project Update May 1, 2015

Simplicity Brings Freedom

We believe life is busy enough, and searching for lost items is robbing us of our precious free time. New Wallet™ combines your wallet and phone into one simple device that keeps you organized, protected, and connected. No more digging through your wallet or purse to find what you need. No more dropping your phone when carrying too many things. No more losing your wallet, phone, or keys. Ever.

New Wallet™ was created by Karen Crawford - CEO, wife, mother of 3, and fitness fanatic. Inspired by her busy, on-the-go life, Karen set out to combine time-proven organization strategies with modern technology to create a project that truly makes life easier.

What is New Wallet™?

  • Never lose your wallet, phone, or keys again
  • Impeccably organized
  • Time-saving (never dig through your wallet, purse, or pockets again) 
  • Built-to-last 
  • RFID blocking (No need to worry about wallet skimming)

It's simple. Organize your cards and ID into your New Wallet™ by inserting them into the protective slots. Insert your phone into New Wallet™. Connect the key tracker onto your key chain. Follow the easy to install instructions on the New Wallet™ app that will connect your wallet and keys.  You will be prompted to set safe zones (where you don't want alarms to sound, like inside your home or office). And that's it. You will now have a safe and secure place to keep your items and you can get on with living life, simply better.

  • Flip ID 
  • Fanned credit card holder 
  • Easy access to your most important items 
  • RFID blocking 
  • Durable case 
  • Locking mechanism (audible sound that lets you know your stuff won't fall out) 
  • Clamshell design (so your stuff doesn't hang out precariously while you're on the phone)
  •  Automatic alerts when lost 
  •  Key fob activated alert 
  •  Distance alarms and safe zones 
  •  Easy-to-use app

Our New Wallet™ application on your phone let's you sync your wallet to your keys and then find them if they are lost. It allows you to find your keys with your phone, or your phone with your keys.

If your New Wallet™ is misplaced or lost, our app can help it get back to you using our online tool or your emergency contact. 

To find your keys, you simply click "find" within the New Wallet™ app and an alarm sounds helping you to find your misplaced keys immediately.

We will post more information about the app specifically in the updates section!

Running iOS 8 or greater. The iPhone 5c is not supported at this time.

Risks and challenges

Forecast Risk: We've spent a lot of time engineering a product that meets the highest standards of performance, durability, and convenience. Our manufacturer has been selected and we are gearing up for production. With that being said, surprises can happen and we will be sure to always be transparent and inform our backers of potential hurdles that may come up.

Supply Chain Risk: We've finalized our production schedule and plans with experienced manufacturers to ensure that the New Wallet™ is on schedule to deliver according to our forecast. Our team is experienced in the full spectrum of the supply chain process. However, unexpected shortages and delays can occur when dealing with numerous components from different suppliers. If anything does come up, we will be sure to notify our backers promptly and work quickly to resolve any issues.

FAQ

  • When is New Wallet expected to ship?

    New Wallet will begin shipping it’s initial units in August 2015

    Last updated: Fri, May 15 2015 11:09 am EDT
  • Does New Wallet come with a warranty?

    New Wallet will replace any unit that does not operate as intended. Simply contact New Wallet (hello@newwallet.com) and request a return authorization and we will ship out a replacement New Wallet when your original unit is received.

    Last updated: Fri, May 15 2015 11:15 am EDT
  • Will my cards or ID get demagnetized while stored inside the New Wallet?

    The ID card is wrapped in a metal holder which keeps it secure. Your cards are sitting inside a plastic card holder which keeps them out of direct contact with the back of the phone. In over a year of testing with this configuration no testers cards have become demagnetized.

    Last updated: Fri, May 15 2015 11:15 am EDT
  • How many items can I store inside my New Wallet?

    There is enough room inside the New Wallet to store your Drivers License, 2 credit cards and a bill or two, but not much more. We intentionally limited the card holder to two cards to reduce the depth of the case so that it still felt good in both your pocket and your hand. New Wallet is designed to streamline your pockets, our principal in designing this product is to reduce bulk and simply life.

    Once you get rid of all the items in your current wallet we are confident that you will enjoy the freedom your New Wallet provides

    Last updated: Fri, May 15 2015 11:15 am EDT
  • Is New Wallet for women only?

    Absolutely not. Men will enjoy the convenience of eliminating the number of items they are required to carry in their pockets every day. Whether on a run or going to the office, your wallet will now be attached to your phone.

    Last updated: Fri, May 15 2015 11:15 am EDT
  • Is New Wallet for teens?

    Any new driver who also has an iPhone will love that New Wallet will allow them to securely carry their new driver’s license with them inside of New Wallet. Parents will appreciate the ability New Wallet’s connected feature so new driver’s won’t misplace their keys or wallets.

    Last updated: Fri, May 15 2015 11:15 am EDT
  • Will New Wallet work with Apple Watch?

    Yes. We are developing an Apple Watch app that will allow you to track your phone, wallet and keys from the Apple Watch.

    Last updated: Fri, May 15 2015 11:15 am EDT
  • How many credit cards will fit in New Wallet?

    Two

    Last updated: Fri, May 15 2015 11:15 am EDT
  • What colors will New Wallet come in?

    Depending on which stretch goals we reach, it will initially be black and white and potentially dark purple and green.

    Last updated: Fri, May 15 2015 11:15 am EDT
  • Do you ship internationally?

    Yes

    Last updated: Fri, May 15 2015 11:15 am EDT
  • What is Kickstarter?

    Kickstarter is crowd funding model that enables scrappy startups – or startups on a tight budget to test market demand without making the hefty investment into inventory that is ready to ship. So instead of offering products ready to be delivered, customers/supporters are given deep discounts to account for the time delay in which they will receive their product. It’s also a way for people to be supportive of innovative startup ideas that are first coming to market.

    Last updated: Fri, May 15 2015 11:15 am EDT
  • Where is New Wallet manufactured?

    The initial units will be manufactured in the Northern California region.

    Last updated: Fri, May 15 2015 11:15 am EDT
