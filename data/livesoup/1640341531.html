About this project

• Gatefold double LP

• Unreleased, 100% exclusive new track

• Alternative artwork by Matt Dixon

• Double coloured vinyl

• Limited to 300 pressings

• Hand numbered

• Signed (optional)

Like our music, we only create things we'd like as fans, and there's no point asking for your help unless we're giving you something very special, and we think this is a good as it gets. The vinyl is the highest quality double LP meaning the best possible sound, and a specially commissioned piece of cover art created to fit the gatefold sleeve.

We've also written an exclusive track that will only appear on this LP. No digital release, no CD.

This is going to be a true labour of love, and the best possible way to hear our new album. We really want our friends and fans to share in this, so we'd like to share the costs. The band will fund two-thirds, and we'd love it if we can get our fans to help raise the rest. We'll make sure you get more than your money's worth in return.

Risks and challenges

There are delays at pressing factories Europewide at the moment thanks to the huge demand for vinyl, so if these affect us we'll endeavour to keep everyone updated.
