About this project

A local boy, the warrior’s gift and a new legend. Isamu Pahoa must face the ghosts and monsters of old Hawaii, even if he doesn’t believe in them. Back by popular demand, the critically acclaimed Hawaiian Superhero, Pineapple Man returns! 

Pineapple Man: Old School Lock up!   This new adventure will tell its character’s never seen before origin. This will be Pineapple Man’s 1st 48-page, full color book!!! Written and illustrated by Sam Campos and colored by Napua Ahina.

I have heard your call and here is my answer!

 PINEAPPLE MAN IS BACK!!!

I can't believe that people remember and are still eager to see new Pineapple Man stories. I am ever thankful for your artwork and kind words regarding our boy.   Currently I have 3 other Pineapple Man comics that have not been published yet, but for the return of Isamu Pahoa, I felt we should start with a fresh story and take you on the journey on how he came to be the meta-human know as the Pineapple Man.  I have laid the entire book out and have already finished 9 pages, so we are well on our way!!!  I'm excited to be once again working with Genesis Maya on this book and our new digital colorist Napua Ahina.   Not only will Pineapple Man be returning for duty on his series, he will be making a guest appearance on Mana Comics 'Aumakua Guardians comic books as well.  So keep an eye out for that!!!

After so many years it will be great to get back into the saddle and tell tales of high adventure on the shores of Hawai'i!

Mahalo for you time and support!

ALOHA!!!

Risks and challenges

As an experienced artist and publisher, I am ready to take on any challenge that may arise. I will do everything possible to make this project a success.

If you choose to pledge this Kickstarter please leave your complete name and complete current address. (e-mail is optional) I would like to ensure you receive your rewards in a timely manner. Thank you so much for taking the time to review our project!

Mahalo!

Sam Campos
