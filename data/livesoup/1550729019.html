About this project

Atlas Needs a New Home! 

Atlas: DIY (Developing Immigrant Youth) was founded by three young people and an attorney in 2012. In the past three years we have worked alongside hundreds of young people from over thirty countries to truly create a cooperative center, where our members work alongside professional staff to develop, implement and oversee programs most needed by our communities. We have outgrown our home on 44th Street and will be moving to new offices on 36th, still in Sunset Park, with more space for programs in a more convenient location for our members. We need your help to build the best, most welcoming, most innovative, most functional office ever!

Update - But wait, haven't you hit your goal?!

Due to the amazingness of our community we were able to raise $10,000 on May 18th, almost two weeks before the end of our campaign! It doesn't end here, however, as our community has made us believe that we can go ever further! We are now aiming to raise $15,000 and use the additional funds to outfit a tech lab for our youth! Check out our updates for more! 

***Please note: As a 501c3 non profit organization all donations are 100% tax deductible!***

About The Project

 We are working with the awesome architect Ryan Labrum (www.labrum-redd.com), who is offering his services pro bono to help design our new home, so we only need to raise moving, construction and design costs. The new space will incorporate a legal suite to assure confidentiality for immigration cases; our signature "CASA" activity room with couches, a library and a place to hang; and our first-ever devoted back office where our staff can have their own cubicles (hurrah!)! All with an innovative, inspiring, feel! We want to build the most inviting space ever! We want to get really creative with the space, with rolling garage doors that can split rooms when we need to, and even a kids’ corner for our members with little ones. The more money we raise the more creative we can be!!! 

We have the space. We have the community. Now we just need YOU to make it a reality!  

 

Risks and challenges

We believe this is a low risk challenge because our move is happening! Depending on how much we raise, of course, certain things (like graffiti murals) may not come to fruition but we will use every penny we get from this campaign and pour it back into our space making it the best ever! The more we raise the more we can do!

FAQ

  • What Exactly Will My Money Go Towards?

    The initial $10,000 we hope to raise will go towards everything we need in our new office! It will NOT pay for our rent (we already have a family foundation that has that covered!) but will include the following:

    *Contractor costs to design the interior of the legal suites (we will have three full time lawyers by the fall and need to make sure they have the utmost of privacy and functionality!)
    *Cost of furniture for our staff (cubicles and offices and storage, oh my!)
    *Paint costs for murals painted by our youth
    *Cost of outdoor furniture to create homework/tutoring lounge on the balcony
    *Building costs for our small garden (to be made with 100% recycled wood!)
    *KidZone materials and toys for our members with small children!

From helping with painting to choosing the best seeds for our garden to tutoring a student in English we can find a great fit for you! If you are interested in volunteering with Atlas please contact Juan at Juan.Marquez@atlasdiy.org!
