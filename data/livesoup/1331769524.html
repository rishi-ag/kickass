About this project

Funtown Mountain is part Roadside Attraction and part Amusement Park and all kinds of Fun, located in Cave City, Kentucky - The Roadside Attraction Capital of America. Funtown Mountain will open June 19th at the former historic Wild West Town, Guntown Mountain and will include the following in Phase I.

Our fundraising efforts are focused on Phase I.

The Haunted Hotel - The oldest haunted attraction in America.
Puttin 'N' Kentucky Mini Golf
Ski Lift Ride
Funtown Souvenir Shack
Hillbilly Tea Shack
The Wolf Pack 5 Animatronic Band
Soaring Eagle Zip Line
Carnival Rides
Lucky Lady Saloon Music Hall
Imagination Land Pop-culture Museum

Â Â 

Phase IIÂ  will include:

The Treetop Village - An Ewok Village inspired land of treehouses
The Kraken Waterslide - To be released daily
Kentucky Rushmore - A massive sculpture with Kentucky Icons.
Lolley Land - A Magical forest experience designed by Kathleen Lolley
The Sunset Dragon - A massive dragon you can sit inside the mouth of and watch the sunset.
Mountaintop Amphitheater - For outdoor concerts and festivals


Funtown Mountain's mission is to promote Kentucky and elevate its reputation by celebrating Southern Arts and Culture but above all else, we absolutely insist on having Fun!

Visit http://funtownmountain.com for more!


The Future of Fun is in your hands.

Risks and challenges

Our biggest challenge at this point is development time. Weber Group, who built Kentucky Kingdom and Holiday World is tasked with developing the 28 acre property. We will be using these funds to create a magical place for children and adult alike. Thank you for your support.

Let's go have some Fun!
