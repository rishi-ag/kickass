About this project

:: WHAT IS PAKROLLER?

Have you ever wished that shipping an item was QUICK and EASY? Well now it is with the all new PakRoller. Slide your item to the bottom of the PakRoller, pull off all 3 adhesive strips, roll up item inside of the PakRoller, seal with top flap, and grab sides pinching them together for the ultimate protection. Finally shipping made easy!

:: THE DESIGN PROCESS

For several months I have worked with my team of engineers to create the perfect product for quickly packaging items without tape, boxes, bubble wrap, or packing peanuts. Best part is that within 10 seconds you've added multiple layers of protection.

:: RESEALABLE FLAPS

With resealable flaps all your have to do is include an extra flap in the shipment and that way your recipient can reuse the PakRoller for their own shipping needs.

The PakRoller is 8" by 20" in order to provide plenty of space for you favorite items (Also comes in 12" by 20") and also enough room to roll it up inside of enough layers of protection. The lightweight design allows your to save money on postage fees. The self sealing design creates the most secure shipping experience possible.
