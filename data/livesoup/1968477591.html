About this project

Welcome to an alternative reality!

Hi, Lee Mothes here...

New Island is ready to reveal itself! 

It is an "as-if" place, somewhere between Middle Earth and Wonderland, but not too far from the real world.  Set in the Indian Ocean near Australia, It is a large island riddled with mysteries such as the Old People, Roger's Dreamland, magical otters, and endless landscapes. 

Last year I published my art book, An Artist's Journey to Nevermore, which contains an introductory chapter on New Island.  Now I'm ready to publish The New Island Guidebook which tells the whole story!

New Island emerged from a long and continuing series of paintings of imaginary coastlines, beaches and towns. Around 1998 I developed a map to locate everything, and then began to write the island's story. Beginning in 2003, a few art lovers wanted to own a part of this island, adding a new dimension of deeds, plat maps and paintings of real peoples' dream houses!

 But most of New Island is empty land...

To make owning part of New Island even easier, I have finally completed the full-color New Island Guidebook! This is the book that will tell you the entire story, with pictures and maps, of the Commonwealth of New Island. Now it is ready to print.

 Here is what you'll find inside:

  • Origins of New Island
  • Climate and weather
  • Plants and animals
  • Paleo culture
  • European settlement-by-accident
  • Shipwrecks and other incidents
  • The Russians

  • Travel by train

  • New Island festivals and holidays
  • Religion and mysteries
  • Growing up on New Island
  • Life on the Very Edge
  • How to get there

The Guidebook, combined with the official road map (below) tell the story of a place that is yours to play on!

Here is a close-up view of the map:

Do what you want: explore as a tourist, write a tale of adventure, or claim a building site and then build your own dream house, cottage, retreat, studio, or secret hideout! You design it, or have us help you. 

When you acquire a site (up to 20 acres), the area you have chosen, or claimed, is then drawn out on a custom plat map, and typed up on a deed. (See below). That piece of New Island remains in your name until you sell it or give it away. Whatever you create there then becomes a part of the overall work of art, and will always be your (imaginary) retreat. You will have the guidebook, map, tickets, one-roger coin, etc. plus the deed, the plat map, and a painting of your New Island property (if you wish) to show to all your friends.

New Island offers vast areas of uninhabited space: forested mountains, miles of beaches, sand dunes, unexplored back-country, spiritual places, hot springs, a steaming volcano, a favorable climate and a few interesting neighbors who can be whomever you want them to be. 

Use the Guidebook to travel around the island...

...see what the locals are up to...

...or build something like a stone house near the beach...

... or your own store on a back road in the desert...

...or an earth home in the mountains. Anything is possible!

The Guidebook will give you a thorough overview of the landscape, history and people of this remote island-nation. The road map will help you navigate the entire island - all the back roads, railways, city streets, every town and village, every beach, stream, mountain peak (with elevation) and everything in between. 

You can enjoy New Island as a traveler...

Risks and challenges

The New Island Guidebook is complete and ready to print. I have sent the manuscript to Bookmasters, in Ohio, and they have assured me that everything looks good! At the end of this campaign I'll order the printing, and the turnaround time should be about 45 days. I will then ship the books, maps and other rewards out to you.

The building-site rewards are a two-step process: Once you receive your Guidebook and the road map, look them over to help you find your dream site. Then call or e-mail me and let me know where on the island you would like your site located. I'll be happy to help you with your questions about any areas you are interested in.

I type the deeds on my Royal 440 manual office typewriter, and draw up each plat map by hand. In most cases, yours will be the only claimed property on the entire four-square-mile plat, though you might have neighbors later on. I estimate that each deed and plat map will take up about two days to complete after I receive the claims from this campaign. I'll ship yours to you on a first come, first served basis.

The newisland.net website will have a page for owners of building sites on New Island. You'll be able to see what your neighbors are up to, and post your own projects, pictures or stories. This service will be ready upon the completion of this campaign, and I will send you information on how to use it.

If you have pledged for a painting of your house-and-property, I'll be ready to help you design it and create the painting once you have claimed your site. Hint: there are no building codes on New Island.

I will complete each painting on a first-come, first-served basis, and estimate that each painting will take about two weeks to complete and ship. Dream-house paintings will be shipped in flat boxes, though unframed, to save shipping costs.

The Map, Travel Package items, and the original works of art are ready for shipping. Shipping the larger paintings can be arranged via FedEx, UPS, USPS, or by direct delivery. (A good reason for a road trip!)
