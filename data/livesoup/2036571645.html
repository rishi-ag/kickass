About this project

Most campaigns fail for one reason.

They don't get any publicity.

Whether the campaign owner doesn't know how to get the publicity he or she needs, or simply doesn't realize that publicity is needed, they never reached their target audience who might have been interested in backing their project.

No publicity = no one knowing about your campaign = no one backing you.

"But I posted on crowdfunding message board forums and social media sites!"

There are dozens of websites for the crowdfunder - telling you "how to do it" and with community forums where you can get advice and even announce your campaign.

And there are a handful of social media sites dedicated to crowdfunding, which allow you to post an announcement of your campaign without being worried that you'll be accused of spamming.

The problem is, you're making your posts and your announcement to "the choir" - to other crowdfunding aspirants, not to anyone who is actually going to back your campaign!

The solution is:

  • a print and digital magazine
  • website
  • a video channel featuring different content than the print and digital magazine
  • a daily blog, also featuring different content
  • social media accounts

...all targeted not to aspiring crowdfunders but to their potential backers, the micro-micro investor.

What crowdfunders need is a ready-made audience of potential backers who seek them out - not the other way around.

The For Angel Eyes Only Empire, as I call it, is going to build a tremendous pool of backers, or micro-micro investors, who will back projects for a variety of reasons - either just for the fun of it, or just to help out someone with an interesting idea, or because they are interested in the perks offered.

For Angel Eyes Only: The Magazine

The magazine will consist of both print and digital versions.

The goal for the print magazine (bi-monthly to start with) will be for it to be on the shelves of  magazine stores, big box stores like Walmart, Target and Sam's Club, and drugstores throughout the country. People browsing through magazines will see FAEO in the financial/investment section and be introduced to the joys of micro-micro investing.

They will be educated as to what crowdfunding is, and how they can acquire cool stuff by simply pledging as little as $1 to help out an aspiring entrepreneur.

Print and Digital

A print magazine which comes out monthly or bi-monthly cannot present news of current campaigns, of course. The print magazine will focus on interviews with backers - large and small - who have had their lives enriched by backing a campaign or campaigns.

The digital magazine will present the same content as the print magazine and will be available for purchase for the Nook, Kindle and other e-readers. It will also be available for viewing on the web. (This is an example of a magazine "flipbook", from the USC Alumni Association - it can feature video and music as well as text. Note I didn't produce this - it's an example of what I can and will do, however: (http://mzines.net/publication.aspx?pid=763&pkey=jtugameso&pageid=1) [Depending on computer speed it will take some time to load).

FAEO Video Channel

After Google, YouTube is the largest search engine on the planet.

The FAEO Video Journal will feature several crowdfunding campaigns per issue, focusing on a specific subject - art one day, film and video the next, community projects the next, and so on.

Social Media Accounts

Facebook, Twitter, Pinterest and Instagram are the most popular social media accounts, and can be used to share news of new campaigns, and drive backers to the website and magazine.

News Blasts

People who subscribe to the website news blasts will receive daily updated listings of new campaigns in whatever niches that they select.

The FAEO empire will raise the visibility of crowdfunders one-thousand-fold, and encourage micro-micro investors to actively seek out people they can help on a regular basis.

We'll also feature several crowdfunding campaigns each day, giving campaigns more exposure.

There is no charge to crowdfunders

After this crowdfunding campaign, and once everything launches, I will list all crowdfunding campaigns (that are professionally presented) on the website, blog, video channel and social media sites based on their merit.  I will also feature as many as possible, that interest me. There is no charge for this service.

The "Feature Your Campaign" perk here is an exclusive, offered only to people who pledge funds to this campaign. Once this campaign has run its course, featured campaigns will be chosen on the basis of whether or not they intrigue me.

At that time, crowdfunders can email me requesting I feature their campaign, but no monetary inducement will be asked for or accepted.

I don't need to hire anyone to contact and interview people, write articles or the monographs, or design the magazine (in either its print or digital editions). I don't need to hire anyone to produce the videos for the video channel. I have the skills to do all that myself.

What I need to be able to do is hire myself. What I need is time.

Because I'm a freelance writer with clients in different time zones, I don't work from just 9 to 5. I work 18 hours a day. Because of this, I've been unable to devote my full energy to FAEO.

With your help, I'll be able to spend 18 hours a day, over 60 straight days or whatever it requires,  working on FAEO, completing the articles for the first issue, designing it, sending the print version off to the printer, and uploading the digital version to its own section of the website.

I also need time to develop the video magazine, which will present different content than the print and digital versions.

Printing the magazine

I'll need funds to have the first issue printed and then delivered to all outlets that order them (as well as to the backers of this project here on Kickstarter!).

Stretch goal 1: Advertising for magazine

I expect to publish the first issue with only selected advertising. For the second issue and beyond, I'll hire an Advertising Director to actively seek out advertisers to purchase ad space.

Stretch goal 2: Employing staff

While I can complete the initial article writing, magazine design and blog and video log entries - all of this takes a great deal of time. While I don't mind working 18 hours a day, I will be more efficient if I can hire a research assistant to help gather campaign information from the wide variety of platforms out there.

Stretch goal 4: Website coding

My html coding skills are out of date, and a stretch goal will be to hire a professional to improve the site.

Check out the website in its current incarnation at: http://forangeleyesonly.com

Stretch goal 5: Purchasing advertising

Having the print magazine appear in stores nationwide will be a big help, but I'll also need to increase visibility by putting ads on Google Adwords and so on.

Rewards are divided into those that will be of interest to the micro-micro investor, identified with the word Backer, and those that will be of main interest to the crowdfunder (looking for tips on how to run great campaigns) identified with the world Campaigner.

The names of everyone who contributes any amount will appear on the virtual Founder's Wall of the FAEO website.

Your crowdfunding campaign URL will be listed on the FAEO website, by name, in the appropriate category, beginning in April, and stay on the site until your campaign's expiration date.

(The website and blog are in existence now, and I'm inputting content, but it will take 30 days to get them into search engines and start developing a readership, so there's no point in fulfilling this reward until April 16. )

Backers  - First Issue Special: Pledge $10

All backers at this level will receive a print copy of the magazine.

First issue is currently anticipated to be available in October, 2015.

Backers - How Angels Got Their Wings: Pledge $20

A book written from the point of view of the backer/micro-micro investor, sharing details of a lot of the fun campaigns that people have backed as crowdfunders over the last decade.

This is what is in store for practically everyone who wants to be an angel and earn their wings!

Critique Your Campaign: Pledge $25

As an experienced crowdfunding campaign writer, I know what needs to go into campaigns - and what shouldn't.

I know the latest trends (from placing images as headers, to making headers "hot-linked" so people can go up or down a page quickly.)

I'll report any typos, any ungrammatical sentences (essential for individuals to whom English is not a first language, or native speakers who aren't really comfortable with writing).

I'll take a look at your supporting documentation (website, social media) and let you know if it serves its purpose also.

Campaigner's Tool Box Tool #1:  Pledge $35

In addition to the above perks, you will receive a Pre-Campaign Business Preparation Workbook.

Learn what you need to do to have the professional presence which will engender trust from potential backers and success for your new business.

(Both new and experienced business people can benefit this guide, as I cover everything that needs to be done so you can create a professional business (if you're a newbie) and what you need to have, as an established business, to be successful on crowdfunding sites.)

In addition, if you've already launched your crowdfunding campaign, your campaign will also be listed --immediately - by name on the FAEO website in the appropriate category.

Campaigner's Tool Box Tool #2: Pledge $45

In addition to above perks you will receive 2 more monographs: Create Compelling Crowdfunding Campaigns and Creative Perks and Rewards.

The next two reasons why most campaigns fail:

Backers don't trust your ability to bring your campaign to a successful conclusion

If your potential backer doesn't believe you know what you're doing, doesn't trust that you'll be able to accomplish what you say you will, they will not back your campaign.

It is not enough to post a single paragraph saying the equivalent of:

"I need $200,000 to open up a restaurant featuring Mexican food. I've never worked in a restaurant in any capacity, let alone managed one, and I don't actually know how to cook. I haven't actually scoped out how much it would cost to rent a building and hire staff, but I'm sure $200,000 would cover it. I've dreamed of doing this for years, so please help.  Anyone who pledges will receive a free meal!"

That's just not going to cut it, and you'd be surprised at how many people actually start campaigns equivalent to the example above!

Professionalism is the key, and Creating Compelling Crowdfunding Campaigns  will help to ensure that you not only know how to found a business professionally, but also how to present it professionally on your crowdfunding page.

Perks and rewards

People back most campaigns because they want the perks/rewards.

Unfortunately, lots of people have problems deciding what perks/rewards to offer. (The term "perk" or "reward" depends on what crowdfunding platform you're using!)

They either offer too few perks, or too many, and make the perk structure unnecessarily complex.

Creative Perks and Rewards Ideas shares the strategy behind offering perks and rewards, and present examples from successful campaigns.

Campaigner's Tool Box Tool #3: pledge $55

In addition to the above perks you will receive a Crowdfunding Campaign Publicity Action Plan.

Although you'll be getting publicity from the For Angel Eyes Only website, that's only one string to your bow. You need all the publicity you can get.

This monograph explains how to get as much publicity as you can.

Your Campaign Featured at FAEO website: Pledge $75

In addition to all the perks above, your campaign will be featured on the FAEO website, and in the blog and video journal.

Featured on the website means I'll write 500 words on your campaign and share a screen capture of the campaign itself. Different text will appear on the blog (for SEO purposes, so as not to have duplicate content) .

Please note: this will only occur if I believe your campaign has been professionally put together.

If your campaign is amateurishly done, what you'll receive is a detailed critique explaining what's wrong with it. (I don't expect many of these, but I must protect the quality of the FAEO brand by putting in this caveat.)

(I make no judgments as to whether people would be interested in backing your campaign, or not. My concern is only with whether or not you have presented enough information so people can make an informed choice, and whether your supporting documentation (website, or lack of it for example) is sufficient or needs work. The detailed critique you receive will explain all issues, and should you correct them, your campaign page will be featured.)

Free publicity for the FAEO Empire:  Pledge $100

One of the most popular perks that people offer is a T-shirt, featuring the logo of the entrepreneur's business idea. Typically this is a perk offered at the $25 level.

However, since you're getting these fantastic monographs which are going to help you create compelling crowdfunding campaigns at those levels, I've upped the ante, and you'll be receiving this T-shirt at the $100 level, along with everything else, of course.

The FAEO logo has yet to be designed, but you may be sure it will be a fantastic-looking t-shirt that you'll be proud to wear it. And of course, by wearing the t-shirt, you'll be giving our media empire free publicity.  Thank you!

And, of course, you'll also receive a one-year subscription to the print and digital editions of the magazine For Angel Eyes Only, which will start life as a bi-monthly.

I've been a freelance writer for twenty years, working for clients through a variety of online freelance writer websites. I write press releases, blog entries, website content, and ghost-write books.

In the last year I've developed a profitable niche at Fiverr, specializing in writing crowdfunding campaigns. (And no, despite the website's name, I do not write these campaigns for $5!).

Check out my crowdfunding "gig" at: https://www.fiverr.com/omnivoreink/write-a-compelling-kickstarter-proposal. (But note if this campaign succeeds, I will immediately take a hiatus from writing campaigns to work on FAEO!)

Please share this campaign with anyone whom you feel might be interested. 

And again, thanks for your support!

Risks and challenges

Getting the print edition of the For Angel Eyes Only magazine in stores throughout the country will be the greatest challenge. Even stores devoted only to magazines have limited shelf-space, so it will be my challenge to persuade them (and/or their middle-men) that FAEO will be a popular magazine, in their finance and investment section and worthy of such shelf space.

The digital version and the website will always be available, and the print magazine will be for sale from the website as well.
