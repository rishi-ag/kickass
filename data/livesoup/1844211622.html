About this project

THE BRAND

SOFIT® is a fitness apparel brand that features sports and performance wear with a unique, trendy style. Geared toward the fitness enthusiast, SOFIT is the “go-to” active-lifestyle brand, where fashion meets fitness. Our premium performance apparel doesn’t just meet your high standards, it sets them. Elevate your fitness potential with a brand of active wear that is bright, bold, sexy, and a little arrogant. We want you to standout, while you workout.

WHAT MAKES OUR PRODUCT SO SPECIAL?

Let's be blunt: for most people that pursue fitness, the underlying motivations are performance, health, or vanity. Our brand embodies these qualities; our products are made of high-quality fabrics that enhances performance, maximizes your activity, and is the perfect way for you to express your personality with color and style! The fabrics and designs were specifically selected to provide the best fit and comfort, while accentuating the contours of the body that you’ve worked so hard for.

PRODUCTS

HOW CAN YOU HELP?

We've invested a lot of our own money to get off the ground, but we're going to need your help to get it all the way to the finish line. We are looking to raise $20,000 to purchase materials at a bulk rate and meet minimum order quantities from our factories.  Your upfront funding will allow our team to place bulk quantity orders for this launch collection.

To help, you’ll need to back our project and make what Kickstarter calls a "pledge". You can easily get started by clicking the large, green "Back This Project" button to the right of our Kickstarter video.

In exchange for pledging to the campaign, you can choose an offered reward. You can pledge any dollar amount you like. If you’d like more than one item, please send me a Kickstarter message specifying your desires and add the cost of the pledge for the second item to your overall pledge.

You don't spend a penny unless we are successful in raising our goal amount.  Just think of pledging as a way to support us right now and pay later for your fitness apparel.

If we’re fortunate enough to surpass our funding goal, we’ll not only be able to produce our launch collection, we’ll be able to beef up our inventory, expand our collection with additional colors and new designs, and introduce our online store. 

Our team needs your help to meet this goal! If you like what we're doing, spread the word where you can.  Every bit of exposure can help to bring us closer to meeting our goals!

PRODUCTION PLAN

Once funded, we will put in our manufacturing order for the featured items. Manufacturing and delivery of items may take up to 4 weeks. During that time, we’ll confirm sizing for all of our backers and prepare for product shipment (1-2 weeks).

THANK YOU SO MUCH FOR YOUR SUPPORT!

Please take a look at the rewards - we hope you'll take advantage of our exclusive pre-sale pricing, but any contribution, big or small, helps a lot! We'd be so grateful if you would help us achieve this dream by spreading the word; share this campaign via email, FB, and twitter!

Once this campaign is funded, I will send you a survey via email to request your desired sizes, as well as your mailing address.

Once you receive your pledge rewards, don't be shy, take plenty of selfies and don't forget to tag us @sofit_fitnessapparel.  ! We cannot wait to see what makes you SOFIT®!

Risks and challenges

Producing anything at a high-volume could potentially pose a number of problems. With that said, we're not starting from square one here. We've already designed, tested, and carefully selected a manufacturer, with whom we have a great relationship. They've already helped us create small batches of our prototypes and we’re 100% confident that our apparel will exceed all your expectations.

We are committed to being transparent in the event of unforeseen obstacles, and we will provide regular status updates on our progress.

FAQ

  • How can I pledge for multiple rewards?

    Adjust your pledge amount by the value of the extra(s) you'd like to the total amount. As soon as the project is complete, you will receive a BackerKit survey requesting information pertaining to your rewards (size, address, etc.) On that survey you’ll select Add-On's. If you increased your pledge by $25, your cart should state "Credit Remaining: $25.00" when you get to the Add-Ons screen. Select the item (choose your size) and click "Add to Cart."

    Please note: you only have to add additional items during this step. The original items you pledged for are already included based on the reward level you selected via Kickstarter.
