About this project

$5500 REACHED IN JUST 8 DAYS!!! CHECK OUT OUR STRETCH GOALS BELOW!!!

Ten years ago, in the middle of a freezing Chicago blizzard, I got together with seventeen of the best musicians I could find to make my fourth album, The Wall to Wall Sessions.Â 

We recorded and mixed the whole album, completely live to tape, the way a lot of my favorite records were made years ago - no overdubs, no computers, no nonsense.

My co-producer and arrangerÂ DavidÂ Nagler created beautiful orchestralÂ scores for all ten songs that were then played live by my crack, seventeen piece City That Works band, whileÂ engineer Dan DietrichÂ captured it all in living stereo on a Studer 1/4 inch 2-track tape machine.Â It was a big undertaking, but what we ended up with was (in my opinion) one of the best things Iâ€™d ever done.Â 

The only problem was, even with all of that old-school analog production, the final product ended up in the computer anyway - mastered digitally and then released on CD (digital again!!).

Until now.

For the 10th anniversary of the The Wall to Wall Sessions, Iâ€™ve gone back and re-mastered the entire album, from the original stereo master tapes, completely in analog. Using razor blades, and scotch tape, and various tube-driven knobs and switches weâ€™ve reassembled the album and had it pressed to beautiful 180 gram vinyl by the good folks at Gotta Groove Records in Cleveland, OH - completely computer free.

This Kickstarter pre-sale will help fund the final stages of the production process and give you the opportunity to finally hear The Wall to Wall Session the way nature intended.

As always, thanks for your time, generosity and support.

-Chris
