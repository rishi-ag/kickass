About this project

Spring Break 2015 Limited Edition Batch.

Thank you for checking out my once-in-a-lifetime set of COLOR DREAM Earrings and collaborative-color-design project. Each pair is a worldwide original and won't last long. 

Go ahead and pick your favorite reward, before it's gone!

Fully Customize Your COLOR DREAM! 

Check out the final rewards which empower you to customize 1, 2, 5, 10, or 20 pair of COLOR DREAM earrings. We'll send you a pallete of over 60 colors from which you will choose your favorite color combo. Support your favorite team, outfit your bride's maids with your wedding colors, or complement your summer wardrobe. 

Get creative and make your COLOR DREAM come true!

Breaux Company Ethically Produces.

This Breaux COLOR DREAM Earrings are ethically produced and exclusively detailed in the Breaux Workshop, Lima, with the same team of artisans with whom we've worked for 8 years. We are growing together and sharing profits to go beyond fair trade. 

Collaboration is the future of design! 

Bootstrap our Winter 2015 Edition.

Your pledge will help us to bootstrap our Winter 2015 Limited Edition Batch and create our next amazing set of COLOR DREAM Earrings.

We will create the newest color trends for this upcoming Winter! 

Share in our passion for design.

We love this design because it educates our color sensibilities and promotes our exploration of seasonal color-trends.

Welcome to Breaux, Worldwide Flow!

Worldwide-original details.

You won't find these premium details anywhere else for only $22! 

1) Double-plated silver frames.  

2) Custom-made ear hooks in solid silver.

3) Hand-finished signature teardrop shape.  

4) WRAPPED IN GOLD.

Risks and challenges

This worldwide original batch of Breaux COLOR DREAM Earrings has already been produced and each pair is poised to mail!

All the challenges have been overcome and the only risk is the mail system, with which I am well practiced.

The fully customized color combo's will be made along with my next batch of North Winter 2015 earrings.

The goal of delivery by the 4th of July may pose some challenges yet nothing we can't overcome!

We've taken 8 years to develop this product design and we're prepared to get your production done!

Breaux Company will astound and deliver.™
