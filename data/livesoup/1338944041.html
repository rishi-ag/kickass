About this project

Hi! I don't have a video up! MAJOR POINTS OFF, HAHA!

But let the artwork speak for itself!

I might have a video up soon, just not today!!

Ethereal Manga is going to be a huge printed manga that shows in-depth adventures of Ethereal, a girl made up of Aquashock, or "liquid lightning". How she came to be this way is a mystery, until you read the manga and find out all about her dark yet triumphant past.

At present, she finds herself in many mysterious and reality-questioning situations- mysteries that date back to the dawn of time, on Earth, and of Time itself.

This is meant to be a mentally expanding subject matter that explores philosophical questions in a completely new and original way. This theme has been the setting for many manga/anime in the past, but this manga will take on a tone completely its own and impress you with its originality- both of story and of the artwork.

Ancient tales and myths will play a large part in the unfolding of Ethereal's reality, as she sets out in Volume I to find the purpose that will take her through several more volumes to a conclusion and satisfaction.

Please!

Be a part of this project by donating and receiving your gifts as soon as AUGUST THIS YEAR!!- That's  2 1/2 Months  after the campaign ends!!

JUST enough time to get it all done, and get it to you!

You will NOT be disappointed!

And you will feel very PROUD to have backed a grass roots effort, which, will hopefully turn into a very large (and well-founded) sensation!!!

THANK YOU AND P-L-E-A-S-E DONATE GENEROUSLY!!!

Risks and challenges

Risks and challenges are:

Not having enough funding to reach the goal.

Time constraints regarding the painting, video game, and full manga completion dates.
This will be solved by extending the time frames month by month, with a maximum of 5 months, and a goal of zero extensions.

Shipping costs may differ, but generally the requested donation amount will be sufficient to cover the project and any minor overages.

Original art created for donors will be the original paper and medium, NOT A COPY!, but a copy MAY be used by the Ethereal Manga Team. If the donor who receives an original work of art wishes to showcase it or use the image for any personal reason, they may do so, as long as it does not interfere in any way with our team's efforts at creating our media.

If anyone is unsatisfied with their gifts, they may work out a maximum of 2 >replacement< gifts by communicating with us.

THANK YOU AND KICKSTARTER FOR THIS OPPORTUNITY!!!
