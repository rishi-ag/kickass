About this project

Bronxton Wallets: Minimalistic, Functional, and Fashionable

Ever since the invention of paper money in the 1600s prompted the development of the wallet, men have been in search of a way to carry their essentials in slimmer, lightweight, but secure wallets. For four hundred years, wallet designers have strived to balance design, functionality, and sleekness. The invention of the credit card in the 1950s gave rise to the modern wallet with its multiple slots to carry cards. Until 1996, wallets remained unchanged until the development of the first evolutionary bi-fold wallet, which reduced the bulkiness of its predecessors by nearly half its size. Many wallets have numerous pockets that are great for storing necessary items, but can also allow people to accumulate a lot of junk very quickly.

Here at Bronxton Designs, we eliminate clunkiness by creating wallets that are minimalistic, fashionable and, most importantly, functional—and we do so in a range of styles. Our focus: design accessories that help you carry all daily essentials in true minimalist style. We develop two types of leather wallets: the “1222 Rule” wallets and the Stasher wallets. The 1222 Rule wallets carry the recommended essentials: one ID, two credit cards, two insurance cards (car and health), and two $20-dollar bills for emergencies. The Stasher wallets are for people who prefer or need to carry more than the essentials.

With all our wallets we never rush, taking time to carefully design each wallet with our vision in mind. Using only the best materials, our products are designed and handcrafted from beginning to end in Park City, Utah, under our control. We have been successfully funded three times on Kickstarter since we fired up in 2014. With the help of our backers, we were able to purchase all the tools required to produce our wallets. Having our own tools allows us to keep everything in house from design to fulfillment. To expand our brand with new product lines, we need more tools for the job. That’s where backers like you come in. Your pledges make it possible for us to get the equipment we need to grow our company with new, innovative products and to create and keep jobs in the United States.

 A Brief Understanding of Your Leather

When purchasing a wallet or any other leather article it is important to understand what you are paying for; many companies sell genuine leather for the price of full grain. We use the only leather good enough for our wallets: full grain leather. The leather we use comes from the best tannery in the world, Horween, which has supplied quality leather hides for over 110 years.

Full grain leather comes from the top layer of the hide, which has ALL of the grain, therefore, FULL grain. The natural surface of full grain leather burnishes and beautifies with use. Full-grain leather refers to hides that have not been sanded, buffed, or snuffed to remove imperfections or natural marks on the surface of the hide. This means each wallet will be naturally unique. No two wallets are alike! The grain remains, giving the fiber strength and durability. The grain also has breathability, resulting in less moisture from prolonged contact. Rather than wearing out, it will develop a patina over time. That means your wallet will wear beautifully and it will actually look better as it ages! This might be the last wallet you will ever need since it will last for many, many years to come.

Top grain leather, the second grade of leather, is a split from the top layer of blemished hide, then sanded and refinished. This is how manufacturers get rid of scars and scrapes and light cow brands. Top grain leather does not age nicely with use.

Genuine leather, the third grade of leather, is produced from the layers of hide that remain after the top is split off for the better grades. The surface is usually refinished (spray painted) to resemble a higher grade.


Our Wallets: We have One for You

The Entry

Even when filled with cards and cash, this wallet is so comfortable, that you won’t feel it in your front or back pocket. The Entry is perfect for going out at night, walking long distances, simplifying your life, and carrying the absolute bare minimum. Though The Entry is minimalistic and affordable, we kept the elegance and quality of well-designed wallet made with Horween leather.

At $25 ($20 for early backers) The Entry is the most affordable, minimalistic, handcrafted, full grain leather wallet you’ll find. While we managed to make it very inexpensive, we did not cut any corners. The main difference from other wallets in our line is that The Entry is not hand-stitched; it is laser-cut, engraved, and machine sewn. Thanks to the backers of the previous project, we were able to buy a sewing machine!

The Main Street wallet offers 4 pockets: 2 front pockets, 1 middle pocket, and 1 elastic pocket. The high quality, low-profile elastic pocket expands so it can hold from 1 to 10 cards.

RFID Protection

What is it? Radio frequency identification (RFID) is a system that transmits the identity (in the form of a unique serial number) of an object or person wirelessly, using radio waves.

Almost all of our wallets now come with optional RFID protection! Most banks and credit card companies have been moving away from swipe card technology and issuing RFID-enabled cards and many places in Europe even have them embedded in personal identification documents.

The RFID Problem.

Unfortunately hackers and others use technologies that can pickpocket that information from you without ever touching your wallet! Sometimes from several yards away! To keep your information safe, our optional RFID protection is made with 2 layers of anti-RFID materials that ensure no access to the information stored in your credit cards and documents. And as you can see in the video it works every time!

FAQ

  • Do Bronxton wallets work with bills larger than American bills?

    Yes!

    We tried Bronxton wallets with 50 EUR, which is the largest European bill in daily use and it measures 14 cm x 7.7 cm. It fits inside the Briber and Tri-fold perfectly! It barely sticks out (less than 1 mm on one side) inside the Prospect wallet. The secret is you would put the bill in vertically, rather than horizontally, which makes the bill only 7 cm (2.76”) high. The Prospect wallet measures 6.98 cm (2.75”) high, so less than 1 mm will stick out on one side of the wallet!

    Singapore Bill: 15.6 cm long and 7.4 cm wide
    When folded in half, Singapore bill is 7.8 cm or 3.07” high. Since the Prospect wallet is 6.98 cm or 2.75” high, the bill will stick out about 8 mm (4 mm on each side), which doesn’t look disproportional or bad in any way! Inside the Briber or Tri-fold the bill will get a little bit folded at the end, but it doesn’t damage the bill or make the wallet look weird in any way!

    If your bill is bigger than the 2 above, please send us a message!
