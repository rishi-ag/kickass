- Do you know who Charlemagne was?
 - Yes of course, his majesty, the King of the Franks.
 - And do you know where the capital of his kingdom was?
 - No his majesty, I suppose somewhere in modern France.
 - No, in every part of modern France, and not only.
 - I think I don't understand.
 - The Franks did not have a fixed capital: Charlemagne, and his court with him, moved constantly from one place to another of his reign. In this way he could lead and control the actions of his people.
 - It seems a good idea, the proximity to the king is crucial for  population.   - And so I have in mind to do.
 - You intend to move the capital, majesty?
 - No, I intend to move myself, from province to province in the country, so that everyone can feel close and identify with me: no longer they have to consider me a Foreign King. 

In 1831 a state was born, but a nation has yet to be created. Belgium conquered his indipendence from Holland and call a foreign king to lead the new nation. Now, a German king sits on the throne conceiving great plans for his kingdom. First step is the unification of the whole population, always divided because of cultural and religious differences. Then a high industrialization that will lead Belgium to compete with the powerful European nations. Now everyone works together for the growth of the country, under the leadership of a foreign king. For influential families ancient enmities and differences are hard to forget and each one will compete with the others to earn trust in the eyes of the king. Will you be able to be protagonist of industrialization and demographic expansion of Belgium, managing to rise your family above the others?

Two mini-reviews:
from Giochi sul nostro tavolo (Italian)
from BGG (English)

The Foreign King is a light-to-medium weight strategy game for 2-4 players, in which you lead one of most influent bourgeois family of Belgium. Main mechanics are placement and majority, and thanks to his ease of learning and fast pace, a game lasts about 40 minutes. During the game you must pay attention to many factors and to the different strategies that can lead you to the victory. In The Foreign King, opponent's actions will be important for your strategy as yours. Especially remember: not only you must choose the best action but you also have to take it at the right moment or your opponent will prevent you to do it. 

The game is divided in turns. In a turn each player takes one action, choosing it from the action that are not yet performed by others players. Availables actions are: 

During the game you have to obtain the majority of citizens of your faction (your color) in the Belgian provinces (place and move citizen actions) and build as many factory as possible (industrialize province). Who industrializes first a province takes a Royal Honor, the greatest King acknowledgment. 

When the King Leopold I comes in a province where you have the majority of citizens (score whit the King action) you gain Victory Points (VP); you gain more VP if the province is industrialized and depending of which type of factory there was built. To implement your strategy you need to get coins (receive coins action) and to obtain the majority of congressmen (place a congressman action). When a player scores with the King, congressmen can be moved in the scoring province to change the majority of citizens. 

Only one player per time can move his congressmen in a province. First chooses who has the majority in the Congress, then the second and so on. Additional actions are the change turn order action, that allows to perform the best action at the best moment, and the Queen action, that allows players to perform an action already chosen by an opponent. 

At the end of the game you gain VP for the majority of citizens in more provinces than other players, for the majority of congressmen, for coins owned and for the Royal Honors you gained during the game. 

So there are many ways to win in this game and in every moment you are divided by two goals: concentrate your citizens and build more factory in the same province to gain VP during the game or plan the widest possible expansion on the whole Belgium to gain more VP at the end of the game. if you'll maneged to match these two aspects of the game you'll obtain the esteem of your King and your family will be the most influential of Belgium. 

The Foreign King is a great strategic game so is perfect for gamers who wants a quick game with contained duration and like complete information, no alea and interaction among players. But his simply mechanics and ease of learning make The Foreign King perfect for occasional players as entry level in strategic board games.

Railroads expansion

 - Preparations for your trip are completed Majesty, everything is ready. With which means of transportation you want to move in the country? By horse, carriage?
 - I would like to travel by train.
 - By train? Which train?
 - The train that does not exist yet, but there will be soon. Belgium will soon be famous for its metallurgical industries and railways.
 - But it is a huge undertaking, no one has yet built it in Europe.
 - Exactly, it's time to progress into the future, a great future! 

Considering that railways would be a great economic resource and that a complete national network would be necessary, King Leopold I was unusual for its time in planning a national network in advance before each other European country: first railway in continental Europe in 1835 has been built between Brussels and Mechelen. In 1843, Belgium had the most dense railway network in the world, taking into account of the extension of his territory. In the following years, Belgium becomes one of the major producer of railroads of the world. 

Railroads expansion, allows players to build railroads to link the provinces of Belgium. Starting from any metallurgical factory your railroad will cross the Belgium, providing as many victory points as it will be long. 
Contens: 10 rail tokens, mini-rulebook. 

Catholics vs Liberals expansion

 - What Belgians say about our politics?
 - People thinks that you are a great king. But you know, Majesty, people is not interested in politics, their unique interest is a good life.
 - And what politicians says about Belgium?
 - Politicians think that you are a great king. But you know, Majesty, politics are not interested in good life, their unique interest is politic.
 - Are you sure you are sincerely answering to my questions?
 - The truth, majesty, is that Belgian population, and politicians too, are divided in two groups: Liberal and Catholics; Liberals love you because you are progressive and Catholics love you because you are catholic.
 - So Liberals oppose me because I'm catholic and Catholics oppose me because I'm progressive. At least in opposition I was able to join together the entire population! 

Belgium was dominated by a struggle between two political groups, known as the Catholics and the Liberals. In general terms, the Catholics represented the relatively religious, conservative and rural elements in the society, while the Liberals represented the more secular, more progressive and more urban middle-class elements. So the nation was therefore polarized between the conservative Catholics Party and the secular Liberal Party. 

With the Chatolics vs Liberals expansion you will participate in the political struggle that took place in Belgium until 1917. Players will join Liberal and Chatholics party and vote in the election to change the political balance of Belgium in their favour. At the end o the game the party with majority of the provinces will earn victory points.
Contents: 10 politic tokens, mini-rulebook. 

Riots expansion

 - Europe is up in arms, the revolution is everywhere.
- The people rebels against their monarchies to have the Constitution. - Do you think it will happen also in our country?
- I do not see why, majesty. You are a great king and Belgium already has a Constitution.
- But there\'s always someone unhappy and Belgium is no exception; riots will happen here as well and we will have to intervene.
- You will not want to use weapons against your people, majesty!
- Yes of course, my weapons will be firmness and justice.  

In February 1848, Leopold I helped to maintain peace remaining neutral during the revolutionary events that upset Europe between January of 1848 and spring of 1849. He also played an important role in stopping the spread of the Revolution into Belgium These riots were a series of political upheavals throughout Europe, that remains the most widespread revolutionary wave in European history, but within a year, reactionary forces had regained control and the revolutions collapsed.  

With the Riots expansion, players have to manage riots in the provinces of Belgium and use them for their purpose ruining the plans of the opponents. They can also use the King Leopold I not only to get victory points by majorities and factories, but also turning off the hotbed of revolution in the country.
Contens: 8 riot tokens, mini-rulebook.
