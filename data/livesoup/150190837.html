About this project

  • Your pledge is a purchase of one of our card holders or wallets with your personal image on.
  • Pledge now, decide your image later!
  • Our Kickstarter works like this: If we reach our target for £10,000 in June you will be sent our customisation tool to upload your image. 
  • Your product will then be custom made and then sent to you. 
  • If we don’t reach our target your money doesn’t leave your account.
  • To purchase more than one product, simply add the relevant prices onto your existing pledge. E.g If you want a Billfold and a Slim wallet click on the Billfold reward. Then on the next page add an extra £40 onto the total. You can then send a quick message to us letting us know the addition to your order. (When purchased it will only say 1 Billfold brought. Don't worry your order for both will still go through as long as the amount of money pledged is correct)

Isolana London hand-make the world's most customisable leather wallets. Using our innovative printing technology we are able to print any design directly onto leather. This combined with skilled British leather craftsman and the finest Italian leather helps to make our wallets totally unique and long lasting.

Our brand embodies the freedom of choice and expression. We want you to express yourself the way you want. Isolana London aims to create wallets which are truly unique to each customer, wallets that are great looking, premium quality, functional and affordable!

Our products are totally unique. Never before has anyone been able to customise luxury accessories at an affordable price. By utilising our experience in crafting premium products we are able cut down on costs by producing everything in house, through our London based workshop. The result is a premium luxury leather wallet range starting from £20 (over 50% off RRP).

We are offering three unique handcrafted products for you to personalise:(The wallet images below are examples) 

The Card Holder is our most minimal product, it is both compact and slim featuring two slots perfect for your travel cards and middle slip pocket which is suitable for folded banknotes and receipts. You can print any image onto the rear side and choose the colour of leather for the front. The Card Holder is the ultimate personal gift for anyone. 

The Slim Wallet is not only thin but extremely functional. With 4 slots you can hold all your cards on the go alongside folded banknotes and receipts. You can print any image onto either the inside or the outside of the Slim wallet.

The Billfold wallets design is based on traditional London wallets used for generations. We have brought together an iconic design with a slim form factor which allows it to fit easily in your pocket. Featuring 8 card slots and a full sized banknotes sleeve. You can print any image onto either the inside or the outside of the Billfold wallet.

 Here at Isolana London we feel we are unique for the following three reasons:

Isolana London is able to print any image directly onto our luxury range of leather wallets. This has never been achieved commercially before. You design your wallet through our online design system which allows anyone to upload and scale an image onto our wallet templates. You will be given a unique code at the end of the campaign which will grant you access to the system and the wallet you have purchased to customise.

Our online customisation system (beta) allows you to upload any image from Facebook, Instagram, your computer or use one of our cool custom templates. You can then scale the image and position it however you want. You can even add multiple images together on the same wallet! This tool will also allow you to choose which leather and add any initials you would want to emboss (£5 extra). We will send you individually a link to our system (after the campaign is complete) which will allow you to customise the precise order you pledge. 

If you are undecided on what image to get you have up until June 5th to choose one.

We pride ourselves on our attention to detail. Quality is at the heart of everything we do and we will not cut corners in our aim to create the perfect wallet for you.

Isolana London have developed strong relationships with the very best leather tanneries in northern Italy. We hand select only the finest vegetable tanned calf leather to make our wallets both beautiful and premium quality. We believe a wallet should last a lifetime not just a few years. 

Every wallet is hand made in London in our workshop based on a small Island on the Thames (hence the name Isolana which is Italian for Islander). Our skilled London craftsmen use traditional manufacturing methods to individually make each wallet.

Each wallet comes carefully packaging in one of our felt carrying cases, embossed with our logo. Its the perfect companion for every wallet.

Each of our products have been through extensive prototyping in order to reach our final products. We decided to keep our designs simple by taking inspiration from the past classic wallet designs.

Each product has been created to hold the optimum amount of cards and cash for everyday use.

Below is a video showing the functionality of each product.

We support rising brands and artists to utilise our new product medium to showcase their great work and retail their own designed wallets. Our wallets are the perfect way to offer your customers something unique. We allow you to purchase a bulk order of 10 wallets at a further discounted price (up to 70% off RRP).

Our wallets are also perfect for a business/team/society to print their logo or crest on. Stand out from the crowd with something different, not another tacky cheap custom product. But something which you will want to keep not throw away.

Our founder Forrest Skerman-Stevenson comes from a blood line of leather craftsman. Through studying product design at university he has merged his passion for design with his leather heritage in order to create innovative fashion accessories.

Forrest is being supported by a young and passionate team. Working together with experienced skilled craftsman and forging strong relationships with high quality Italian leather tanneries Isolana London is able to set a new standard for luxury customisation. 

Please help spread the word by sharing our social media pages below:

Thank you for spending the time to check out our project. We hope you can help share our vision to bring luxury customisation to everyone by talking about us on your social media platforms. Please tag @IsolanaLondon and link our page. Our team will be looking out for whoever helps spreads the word the most and they will be rewarded with surprise gifts added on to their order! 

Risks and challenges

We have done our best through thorough preparations to limit the risks and challenges we face when completing our project. However there are always unexpected circumstances which could strike any business when producing a product. But due to controlling all areas of production and supply we are able to be confident in our ability to handcraft each product to the finest quality.

We will invest the money wisely in planned machine purchases and buying the leather in bulk from our north Italian tanneries. We have built up relationships with our leather suppliers and our London based craftsmen over years and so understand their outputs. We are confident in our ability to meet our expected delivery date. We will be in constant contact updating you in detail along the way.

FAQ

  • How can I earn a special free gift?

    By being the best supporter! Share our page to as many people as possible and let us know by messaging us on Facebook, Twitter and Instagram. We will be picking people throughout the campaign to get exclusive free gifts worth hundreds of pounds!

    Last updated: Fri, May 1 2015 8:07 pm EDT
  • How to do I purchase a wallet?

    You must purchase a wallet through one of our rewards. No money will leave your account until after our campaign has ended and we have reached our financial goal.

    Last updated: Tue, May 5 2015 6:28 am EDT
  • How do I buy more than 1 wallet?

    If you want to buy more than 1 wallet please pledge for the wallet you want and then add the relevant prices onto pledge:

    - Add £20 for an extra Card Holder
    - Add £40 for an extra Slim Wallet
    - Add £80 for an extra Billfold Wallet

    E.g if you want 1 Billfold, 1 Slim wallet and 1 card holder. Please click on the Billfold reward. Then add £40 (slim wallet) + £20 (card holder) onto the total to make £140. Then just send us a message confirming your order.

    It will state that you have purchased only 1 wallet but don't worry we will process your full order accordingly!

    Last updated: Tue, May 5 2015 6:38 am EDT
  • How do I customise my wallet?

    You have until the end of the campaign to think of what you want to put onto our wallets! So no excuses for being uncreative.
    We will send you each a link to our new online design system which will be relevant to your order. You will be able to:
    - Upload photos, scale and re-size to your hearts desire!
    - Choose which colour leather you want
    - Select the want to emboss (if you have added the £5 for embossing)

    Last updated: Fri, May 1 2015 8:03 pm EDT
  • Do you deliver to people outside the UK?

    Yes of course! We want everyone to have access to our innovative fashion accessory. We have tried to keep the costs down as much as possible so it should be affordable for all! No matter where you are we will ship to you!

    Last updated: Fri, May 1 2015 8:03 pm EDT
  • Why are they so cheap?

    We are all about the community and want to make sure our products are available for all. We are fortunate enough to have the scope to produce everything in house. We have utilised our experience creating products for major fashion designers to bring our costs down. We now cut out the middle man and the designer market up to offer you an unbeatable price.

    Last updated: Fri, May 1 2015 8:03 pm EDT
  • What leather do you use?

    We use only the finest vegetable tanned leather from established family run north Italian tanneries. Each hide is individually inspected for imperfections before it gets used for one of our luxury leather wallets. We use the same leather as major fashion brands across the world.

    Last updated: Fri, May 1 2015 8:03 pm EDT
  • When does the money leave my account?

    Not until the campaign has ended on the 5th June and only IF we have reached our financial goal of £10,000.

    Last updated: Fri, May 1 2015 8:07 pm EDT
